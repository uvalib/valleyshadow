A Bibliography of Civil War Letters and Diaries

Listed on this page are the repositories from which the letters and diaries of Franklin and Augusta were gathered, and the sources for the images of the site. The scholarship on the Civil War which follows provided an excellent background context essential for the analysis of the letters and diaries from Franklin and Augusta Counties. In ascertaining a complete view of the Civil War, it became possible to fully understand the circumstances surrounding the authors and their influence on the writings.
