During the Civil War, Lucius Mox was a baker, serving in several different units, including the 101st Pennsylvania Infantry and the 17th Pennsylvania Cavalry. The letters collected here are to his sweetheart, Jennie. Mox tells many stories of camp life, and writes to Jennie about how much he misses her and the rest of his friends and family at home.
