Summer of 1864: Rev. Schenck

The Burning of Chambersburg by Rev. Schenck

Rev. Schenck was a a minister in Chambersburg and was also notable for his weekly medicine column in the Valley Sprirt. He wrote this book concerning the burning in October and it was reprinted in 1864 and 1865.

In this account he describes in depth many incidents of the burning such acts of thieft, looting, etc. He maintains that only 20 min. was given from the time the ransom was delivered to the town to the first building being burned.
