Valley Spirit, January 25, 1864

Relief for the Prisoners



Many persons are under the impression that since the escape of the Federal officers from the Libby Prison that no packages can be sent to our prisoners. Dr. Reid, however, informs us that he has recieved a letter from the Express agent at Fortress Monroe, stating that articles of food and clothing from private parties, for prisoners, are forwarded to and recieved as usua. This will be good news to those whose friends are still in the hands of the rebels, which, we feel confident, they will profit by.
