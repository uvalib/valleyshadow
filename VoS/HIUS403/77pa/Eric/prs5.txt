<font size=6><bold><center>PRISON NO. 5--DANVILLE, VA</bold></center></font>
<br>
<hr>
<br>
<font size=6><bold>P</bold></font><font size=4>rison No. 5 was located  in the tobacco district of Danville, VA.  Built (and still standing) on the corner of Floyd and High Streets, it was originally used as a tobacco plug factory before becoming a death house.  It had three stories and held as many as 1,000 Union soldiers at one time.  Today , Prison No. 5 is empty.</font>
