<font size=6><bold>W</bold></font><font size=4>hen prison overcrowding got out of control at Libby Prison in Richmond, VA, the Confederate leadership decided to open prisons in Danville, VA and Andersonville, GA.  There were six prisons established in tob
acco factories in dowtown Danville.  These were all three story buildings capable of housing hundreds of soldiers.  Prison No. 5 was outstanding as the home of the Seventy-seventh Pennsylvania's "Great Escape."
