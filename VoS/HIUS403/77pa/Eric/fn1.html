COL. OBREITER
Not only was Col. Obreiter an officer in the Civil War and a leader of the most successful prison break during the entire Rebellion, he was also the co-author of the regimental historyof the Seventy-seventh Pennsylvania. This regimental history allows historians to have a perspective that no contemporary writer can offer. This account of the famous prison break was written by Obreiter himself. Readers should note that he wrote of himself in the third person.
