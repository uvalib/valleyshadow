This letter is on official Bureau stationary.

Winchester, VA, July 27, 1867
Mr. Thos. P. Jackson
Agent
Staunton, Va

Sir,
In reply to your communication, I have the honor to state that I cannot authorize the gratuitous issue of the Bureau's clothing in your charge.


Very respectfully,
Your Obd. Sert.
John A McDonnell
Capt.
