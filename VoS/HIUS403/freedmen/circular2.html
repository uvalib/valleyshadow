Bureau R. F. A.L
Head Dis. Asst. Com. Dist -- Va.
Richmond, Va. May 27, 1868

Circular No. 8.

So much of par. 4 Circular 10 -- 1866 from these headquarters as forbids officers and agents of the Bureau from acting as attorneys in cases wherein Freedmen are a party, is hereby revoked.

By command of
Bt. Brig. O. Brown
Asst. Com.
(Signed) Will A. Goulter Bt. Capt. asa
