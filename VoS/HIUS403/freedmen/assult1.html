Letter
Sept 25th 1867

Capt Jackson
Dear Sir
Frank Harris has just handed me a communication from you, in which you inform me that he asks process against Newton Wilson for an assult on him. I will issue the process and make it returnable on next Tuesday at the Valley Mills, near Westview at which place you are invited to attend at 10 Oclock A.M.

Yours Respectfully
James Wilson
