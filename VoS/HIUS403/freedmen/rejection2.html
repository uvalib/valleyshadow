There was no year specified for this letter, merely the date "June the 15th." At the bottom of this letter was the writing "Not Granted," and signed, "TPJ"

Waynesboro
June the 15th

Mr. Jackson
Sir I write to inform you that I am suffering for anything that support life. I have neather food nor raiment. I have bin begging for too years and the citizens are getting tyard of my begging and I am very old and infirm and unless I receive help from you or some other source I can not possibl get along.

Yours respectfully
Feby Leach
