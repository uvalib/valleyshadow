NEGRO CONGRESSMAN


The Staunton Vindicator, November 8, 1867
The result of the Virginia election should satisfy every observing man in the North, that Congress will be filled with negro representatives from the South. This will be the legitimate fruit of universal negro suffrage, and, so far as we are concerned, they shall enjoy it to their hearts content, and their personal annoyance too. We trust that not a white man in the whole South will ever be sent to a Radical Congress. We would dose them with nigger until they cry, "hold, enough!"--Lynchburg Virginian
