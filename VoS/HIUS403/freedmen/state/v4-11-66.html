Valley Virginian


BRUTAL MURDER
vol. 1, no. 19, April 11, 1866, pg. 2, col 4
We regret to learn that Amos Krat, Esq., was brutally murdered by a negro at his residence, near Shadwell Depot, Albermarle county, Va., last week. He left a large family to mourn his lost.
