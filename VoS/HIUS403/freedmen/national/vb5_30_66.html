Valley Virginian


vol. 1, no. 26, May 30, 1866, pg. 4, col. 1
When the negores were worsted in the Memphis riots, a Congressional committee had to be sent down to investigate. When white people were slaughtered in Norfolk by negroes unprovoked, Congress had no tears to shed.

Is this a white or black Government?
