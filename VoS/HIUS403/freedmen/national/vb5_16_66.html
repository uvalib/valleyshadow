Valley Virginian


vol. 1, no. 24, May 16, 1866, pg. 1, col. 3
The infamous disfranchisment law just passed in Tennessee, is almost certain to result in trouble. The similar acts in force in Maryland, West Virginia and Misouri, and the proscripive policity of the Radicals, justify serous fear that the peace of the country will be broken. It seems to be the diligent effort of the politician madman....
