Valley Virginian


ANOTHER WAR
vol. 1, no. 18, April 4, 1866, pg. 2, col. 1
Leading jorunals North and in Washington, fear that another civil war is near at hand. They say if President Johnson refuses to carry out the provisions of the civil Rights bill, after it is passed over his veto, the Radical Congress will commence the war. As things stand, we can see no other solution of the question that now agitates the Country, unless somebody "backs down."
