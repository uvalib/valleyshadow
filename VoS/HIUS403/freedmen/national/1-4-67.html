Staunton Vindicator


January 4, 1867, vol 22, no 1, pg 4, col 1
The negroes of Dr. O.B. Early, of Memphis, returned to him last week, and claimed and received their former situations. Lured off by Bureau agents, they stated that they had been told they must work for $3 a month whivh left them the alternative either to steal, starve, or go back to their master.
