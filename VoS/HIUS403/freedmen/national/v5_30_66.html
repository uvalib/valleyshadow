Valley Virginian


A DAMNABLE OUTRAGE
vol. 1, no. 26, May 30, 1866, pg. 2, col. 2
It is reproted that the agent of the Freedmen's Bureau, at Franklin, Tennessee, ha turned over the lands, in which our Soldiers are buried, to the negroes, and, that under the direction of a radical preacher, who is the agent, they are ploughing up the graves.
