Letter

Waynesboro Va. Jan. 23 1868

Mr. T.P. Jackson

Dear Sir
I wish to hire a good cook & worker for my family 5 persons. For a good reliable woman I will give $6.00 Six Dollars per month the year round. I prefer hiring 12 at a time everything is very convenient. Water is 10 steps of the kitchen & the wood always in the House. The Labour is very light any Harty [sic] woman can do the work with ease & not employed 2/3 of her time. if you know of such a One or one should call at your office for hire let them know of the situation. I live in 1 mile of Waynesboro depot. You will much oblige me by doing so.

Respectfully Yours,
Peter Hanger Jr.
