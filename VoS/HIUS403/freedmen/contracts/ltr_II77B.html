Bureau of Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands
Office 4th Division, 9th Sub-District, Virginia.
Staunton, Va. Dec 17 1867

Capt. John A. McDonnell
Sub Asst Comm
Winchester

Captain
I respectfully refer to a communication from Brev. Lt. Col. James Johnson S.A.C. 6th Dist. Va. dated May 1/67(returned to your Head Quarters May 12/67 with an endorsement thereon made by me. May 12/67) asking [?] to collect a claim of $130 said to be due to the children of Henry Alexander (col'd) by James McPhetus McCutchen, deceased, late of Augusta Co. Claimant in behalf of the children of Benjamin Moten (col'd)

The heirs dispute the claim and the administrator notifies me that unless legal proof is presented he cannot pay the amount or any portion thereof. As the estate is solvent and must soon be settled, I ask Henry Alexander's children present me at once with proper legal proof of the solidity of their claim.

Very Respectfully
Yr. Obt. Servt
Thos. P. Jackson
Agent
