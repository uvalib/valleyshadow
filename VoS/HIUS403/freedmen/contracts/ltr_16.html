Letter
Bureau of Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands
OFFICE 4th DIVISION, 9th SUB-DISTRICT, VIRGINIA,
Staunton, Va., July 11, 1867

Capt John A McDonnell
Sub Asst Comm
Winchester
Captain

At the request of Mr. Alexander Anderson of Augusta Co. I have the honor to transmit a claim made by him against the United States for property seized by and furnished to U.S. troops from June 7 to 9th 1864, amounting to $476 00/100.
Many similar claims have already been presented and will be almost numberless if the U.S. govt. commences paying them.
For Mr. Alex. Anderson I can only say that his account is a just statement of his losses and that his loyalty throughout has been decided and unconditional.

Yr. obt. servt.
Tho. P. Jackson
Agent
