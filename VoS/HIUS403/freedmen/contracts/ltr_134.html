Letter

Went in Feb-Mar and left at Xmas 1866

Sir. James Wait desires me to make a memorandum of the articles Mr. Burdett gave his sister Dorcas Wait last year when she was living with him.
One new calico dress
One mouslaine [sic] dress a second one belonging to one of his daughters
also a linsay dress which was taken from her and a pair of shoes at $2.50 he was to pay her $4 per month and she lived there ten months
he also gave her a shaker at 50c.

If Mr. J. is not at home James Wait would be very glad if you would get the money for his sister and enclose it to Mrs. M. Tate, Greenville he has been trying all year and has entirely failed.
