Letter
Jackson River Va.
April 9th '67

Dr Sir

I wish you can do it that you would send me out a dozen or two hands. Or at least let it be known that I want hands. I want ten or a dozen for choppin whom I will pay $15 per and 8 or 10 common hands whom I will pay 12 The chopper to go to Millboro & the other hands here

If you can do anything that way I will be obliged--

I send you with this the paper regardin Mr. Fulty
Truly Yours
C. Clay
