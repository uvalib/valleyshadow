Arbitration bond

Augusta Co. Va. April 22, 1867
We the undersigned parties to a contract between Samuel Parnt of the first part and William Saulsbury of the second part hereby agree to refer all matters in dispute between us to Dr. Samuel Kennedy [whose name is crossed out and replace by one A. J. Thompson, presumably after Kennedy declined to arbitrate] and mutually bind ourselves to abide by the terms of his decision and to make prompt settlement in accordance therewith.

William Saulsbury + (his mark)

Witness to signature of William Saulsbury
Thos. P. Jackson
Agent
B. R. F. & A. L.
