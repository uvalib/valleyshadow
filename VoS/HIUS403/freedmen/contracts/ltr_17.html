Bureau of Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands,
OFFICE 4th DIVISION, 9th SUB-DISTRICT, VIRGINIA
Staunton, Va., June 4th 1867

Mr. Joseph Milsted
Augusta Co.
Sir
Peyton Harris (c) complains of bad treatment of his child by your wife and that you attempt to break an agreement made with him for present year.
I need not tell you that the law gives you no right to beat or threaten anyone and before you can put Harris out of his house & garden you must assign sufficient reason, such as breach of agreement on his part.
Yr Obt Srv
Thos. P. Jackson
Agent
