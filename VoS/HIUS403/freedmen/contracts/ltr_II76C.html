Near Brownsburg
January 2nd

Mr. T.J.[sic] Jackson
Your letter of [?] was duly received concerning John Brent col. against Jos. Miller. The case was duly tried and upon John's our acknowledgements. Miller brought him in debt. Not having the docket I cannot give a full statement as there is two of us sets together and renders up judgements time about.

Yours Respectfully
Daniel Brown J.P.
