Contract

I have this day hired of Jordan Grandson his nephew "Dick" for whose services from this time until the 25th of December next, I hereby obligate myself to pay to the said Jordan Grandson the sum of Four dollars per month and furnish Dick good comfortable board & lodging.
As witness my hand at Staunton this 20th day of April 1867.
J. H. Hoffman

[Attached: a copy of Richard Banheart's account with Hoffman.]
