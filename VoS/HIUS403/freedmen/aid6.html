This letter was sent on official Bureau stationary.

Winchester Va, April 18th, 1867

Mr. Tho. P. Jackson
Agent
Staunton, Va.
Sir

You will promptly report to this office the number of white persons in the counties under your charge, who require supplies of food to prevent starvation and extreme want, who are not now supplied by County authorities, and whose cases are not properly included under the present regulations of the Bureau. the number in each county will be given separately.

Very Respectfully,
Your obd. Svt
John A McDonnell
Captain V.R.C.
Sub Ast. Com.
