This letter was on official Bureau stationary
Winchester, Va., May 9, 1867
Mr. Thomas P. Jackson
Agent
Staunton, VA
Sir,
In your reporting Bureau affairs for April you state "I regret to find myself without food or clothing to ration."

On the second of March last, Mr. Tukey, your predecessor, reported to this office, that he had received two (2) boxes of clothing for gratuitous distribution. Since that time, no application for clothing has been made from your sub-distric at this office.

No Estimate of Rations required to prevent destitution has been received from your sub-district since the month of February, as required by circu;lar 32. ---

Very Respectfully
Your Srvt.
John A McDonnell
Capt.
Sub. Ast. Comm.
