Letter

Staunton, Va.
July 11. 1867

Jackson, Tho. P.
Agent

Encloses claim of Alexander Anderson for property seized by U.S. troops.

Bureau R.F.& A.L.
Head'Qrs 9th Sub. Dist. Va.
Winchester, Va. July 12, '67

Respectfully returned to Mr. Thos. P. Jackson, agent. The collection of claims of this character does not come within the province of this Bureau.

John A. McDonnell
Capt. S.A.C.
