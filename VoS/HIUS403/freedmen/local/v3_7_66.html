Valley Virginian


ROBBED
vol. 1, no. 14, March 7 1866, pg. 3, col. 4
John Briggs, one of the most respectable colored men of Staunton, was robbed last week by a negro, of specie, &c. John took the case and the robber, before Mr. Tukey and was satisfied with the result. In this connection we are pleased to notice that Mr. Tukey is doing every thing in his power to regulate the Freedmen, and his decisions give general satisfaction. In this good work he should have the assistance of all good citizens.
