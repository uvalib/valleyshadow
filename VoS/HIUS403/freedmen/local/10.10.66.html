Valley Virginian


no. 45, pg. 2, col. 3, October 10, 1866
The Mulatto Convention--The "Mulattoes" are the favorite terms used to designate the despicable set of rascals, known as "Southern Loyalists." we object to the term. It is an insult to respectable colored people, and does not convey a proper idea of the lowest depths reached by this sort of people. "Southern Yankees," are the only two words that do.
