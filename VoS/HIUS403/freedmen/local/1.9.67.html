Valley Virginian


January 9, 1867, vol. 2, no. 6, pg. 3, col. 3
Statistics of Augusta County.--Through the courtesy of Wm. A Burnett, Clerk of the County Court of Augusta, we are enabled to give the following statistics: During the past year there was issued from the County Court office, 221 (white) and 57 (colored) marriage licenses. From the Corporation office, 7 (white) and 5 (colored)--total, 290. . . .
