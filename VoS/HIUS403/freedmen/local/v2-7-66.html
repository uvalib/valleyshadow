Valley Virginian


vol. 1, no. 10, February 7, 1866, pg. 3, col. 2
We understand that a number of persons in this county, summoned to appear at the Freedman's court have refused to appear. If this is so they do a great wrong to our people in town, for if they do not appear it gives an excuse for sending troops hers.

Let every man summonded appear and give no excuse, to the little squad of "so-called" union men in this county, to inflict troops upon us.
