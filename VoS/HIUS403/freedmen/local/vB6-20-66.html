Valley Virginian


vol. 1, no. 29, June 30, 1866, pg. 3, col. 2
Another letter from Rev. Mr. Lawson, the gentlemanly colored preacher her, will be found on the outside of our paper.

Mr. Lawson ahs acted properly since he has been in our midst and his influence has been for the good of all parties.

We can not too highly commend such conduct on the part of our colored people.
