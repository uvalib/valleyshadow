Valley Virginian


vol. 1, no. 37, August 15, 1866, pg. 3, col. 2
The colored people of Harrisonburg realized over $100 dollars by their dinner last week.

It was largely attended by the white people ; the ministers of the various denominations addressed them and the best feeling prevailed among all parties.

We are always glad to chronicle such pleasant facts about our colored population in the Valley, which we believe is the best in the South.
