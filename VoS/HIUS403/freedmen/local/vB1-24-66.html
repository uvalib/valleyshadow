Valley Virginian


THE EFFECT OF THE REMOVAL OF THE TROOPS
vol. 1, no. 8, January 24, 1866, pg. 3, col. 2
The U. S. troops have left the whole Valley. The quiet and peace that reigns now makes our country look like old times. The wisdom of the President's policy can be seen by any "radical" who will take a trip up this way and see how smoothly and quietly everything goes on.

We are glad to notice the disappearance of the crowds of negroes from our streets and to learn they are generally going to work by the year.
