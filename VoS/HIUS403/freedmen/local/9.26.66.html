Valley Virginian


no. 43, pg. 3, col. 2, September, 26, 1866
The Colored Church.--The Colored Church, at the old Market House, was sold by the Committee last week to the Presbyterians for $2,000, an advance of $800 on what they gave for it. The colored people have purchased a beautiful lot near the Academy, known as the Sealy lot, and intend erecting a fine church on it.--They have the best wishes of the community in their interests.
