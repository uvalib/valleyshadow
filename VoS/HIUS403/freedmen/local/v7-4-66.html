Valley Virginian


vol. 1, no. 31, July 4, 1866, pg. 3, col. 2
We are sorry that our notice of that "lucky ex-typo, Harlan," was misconstrued by some of our Methodist friends.

We can assure them we simply intended to give him a notice, and that the idea of our being "prejudiced" is simply ridiculous, but worthy of a soft head that conceived it.
