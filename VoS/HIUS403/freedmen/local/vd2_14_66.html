Valley Virginian


vol. 1, no. 11, February 14, 1866, pg. 3, col. 2
A white man fooled a negro in the employ fo Col. Jas. H. Skinner, of this place, into letting him have a pair of two horse wagon springs, on the 5th inst., by saying the Colonel said he could have them. The thief and the springs have not been heard from since.
