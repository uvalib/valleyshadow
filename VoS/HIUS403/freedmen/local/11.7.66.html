Valley Virginian


no. 49, pg. 3, col. 2, November 7, 1866
The Circuit Court--This Court met on last Friday, November 2d--Judge harris presiding, in the absence of Judge Sheffey. The Grand Jury made the following indictments. Reuben Hill and James Burgy, (colored,) in two cases, one for burglary, and one for house breaking,--making three cases. james Williams, (colored,) for housebreaking--three cases. . . The trial of Reuben Hill and James Burgy, (colored,,) was going on yesterday. . .
