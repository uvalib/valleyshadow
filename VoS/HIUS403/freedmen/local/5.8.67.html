Valley Virginian


vol. 2, no. 23, pg. 3, col. 2 May 8, 1867
Registration--Capt. Jackson, Supervisor of registration for Augusta, informs us that no appointments for District Registrars have yet been made, and that it is probable the registration will not commence before the 1st of June. Ten days notice will be given and the Registrars published as soon as names are received.
