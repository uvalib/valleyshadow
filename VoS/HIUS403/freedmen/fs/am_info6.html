Attached Report
Bureau R. F. + A. L. Office Sub Asst Comr
Sub Dist of Goldsboro, Goldsboro N.C.
June 18 1867

Respectfully transmitted to the Sub Asst Com Sub Dist of Berne (__ HD Qrs. Asst Com Bureau State of North Carolina) Roderick Ham left the plantation of Hay-ward Ham in the Spring of Summer of 1865 and went to New Berne N.C. Sally Bess left the plantation of Nicholas Bess about the same time and also went to New Berne. I infer that the parents name above and referred to in his letter as the _______ ________. and am informed, in direct, that they now reside as New Berne, N.C.

C S Compton
Maj + Sub. Asst. Coms
