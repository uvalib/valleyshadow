Attached Letter

Parkersburg, W. Va

February 29th 1868
Bvt Maj. F. N. Clark
Washington, D.C.
Dear Sir
In reference to your com- munication dated 20th ________ concerning Cynis Bannister, I will state that I visited him this morning, read him your letter. He stated that there had been some difficulty between them which induced him to leave. Has been absent about three years.
I endeavored to impress upon him the importance of caring for his children. He directed me to say that he intended to return this spring, perhaps about a month hence.

Very Respectfully
(Sgd) S. E. Colburn

Official
F. N. Clark
