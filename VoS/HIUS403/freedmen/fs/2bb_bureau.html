Bureau Correspondances
  • December 26, 1867: Agent Thomas P Jackson informs Assistant Commissioner John A. McDonnell of the case of Betsy Brown and requests inquiry be made to find Gabriel Brown, Betsy Brown's father.
  • January 16,1868: John A. McDonnell requests more information about the Betsy Brown case.
  • January 24, 1868: Thomas P Jackson forwards requested information to John A McDonnell.
  • January 27, 1868: John A McDonnell refers the case to O Brown, Assistant Commissioner of Virginia state Bureau.
  • January 29, 1868: O Brown informs John A McDonnell that this case falls under local jurisdiction.
  • January 30, 1868: Thomas P. Jackson forwards new information regarding the case to John A McDonnell.
  • January 31, 1868: John A. McDonnell informs Thomas P. Jackson of O Brown's decision on the case.
  • February 1, 1868: John A. McDonnell forwards all information on the case to Thomas P. Jackson.
  • February 3, 1868: John A. McDonnell requests update information about the case from Thomas P. Jackson.
