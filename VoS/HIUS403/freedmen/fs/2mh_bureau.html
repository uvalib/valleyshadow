Bureau Correspondances
  • July 10, 1867: Agent Thomas P. Jackson informs Assisstant Commissioner John A. McDonnell of the case of Marian Hall petitioning help to obtain his daughter, Marian Elizabeth from Stewart C. Slaven.
    • January 22, 1867: Enclosed statement of S. C. Slaven regarding the situation.
  • July 12, 1867: John A. McDonnell refers the case to Assistant Commissioner O Brown of the Virginia state Bureau.
  • July 15, 1867: O Brown refers the case to Agent R. S. Lacy.
  • July 17, 1867: R. S. Lacy assigns case to Agent J. W. Sharp.
  • July 29, 1867: R. S. forwards report of J. W. Sharp to Genl. O Brown.
  • July 31, 1867: O Brown forwards report to Capt. John A. McDonnell.
  • August 3, 1867: John A. McDonnell forwards report to Thomas P. Jackson.
  • August 7, 1867: Agent Thomas P. Jackson informs Marian Hall of the results of the Bureau's investigation.
