Attached Letter


Aug 28 1867
Capt John A McDonnell
Sub Asst Comm
Winchester

Captain
David Collins(c) asks that enquiry be made at Winchester for his wife Hannah Collins (Reed) who left Staunton on September 1866, with the intention of visiting her mother Lamica Smith (c) at Winchester since which time nothing ahs been heard of her David and Hannah are Registered (No 632) as husband and wife and he is anxious to hear where she is and if alive that she return to her home at West View.

David has made enquiry himself without success and is not able to make the journey to Winchester.

Yr. obt sevt
Tho P Jackson
Agent
