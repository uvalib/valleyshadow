Bureau Correspondence 1
Stuanton, Va
May 30 1867

Jackson Tho.P

Writes on behalf of Alice Moore (Freed girl) asking that information be sought of her father Roderick Moore living with Wm Hayward Ham, 10 miles from Goldsboro N.C. and of her mother Sally Moore living with Nicholas Bess, 6 miles from Goldsboro. Alice was sent to Richmond and sold in 1864-5.
