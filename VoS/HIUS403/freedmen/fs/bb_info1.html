Attached Letter

Dec 26th 1867

Capt John A McDonnell
Sub Asst Comm
Winchester

Captain

Betsey Brown (col'd) sold from this county some 20 years ago has reached Staunton from Mississippi with three children and her means are exhausted. She is a daughter of Garbriel Brown (col) who is living about four miles from Winches- ter on the Chanelestorro (river) Please have enquiry made for Gabriel or he can send money to pay her passage to Winchester or if he is not able I respect- fully ask transportation for her adn her children as she is penniless

Very respectfully
Yr obt serv
Thos P Jackson
Agent
