Bureau Correspondence 2
Bureau R. F. and A. L
Head Qus. 9th Sub Dist. Va
Winchester, Va, Aug 31 / 67

Respectfully returned to Mr Tho P Jackson agent.

Hanna Collins is now here living with George W. Grey col'd, as his wife.

John A McDonnell
Capt A. S. A. C.
