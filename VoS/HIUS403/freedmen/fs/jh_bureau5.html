Bureau Correspondence 5
Stuanton, Va
Dec 28, 1867
Jackson Tho P
Agent

Refers to a former eltter to which no answer has been received asking that enquiry be made at Danville, VA for James Harris, and that he be made to return + provide for his wife or send her some assistance Encloses a letter used by his wife from him, to used in finding him
