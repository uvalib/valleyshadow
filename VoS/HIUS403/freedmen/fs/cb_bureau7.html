Bureau Correspondence 7
Bureau Refugees, Freedmen, & Abandond Lands
Head Quarters Asst. Commr., D.C.,
WASHINGTON, MAR. 4, 1868

Respectfully returned.
Attention invited to enclosed copy of a letter from Rev. S. E. Colburn, Detailing the results of his interview with Cynis Bannister.

F. M. Clark
A. A. S. General
