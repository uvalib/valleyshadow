Bureau Correspondence 1
Staunton Va Nov. 29 1867
Nov. 29th 1867

Jackson Thos. P.
Agent

Asks that enquiry be made at Danville, Va for James Harris, Col'd, and that he be made to send for his wife at Staunton, or to return and take care of her. Official seal of State of VA BRFAL Dec 4 1867
