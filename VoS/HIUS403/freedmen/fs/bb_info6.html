Attached Letter


Jan 30, 1868
Capt John A McDonnell
Sub Asst Comm
Winchester

Captain
I have the honor to refer to a communication made by me to your headquarters under date Dec 26 / 67 enquirey for Gabriel Brown(c) on behalf of his daughter Betsey Brown, returned to to you. Hd Qu by endorsement at this office Jan 24 / 68 I hear this morning that Gabriel Brown lives at Stevenson's Depot near Winchester and if transportation asked for has not been received by you Gabriel if found may be able to send for Betsy and her children. She is a charge now upon friends nearly as poor as herself

Very Respectfully
Yr obt servt
Tho P Jackson
Ass Sub Asst Comm
