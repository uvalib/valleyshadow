Attached Letter


Dec. 28th 1867
Capt John A McDonnell
Sub Asst Comm
Winchester
Captain
I respectfully refer to a letter from the office in 347 12/67 Nov 29/67 asking enquiry be made at Danville Va for James Harris (col'dd) who left his wife and child at this place for the pur- pose of procuring work. No answer having been received and Elizabeth having fear ordered to have the place where she was living, she being within one or two months of confinement. She hopes James be sought and found that he came to her or send her some means of support. I enclose the letter she received from him which may assist in finding him

Very respectfully
Yr. obt sevt
Tho P Jackson
Agent
