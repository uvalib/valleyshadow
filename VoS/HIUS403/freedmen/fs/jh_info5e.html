Enclosed Letter
Danville Sept 23d 67

My Dear Wife
I landed here on Suday Night at 11 oclock I have seen all of uncles John Ropld. They all send much love to you and say you must certain to come up Christmas you must write soon and let me know how you and the Baby is and you are well or no I hope you are when I arrived I found all of My People well and hearty I kund got a home for you and I wild od By. after you al tho and of few months for you asking will as I am so tired now I will Bring this to a close your sister was well and sends much love to you so write soon and let me hear from you all tell me whether you or the Baby has been sick or no since I have been absent good bye
affectionate Husband

James Harris
