Bureau Correspondances
  • December 5, 1867: Agent Thomas P. Jackson requests action from Assistant Commissioner John A. McDonnell to locate "Uncle Flemming."
  • December 7, 1867:Case is referred to Virginia state Bureau.
  • December 11, 1867: Case is referred to local Bureau office at Richmond.
  • December 12, 1867: Local Bureau agent is assigned to the investigation.
  • December 17, 1867: Results of the investigation are submitted to Richmond Bureau.
  • December 17, 1867: Results of investigation are reported to state Bureau.
  • December 18, 1867: State Bureau forwards results to John A. McDonnell.
  • December 19, 1867: John A. McDonnell informs Thomas P. Jackson of the results of the investigation.
