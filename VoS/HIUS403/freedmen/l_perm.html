Winchester April 22 1867

Mr Thos P. Jackson
Agent
Staunton, Va

Sir

I am informed by Genl Brown that he authorized the employment of Mr Tukey for one month. You will therefore report the time from which his services commenced immediately. If a continuance of his services are necessary; you will make application to this office, stating such necessity; and asking authority to continue his services.

Very Respectfully
Your Obdt Svt
John A McDonnell
Sub Asst Comr
