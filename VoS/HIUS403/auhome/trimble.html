The items assembled here are from The Trimble Collection, housed in the University of Virginia's Special Collections. These documents pertain mostly to John D. and Joseph Trimble, both Augusta soldiers who fought in the Civil War. Though many of their personal papers survive, biographical information about John and Joseph is scarce. According to some geneology notes accompanying the collection, the Trimble family moved to Augusta in the eighteenth century, during the first wave of white settlement in the central Shenandoah Valley.
