This is a representation of a portion of the Shenandoah Valley campaign of 1864. It portrays May through October of that year. The Confederate forces are in red, the United States forces are in blue. The sp an of the film starts with New Market and ends with the Confederate forces in retreat at Waynesboro after the Battle of Fisher's Hill. The movie was composed by Professor Drew VandeCreek, a member of the University of Virginia's Valley of the Shadow project, who attained his Ph.D. in history here at the University.

The movie is in .MPG format, and can be viewed either by downloading it (press "shift" while clicking on the link) or by using a streaming internet viewer such as the one at ww w.duplexx.com.
