Augusta County
February 21, 1864

It is with pleasure i seat myself to try to rite a few lines in answer to yours although do not no what to rite as regards healthe we are all in our usual health and hoping this may find you all blest with the same blessing as health is the greatest pleasure we can have. I received you letter yesterday evening and was glad to hear frolm] you all and Jesse in particular to hear that he was not frozen nor laid up by the cold as it bin so verry cold. I think that we have had some of the coldes washer that I ever fet lfeltl this last weak was too cold for any hones lhonestl folks to be out mch [much].but the wether has mderated considerable. I have no thing good to to rite there is no good it seams when we [dol good evil is present but one consolation I have is the good lord will not forget those who do not forget him, there seems to be a great stir in our country at this time since this last call has bin made. the substitute men are nearly all gone of [offl. there is some few left that is trying to get of [offl as farmers. if they do not get of [offl they will be very apt to give them the slip for they say they will not fite so that would brake up the war. I say go it seams starvation will end it anyhow, ther is a number of farmes that there is not a man on. if the farmes are stoped I think the war must stop. they cant fight without bread. I think the southern cfederacy has gone nearly up the spout.
We have not hurd a word from rockingham since we was over at your house we do not see nor hear anthing of my folks atall. I think rite hard of them i do not no when I will get down.if nothing happens i try to get down some time this summer. you mst [mustl try and come. It isut wethwile [worthwhile] to stay at home all the time. If Jesse comes home we would pDe] verry glad. If you would come over. I think I mus close. please excuse this bad scribling. rite as soon as you can. yours truly until death

Ether E Niswander
Mary C Rolston

Fare well fare well to all below
My Jesus calls and I must go
I ranch my boat upon the sea
This land is not the land for me

Ive found the winding path of sin
A rugged path to travel in
Beyond the chilly waves i see
The land my savior bought for me

Farewell my friends i may not stay
the home i sek is far away
Where criste is not i cannot be
This land is not the land for me

My hope my heart is now on high
There all my joys and treasures lie
Where seraphs bow and bend the knee
0, that,s the land the land for me.
