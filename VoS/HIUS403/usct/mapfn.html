When black men enlisted in the United States Colored Troops, the Union did not organize their regiments by location. As a result, enlisted men from Franklin County served in at least 21 separate regiments and experienced the Civil War all over the country. Below are regiment histories, a selection of battles in which Franklin men fought, and a map illustrating where these battles took place. Information about these battles as well as the regiment histories were compiled from The Official Records of the War of the Rebellion. Histories were composed only for regiments in which four or more Franklin men fought.
