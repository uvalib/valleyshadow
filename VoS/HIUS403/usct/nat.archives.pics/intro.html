Newspaper Transcriptions Index by DATE

The newspaper transcription index is organized by county and then by topic. Over a thousand articles have been transcribed and indexed. Some readers may use the index to find articles on major events; others may choose to follow the everyday activities of local people and groups.

At the bottom of each list or page there are links to the index pages and to the corresponding topic in the other county's index. We hope that users will toggle back and forth on any topic from one county to the other.

You can also browse the newspaper articles by month and year. These files are organized by newspaper.

Please be aware that the language in these newspapers is often highly offensive. It is important to note that this highly offensive language does not represent the views of the Valley project. Please read this language not as statements of fact, but in the context of mid-nineteenth-century politics and society.

Printing Guidelines: To print a transcription, we suggest that you select only the text in the article you wish to print. Otherwise, Netscape will print the whole file, which contains a month of transcriptions and can be very long.
