Newspapers




Newspaper from the Civil War are a great way of recapturing the era. The Newspapers provide a great way for historians to understand the mood of the time period. Here is a sampling of transcripts from The Valley Spirit and the Franklin Respository -- the newspapers of Franklin County Pennsylvania.

  • The Valley Spirit Black and White January 23, 1863
  • The Valley Spirit The Negro Soldier Bill February 11, 1863
  • The Valley Spirit Boston Papers March 18, 1863
  • The Valley SpiritNegro Recruits March 25, 1863
  • The Valley Spirit Negro Recruits April 29, 1863
  • Franklin Repository Hon. Joseph Holt on Employing Slaves as Soldiers November 25, 1863
  • Franklin Repository Rebel Atrocities December 2, 1863
  • Franklin Repository Our Negro Troops December 30, 1863
  • The Valley Spirit The Negro and the War January 27, 1864
  • Franklin Repository Capture of Fort Pillow by Rebels April 20, 1964
  • Franklin Repository Rebel Atrocities May 11, 1864
  • Franklin Repository Paroled April 5, 1863

These are the actual words used in the newspapers. The USCT site does in no way condone any words that are offensive. We do feel it is important to reproduce the transcripts verbatim as to maintain historical accuracy.
