Note: The quote used on the front page of this site was found in the forward of Joseph Glatthaar's Forged in Battle: The Civil War Alliance of Black Soldiers and White Officers. The word "altar" appears in the original quote as "alter." In the interests of clarity, we have changed the spelling to "altar."
