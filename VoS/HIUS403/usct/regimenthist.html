When black men enlisted in the USCT, the Union did not organize their regiments by location. Due to this practice, enlisted men from Franklin County served in a number of regiments. They experienced the Civil War all over the country. Below is a map of a selection of the battles in which Franklin men fought. Information about these battles as well as the regiment histories were compiled from "The Official Records of the War of the Rebellion."

  • 8th Regiment Infantry
