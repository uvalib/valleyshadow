Valley Spirit, March 25, 1863, p. 3, c. 1

Negro Recruits

A negro recruiting officer visited this place last week and of course was quite a "lion" among the "free Americans of African descent;" but, as far as we know, he did not obtain a single recruit.--It is rumored that one of the "sable brethren" retorted to the urgent appeals of the recruiting officer in favor of his cause: "Nigger has nuf fin to do with dis war. Two dogs fight over a bone--did you ever see de bone fight?"
