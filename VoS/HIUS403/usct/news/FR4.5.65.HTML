Franklin Repository, April 5, 1865, p. 3, c. 1

Paroled

Alex. Lewis, col'd, who was captured at Winchester, Va. in June, 1863, at the time of defeat of Gen. Milroy, and who has been confined in Rebel prisons since that time, was paroled last week and returned to his home in this place. Aleck was a cook for the 3d Penna. Cavalry when captured.
