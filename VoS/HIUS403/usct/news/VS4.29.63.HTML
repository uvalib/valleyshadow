Valley Spirit, April 29, 1863, p. 3, c. 2

Negro Recruits

Some forty or fifty black recruits for the Massachusetts Regiments, left this for Boston, on monday morning last. We are only too glad to get rid of these worthless negroes; but we scarcely like the idea of their being credited to Massachusetts, and thus filling up her quota under the last draft, while Pennsylvania was compelled to fill her quota, under the same draft, with free white male citizens.
