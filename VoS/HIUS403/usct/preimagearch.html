Below we've archived a number of images pertaining to Franklin County and the role of blacks in the war prior to the establishment of the United States Colored Troops. Many of these images relate to the recruitment of black troops. Where possible, we've included the source of the image as well as the date it appeared in the source. Once you've entered an individual archive, click on the thumbnail to see a larger version of the image.
