Contact Information

To report a technical problem with the site, please use the Scholar's Lab Help Form. Please be as specific as possible about the nature of the problem and the part of the site where it was encountered.

If you have questions about Valley of the Shadow content, please contact the Virginia Center for Digital History through their website.

For information about donating items of historical interest, contact the Small Special Collections Library. Please note that there are no plans to expand the Valley of the Shadow project at this time.
