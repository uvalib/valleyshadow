Valley of the Shadow makes the site freely available for non-commercial uses in research, teaching, scholarship, and private study. Citation Information

To cite our sources you should refer to the particular item (for example a database or image), the project (or collection) it comes from, the University of Virginia, and the web address of the item.

For example, if you performed a search on the Augusta County Population Census the appropriate citation would be:

Augusta County, Virginia, 1860 Population Census, Valley of the Shadow: Two Communities in the American Civil War, University of Virginia Library (http://valley.vcdh.virginia.edu/govdoc/popcensus.html).
