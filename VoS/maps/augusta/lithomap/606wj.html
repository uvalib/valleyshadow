606 W. Johnson Street
Staunton, Virginia

Constructed: 1851

Original Use: Residence

Ownership History: George M. Cochran built this house in 1851 and sold it to the Wesleyan Female Institute in 1870.

Number of Stories: 2

Architectural Style: Greek Revival

Exterior Features: Brick construction with hip roof. Two- story portico over porch supported by Truscan columns. Six-panel front door with transom and sidelight.
