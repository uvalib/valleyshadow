Mary Baldwin College Main Building
Staunton, Virginia

Constructed: 1844 (wings added in 1853)

Original Use: Augusta Female Seminary

Ownership History:

Number of Stories: 2

Architectural Style: Greek Revival

Exterior Features: Brick construction with gabled roof. Three-bay portico over porch. Exterior decorated with Greek Doric columns and pediment, a cast iron balcony, and molded doors.

Interior Features: Doorways have architrave trim.
