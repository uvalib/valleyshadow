24 N. Coalter Street
Woodrow Wilson Birthplace
Staunton, Virginia

Constructed: 1846

Original Use: Residence

Ownership History: Built as the manse for the First Presbyterian Church. Thomas Woodrow Wilson was born in the house in 1856.

Number of Stories: 2 and basement

Architectural Style: Greek Revival.

Exterior Features: Brick construction with hip roof and entrance porch with one-story pediment. Greek Revival entrance portico with fluted Doric columns. Three-story rear portico overlooking garden.

Interior Features: N/A
