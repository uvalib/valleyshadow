The Facklers moved to Augusta in the early 1860s. Lizzie Fackler lived with her family in Staunton in Augusta County, Virginia. She remained in Staunton during the war.

Fackler Family: 1870 Census

  • Lizzie Fackler to Mary [?] , November 18, 1862

    Eliza "Lizzie" Fackler writes to Mary about the state of affairs in Staunton. She describes the fear of invasion by Union soldiers, resulting in the flight of a number of residents for a week. She also mentions news concerning friends and acquaintances around Staunton, including a wedding, injuries, and illnesses.
