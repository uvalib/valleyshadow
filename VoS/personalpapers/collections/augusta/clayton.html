Melvina ("Melly") Clayton lived near Staunton in Augusta County, Virginia. Melly never married. According to family legend, she was about to marry when her sister, Mary Ann Summers, died in 1862. Melly dedicated her life to raising her children. After the war, Melly lived with her brothers James A. and John W. Clayton and her sister's children, but she was not listed in the census for the household. This information has been provided by Clayton McClure Brooks, a descendant of Melly.
