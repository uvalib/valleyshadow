William E. Tolbert

  • William E. Tolbert to Emma Tolbert, [?] 4, 1864
    William Tolbert writes to his sister Emma while stationed in Tennessee, where he has met other soldiers from Chambersburg.
  • William E. Tolbert to Emma Tolbert, February 26, 1865
    William Tolbert asks his sister Emma to write soon and to send photographs with her letter.
  • William E. Tolbert to Emma Tolbert, March 11, 1865
    William Tolbert writes to his sister Emma that he has been transferred to Newberne, Mississippi, and he describes the refugees in the area.
