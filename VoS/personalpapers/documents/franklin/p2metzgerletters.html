• Henry C. Metzger to His Father, June 16, 1861
    Metzger writes to his father about his regiment's position and possible movement in Northern Virginia.
  • Henry C. Metzger to His Father, October 29, 1864
    Metzger complains to his father about being kept in the hospital and not being able to vote.
  • Henry C. Metzger to His Father, January 12, 1865
    Metzger writes to his father about the execution of a deserter, recent thunderstorms, and a humorous mistake in the playing of 'Reveille.'
