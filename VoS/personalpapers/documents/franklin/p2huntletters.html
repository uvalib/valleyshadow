• E. and Fannie Hunt to Their Cousins, December 1, 1861
    E. and Fannie Hunt write to Lyman Foster and his wife of illness in their family and among neighbors. They also express support for defeating the rebels.
  • E. and Fannie Hunt to Their Cousins, December 1, 1861
    Fannie Hunt writes to her cousins, Lyman Foster and his wife, about her mother-in-law's efforts to take care of sick family, and about the cold, snowy weather.
