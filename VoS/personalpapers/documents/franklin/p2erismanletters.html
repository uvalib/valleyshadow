• Henry M. Erisman to His Brother, March 12, 1862
    Erisman describes army life in Nashville. He also tells about skirmishes at the picket line, including one involving a ruse by the rebels.
  • Henry M. Erisman to His Brother, August 18, 1862
    Erisman complains that he has not received any letters from his brother. He also describes his conversations with rebel soldiers, the death of a comrade, and his dislike of an officer.
  • Henry M. Erisman to His Brother, November 1, 1862
    Erisman provides a description of marching and camping in snow. He also talks about skirmishes, including one with General Bragg and Kirby Smith.
