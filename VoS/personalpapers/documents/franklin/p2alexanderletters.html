• Alvin H. Alexander to His Sister Lizzie, February 12, 1863
    Alexander describes the comforts of camp life and comments on his like and dislike of various officers around him.
  • Alvin H. Alexander to His Sister Sallie, September 23, 1863
    Alexander describes the slow mail pick-up in camp and talks about visiting his sister in Callensburg. He also mentions the need for more soldiers.
