Papers of the Ott Family


  • Tyler & Son to Henry Ott, June 28, 1865
    In this letter, Tyler & Son encloses a receipt for goods purchased as well as discussing the demands for various goods such as cattle, apples, cheese, and soap.
  • J. A. Spencer to Henry Ott, September 17, 1866
    In this letter, J. A. Spencer informs Henry Ott of the prices for Dry goods locally, mentioning several local companies in the process.
  • O. A. Spencer & Co. to Henry Ott, September 18, 1866
    In this letter, O. A. Spencer & Co. informs Henry Ott of the price of nails and places to buy cloth.
