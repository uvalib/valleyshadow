• Jonathan M. Shipman to Kenton Harper, June 11, 1860
    Lieut. Jonathan M. Shipman writes his Col Kenton Harper to complain that his captain, Capt. O. H. Lumay, has been deficient in his duty to his company. Col Kenton Harper dismisses the charges in a note at the end.
  • Edmund Pickens, Sampson Holsom, and James Gamble to Kenton Harper, March 9, 1861
    Members of the Chicasaw Nation thank Col. Kenton Harper for his past service to them from 1851 through 1852.
