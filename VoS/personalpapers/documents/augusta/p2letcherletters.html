John Letcher

  • Philip H. Trout to John Letcher, July 22, 1861
    Captain Trout writes to the governor to clarify whether or not his company is to be sent away from their current duty in Staunton.
  • Washington Hilleary and D. D. Morrall to John Letcher, August 7, 1861
    Having been accused of aiding Federal troops, Dr. Hilleary writes to Gov. Letcher and asks for a speedy hearing so that he might clear his name.
