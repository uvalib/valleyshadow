John N. Hite
  • John N. Hite to Unknown, May 30, 1864
    Hite writes to a cousin, reporting his health and news from home. He requests monetary aid from his cousin.
  • John N. Hite to Unknown, July 7, 1864
    Hite informs his correspondent that they have moved to a new prisoner of war camp and requests money.
  • John N. Hite to J. O. Miller, September 8, 1864
    Hite acknowledges his receipt of the letter and draft from Miller and says that he wishes to get home soon.
