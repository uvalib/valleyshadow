Description of Page: political articles about Letcher, Democrats
County Claims
(Column 2)
Summary: County court has appointed a committee of justices to handle county claims. Also includes a tally of delinquent tax payers of county taxes. Of the roughly 700 total delinquent tax payers, 441 had no property found, 162 had property removed, 75 were not found, and 23 were listed as other.
New Fire Engine
(Column 2)
Summary: The Staunton Fire Company bought a slightly used fire engine from Baltimore.

(Column 3)
