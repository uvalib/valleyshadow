Staunton Spectator: October 4, 1859

Go To Page : 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 |

-Page 01-

By Request.; The Coral Ring; By Mrs. Harriet Beecher Stowe
(Column 3)
Summary: Harriet Beecher Stowe short story. Seems to be more about manners and society (like Jane Austen), not politics.

-Page 02-

The Democratic Imbroglio
(Column 1)
Summary: Explains how the Democratic party is divided over popular sovereignty and Stephen Douglas. This affords the Opposition a great opportunity, and they may even pick up some disaffected Democrats.
The Science of Horse Taming
(Column 2)
Summary: Dr. Lee has been performing feats of horse taming in the area and is teaching several local men his methods.
(Names in announcement: Dr. R.H. Lee, William Shumate)
The New Methodist Church
(Column 2)
Summary: Cornerstone laid at new Methodist church; account of the ceremonies.
(Names in announcement: James Cochran, Col. Turk, Col. Starrett)
Gas at the Lunatic Asylum
(Column 1)
Summary: The Western Lunatic Asylum has contracted with the new Staunton Gas Works.
(Names in announcement: Mr. Waterhouse, Mr. Bowes)
Sale of Hotel Property
(Column 2)
Summary: McClung has sold his interest in the Virginia Hotel to Jordan.
(Names in announcement: James McClung, William Jordan)
For the Spectator; Agriculture of Augusta County
(Column 4)
Summary: Part of continuing series on Augusta agriculture. This installment includes a discussion of the soil, defining regions in part by whose land they cross.
(Names in announcement: A.H.H. Stuart, Capt. Guy, Capt. K. Harper, Major Walker, S.B. Finley, James Craig)
To the Editors of the Spectator
(Column 3)
Summary: Letter praising Staunton and the surrounding area for having so much progress, so many stores and merchants.
(Names in announcement: Mr. King, Mr. Fishburn, Mr. Withrow, Mr. Darnall, Mr. Antrim, Mr. C. King)
