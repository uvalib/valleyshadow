Description of Page: Political articles about election.
Elect William L. Goggin Governor...
(Column 2)
Summary: Takes issue with the Charlottesville Jeffersonian's claim that electing Goggin will result in the eventual triumph of Black Republicans. Describes the Democracy as being perceived by the North as the Southern sectional party.
Goggin Club
(Column 1)
Summary: Organization of Goggin club of Staunton.
(Names in announcement: D.S. Young, Joseph Waddell, William Guy, James Bumgardner, G.W. Imboden, B[olivar] Christian)
For the Spectator
(Column 3)
Summary: Report of the first meeting of the Goggin club of Greenville.
(Names in announcement: A.G. Christian, John Wilson, Capt. Henry McFadden, Capt. John Hawpe, George McClure, Dr. V.T. Churchman, A.M. Hawpe, E.T. McGuffin, J.A. Patterson, John McClure, John Newton, Henry Burch, John Tate, William Baylor, William Harman, Bolivar Christian, Robert Doyle)
Mail Lettings
(Column 3)

(Names in announcement: John Harman, James Trotter, Benjamin Estill, Thomas Burke, Beniah Vint)
For the Spectator; Congressional Fight-Harris and Skinner
(Column 5)
Summary: Account of "family quarrel" among the Democrats.
(Names in announcement: Harris, James Skinner)
