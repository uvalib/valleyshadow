Negro Suffrage in the Senate of Pennsylvania
(Column 4)
Summary: The second series of extracts from the debate in the state Senate over the issue of black suffrage.
Amendments to the Constitution
(Column 2)
Summary: Issuing a cautionary note, the article warns readers that the implementation of any further amendments could undermine the sanctity of the Constitution.
Origin of Article: Journal of Commerce
The Radicals and the President--Thad Stevens Trying to Cover Up the Breach
(Column 3)
Summary: With an eye toward maintaining their majority in Congress, says the article, the Republicans have sought to play down their differences with the President, hoping to dupe the voters into supporting the "Jacobins" in the upcoming elections.
Origin of Article: New York Herald
The Concert by the "Chambersburg Musical Union"
(Column 4)
Summary: Though he categorizes most musical and artistic performances in Chambersburg as lacking in quality, the writer testifies that the most recent production of the town's "Musical Union" was "faultless."
(Names in announcement: R. A. McClure)
