Be Supportive
(Column 2)
Summary: An appeal to subscribers to join the Vindicator in supporting Douglas for President.
[No Title]
(Column 1)
Summary: A list of possible names for Chief Justice of the county including McCue, Bickle, Guy, Newton and Koiner.
(Names in announcement: Maj. J. Marshall McCue, R.G. Bickle, R. Guy, John Newton, A. Koiner)
[No Title]
(Column 2)
Summary: Discusses the retirement of Messrs Wadell from the editorial and proprietary management of the Staunton Spectator and the introduction of the new editor, Mr. Mauzy.
(Names in announcement: Mr. Wadell, Mr. Wadell, Mr. Mauzy)
Baptist Association
(Column 3)
Summary: Baptist convention meets at Staunton.
The County Court
(Column 3)
Summary: Court cases including Moyers for Bestiality, represented by Baldwin, Tom Cato (free black) for larceny defended by Kinney, and McMullen for larceny defended by Doyle and Bumgardner. Commonwealth's Attorney was Harman.
(Names in announcement: Moyers, alias Cooper Tom Cato, McMullen, Esq. R.L. Doyle, Esq. J. BumgardnerJr., Gen. W.H. Harman, Col. Baldwin, Esq. Arch'd Kinney)
The Concert
(Column 3)
Summary: Discussion of the concert given by Turner's Cornet Band, Orchestra and Glee Club. A good time was had by all.
Richmond Convention
(Column 4)
Summary: Summary of several newspapers' reporting of the Richmond Convention.
Election Returns
(Column 6)
Summary: Election returns for the Judical race between Fultz and Thompson, the Attorney's race between Doyle and Harman, and the races for Clerk and Commissioner of Revenue.
Attention Firemen
(Column 6)
Summary: Capt. Waters said that the monthly parade of the Staunton Fire Association has been postponed.
(Names in announcement: Capt. Waters)
Elliot's Knob
(Column 7)
Summary: There will be a picnic on Elliot's Knob on June 15th and Capts. Imboden and Beyiot and their companies as well as all the ladies and gentleman of Augusta County are invited.
(Names in announcement: Capt. Pohner, Capt. Imboden, Capt. Beyiot, Capt. Moody, Capt. Finks)
Augusta County
(Column 7)
Summary: Congratulates Gen. Harman on being elected Commonwealth's Attorney for Augusta County.
(Names in announcement: Gen. W.H. Harman, Mr. Doyle)
