Summary: Charles Hudson of Mecklenberg was convicted of the second-degree murder of his slave, Jane, and sentenced to 18 years in prison.

The Insult to the Tenth Legion
(Column 2)
Summary: The Address of the State Executive Committee of the National Democracy to the people of Virginia. They looked at the recommendation of Jacob Baylor, namely that the electoral votes of Virginia be based on the percentage of popular vote Breckinridge and Douglas received, but some Breckinridge supporters would not accept that. The Vindicator calls for sensible Breckinridge people to turn to Douglas.
(Names in announcement: Esq. Jacob Baylor, Thos. Michie, W.A. Abney, W.H. Watts, Alex B. Cochran, Col. H.L. Opie, W.H. Peyton)

(Column 4)
