Go To Page : 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 |

-Page 01-

Description of Page: Candidates announcements in column 3.

-Page 02-

District Convention
(Column 2)
Summary: The Democratic Convention of Augusta and Rockingham counties met in Harrisonburg to elect delegates to the Charleston Convention.
(Names in announcement: Dr. S.H. Moffett, S.M. Yost, S.T. Walker, Capt. J.A. Harman, Mr. Hunter)
Murder in Rockingham
(Column 2)
Summary: A man named Smallwood, formerly from Augusta, shot and killed Mr. James Devier in Bridgewater, Rockingham county.
(Names in announcement: John Dinkle, Hugh Devier, James Devier, Smallwood, Hansbarger)
Valedictory Address
(Column 2)
Summary: Alex. Cochran delivered valedictory address before the Washington Society at UVA.
(Names in announcement: Alex B. Cochran, G.M. Cochran)
Accident
(Column 2)
Summary: Harman's son lost a finger in an accident with a knife.
(Names in announcement: Capt. John A. Harman, Dr. Fuqua)
District Convention
(Column 4)
