Why Despond!
(Column 1)
Summary: The editor cautions readers not to despond at every failure in the war but to be encouraged by the frequent successes.
[No Title]

Stonewall Jackson's Admission into Heaven
(Column 4)
Summary: This anecdote relates that when two angels came to take Stonewall Jackson back to heaven with them, he was nowhere to be found. When they returned to heaven, they learned that Jackson had outflanked them and arrived there ahead of them.
Origin of Article: Boston Recorder
A Tribute of Respect
(Column 4)
Summary: The Board of Directors of the Central Bank of Virginia passed a resolution on November 26 in memory of William Kinney, the president of the bank who died on November 25.
(Names in announcement: William Kinney, W. H. Tams)
Married
(Column 4)
Summary: Mary E. Murray of Staunton married Cyrus Grow of Waynesboro on November 25, with Rev. Mr. Dice officiating.
(Names in announcement: Rev. Mr. Dice, Mr. Cyrus Grow, Miss Mary E. Murray)
To All To Whom It May Concern
(Column 5)
Summary: This notice informs indigent and wounded soldiers and sailors, widows and minor children of dead soldiers, and indigent families of current soldiers that the General Assembly has called for the county courts to compile a list of such people in an effort to administer aid to them.
