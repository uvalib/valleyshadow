-Page 02-

Description of Page: Report on the Henry Clay Monument and the restoration of Mt. Vernon.
What Will They Do?
(Column 1)
Summary: Editorial about Kansas. Notes the different Southern conventions making recommendations, blames the Democrats for stirring up trouble.
Blue Ridge Tunnel
(Column 2)
Summary: Predictions notwithstanding, the tunnel will not be completed for another 6 months. SS thinks that the progress is a bit too slow.
Concert
(Column 2)
Summary: Sax Horn Band will be performing at Union Hall; SS highly recommends it.
(Names in announcement: A. J. Turner)
Mr. Davis' Nursery
(Column 2)
Summary: Endorsement of Davis' local nursery. He has thousands of fruit trees, and many beautiful flowers to interest the ladies.
(Names in announcement: Franklin Davis)
Harvest
(Column 2)
Summary: More about the wheat harvest. Even though it is unprecedentedly late, it appears to be quite good.
More of a good thing
(Column 2)
Summary: SS suggests that people visit the new soda fountain in Young's drug store, to refresh themselves in the summer heat.
(Names in announcement: Dr. W. B. Young)
Washington College
(Column 1)
Summary: Report on commencement at Washington college. Bottom is too blurry to read.
(Names in announcement: J. L. Massie, M. H. Christian, John H. McCue)
Our New Market
(Column 3)
Summary: SS wondering when the new market house will be completed; not having one is very injurous to the town. Its residents are inconvenienced and it prevents newcomers from settling.
