Description of Page: Lots of news about a lynching in Rockingham Co.
The Old Game
(Column 2)
Summary: SS is complaining that the VA Democratic party is sowing the seeds of sectionalism for the 1860 election by claiming to be the true protector of the South.
The Comet
(Column 2)
Summary: Despite predictions to the contrary, the world was not destroyed by a comet on 6/13.
Another Split
(Column 2)
Summary: Southern members of the General Assembly of the New School Presbyterian Church have decided to secede and form a new church, one that will not discuss slavery. Should not affect Virginia churches, since most of them are Old School.
