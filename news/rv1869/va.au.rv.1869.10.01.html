Summary: Dr. James Johnston, Surgeon Dentist, has resumed practice in Staunton.
(Names in announcement: Dr. James Johnston)
[No Title]
(Column 01)
Summary: Preparations are progressing for the Augusta County Fair. It promises to be the largest county fair in the state.
[No Title]
(Column 01)
Summary: The commissioners of streets are improving Vine and Winchester Streets by overseeing the work of a number of hired laborers. The $300 appropriation by the city seems to be getting the job done. The paper pleads for more such projects, and suggests buying mules, carts, and forming a chain gang of prisoners jailed for petty offenses.
Married
(Column 02)
Summary: James P. Bowman and Miss Elizabeth C. Hiser, both of Augusta, were married on September 22nd at the residence of the bride's father by the Rev. O. Beard.
(Names in announcement: James P. Bowman, Elizabeth C. Hiser, Rev. O. Beard)
Married
(Column 02)
Summary: Powell Harrison and Miss Jaret Knox Fauntleroy, daughter of Col. Charles M. Fauntleroy, were married in Leesburg on September 23rd by the Rev. C. T. Davis.
(Names in announcement: Powell Harrison, Janet Knox Fauntleroy, Col. Charles M. Fauntleroy, Rev. C. T. Davis)
Married
(Column 02)
Summary: Alex C. King of Augusta and Mrs. Maria P. Blown of Albemarle were married in Staunton by the Rev. George Kramer.
(Names in announcement: Alex C. King, Maria P. Blown, Rev. George Kramer)
Died
(Column 02)
Summary: Mrs. Mildred Hoy, wife of Capt. Isaac Hoy, died near Mt. Solon on September 18th. She was 66 years old.
(Names in announcement: Mildred Hoy, Capt. Isaac Hoy)
Died
(Column 02)
Summary: Washington Swink died at his residence near Moscow on September 19th. He was 56 years old.
(Names in announcement: Washington Swink)
Died
(Column 02)
Summary: John T. Arnall died of paralysis at his residence in Staunton on September 22nd. He was 66 years old.
(Names in announcement: John T. Arnall)
