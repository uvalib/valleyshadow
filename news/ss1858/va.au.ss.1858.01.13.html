Description of Page: Announcement for "War in Kansas" photo display at local gallery.
The Filibuster
(Column 1)
Summary: Discusses Kansas, State bank issues.
Large Hog
(Column 4)
Summary: Wm. F. Ast & Bro. slaughtered a "mammoth porker" for J. Wayt Bell last week. It weighed 410 lbs.
(Names in announcement: William F. Ast, J. Wayt. Bell)

-Page 03-

Married
(Column 1)
Summary: Francis E. Johnson married to Matilda East, both of Augusta, by Rev. J. Killian near Fishersville on 12/18/1857.
(Names in announcement: Francis E. Johnson, Matilda East, Rev. J. Killian)
Married
(Column 1)
Summary: William Towberman and Catherine H. Fauber, both of Augusta, married by Rev. J. Killian on 12/29/1857.
(Names in announcement: William Towberman, Catharine H. Fauber, Rev. J. Killian)
Died
(Column 1)
Summary: Ragen Gusinger, age 58, died Staunton, 12/23/1857.
(Names in announcement: Ragen Gusinger)
Origin of Article: Salem Register
Tribute of Respect
(Column 1)
Summary: Memorial from Sons of Temperance Fountain Division No. 154 to Samuel H. Wright, who died 1/5/1858, age 22. Signed by James Murry, A.V. Tare, U.D. Poe.
(Names in announcement: Samuel H. Wright, James Murry, A. V. Tare, U. D. Poe)
