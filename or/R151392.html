Records Related to Franklin County Regiments


From: F. J. PORTER, Assistant Adjutant-Gen.
Undated

Summary:
Union Colonel and Assistant Adjutant for the Department of Pennsylvania Fitz-John Porter writes Colonel G. H. Thomas with orders to march with his cavalry regiment to Chambersburg.

Col. G. H. THOMAS,
Second Cavalry, Carlisle, Pa.:

Gen. Patterson directs you to march at once to Chambersburg and report to commanding officer. If troops are at Hagerstown, go there.

F. J. PORTER,
Assistant Adjutant-Gen.

Bibliographic Information : Letter Reproduced from The War of The Rebellion: Official Records of the Union and Confederate Armies, Series 1, Volume 51, Serial No. 107, Pages 392, Broadfoot Publishing Company, Wilmington, NC, 1997.
