Records Related to Augusta County Regiments


From: JNO. WITHERS, Assistant Adjutant-Gen.
August 12, 1863.

Summary:
August, 1863, Confederate Special Orders replacing H. B. Davidson with J. Q. A. Nadenbousch as commander of the post at Staunton.

SPECIAL ORDERS, No. 191. ADJT. AND INSP. GEN.'S OFFICE,

Richmond, Va.,

August 12, 1863.

XV. Col. J. Q. A. Nadenbousch, Second Regt. Virginia Volunteers, will relieve Col. H. B. Davidson in command of the post at Staunton, Va. Col. Davidson, on being relieved, will report to Maj. Gen. S. B. Buckner, commanding, &c., at Knoxville, Tenn., for assignment to duty.

By command of the Secretary of War:

JNO. WITHERS,
Assistant Adjutant-Gen.
