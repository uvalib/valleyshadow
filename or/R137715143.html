Records Related to Franklin County Regiments


From: D. N. COUCH, Maj.-Gen.
July 30, 1864

Summary:
Union General Darius Couch, commanding at Chambersburg, writes on July 30, 1864, to General William Averell to report that Confederate forces reached the outskirts of Chambersburg.

Gen. AVERELL:

CHAMBERSBURG,

July 30, 1864

The enemy are just at the edge of town. Let me know what you intend doing.

D. N. COUCH,
Maj.-Gen.

Bibliographic Information : Letter Reproduced from The War of The Rebellion: Official Records of the Union and Confederate Armies, Series 1, Volume 37, Serial No. 71, Pages 514, Broadfoot Publishing Company, Wilmington, NC, 1997.
