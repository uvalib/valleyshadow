Records Related to Franklin County Regiments


From: JOHN E. WOOL, Maj.-Gen.
September 7, 1862.

Summary:
Union General John Wool writes Pennsylvania Governor Andrew Curtin in September, 1862, concerning movements to and from Chambersburg.

HDQRS. EIGHTH ARMY CORPS,

A. G. CURTIN,
Governor of Pennsylvania, Harrisburg, Pa.:

Baltimore,

September 7, 1862.

GOVERNOR:

I have ordered Lieut. Crosby, if he left Chambersburg with his artillery, to go to Harrisburg. If he arrives there you can detain him.

JOHN E. WOOL,
Maj.-Gen.

Bibliographic Information : Letter Reproduced from The War of The Rebellion: Official Records of the Union and Confederate Armies, Series 1, Volume 51, Serial No. 107, Pages 798, Broadfoot Publishing Company, Wilmington, NC, 1997.
