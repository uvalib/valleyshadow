Records Related to Franklin County Regiments


From: F. J. PORTER, Assistant Adjutant-Gen.
April 23.

Summary:
Union Colonel Fitz-John Porter writes in April, 1861, to General Wynkoop with orders that General Williams go to Chambersburg to take command of troops gathering in the town.

Gen. WYNKOOP,
York:

HARRISBURG,

April 23.

Gen. Williams will repair immediately to Chambersburg, Pa., and assume command of the forces mustered into the service of the United States, and as rapidly as possible put them in a condition for active service.

By command of Lieut.-Gen. Scott:

F. J. PORTER,
Assistant Adjutant-Gen.

Bibliographic Information : Letter Reproduced from The War of The Rebellion: Official Records of the Union and Confederate Armies, Series 1, Volume 51, Serial No. 107, Pages 366, Broadfoot Publishing Company, Wilmington, NC, 1997.
