Records Related to Augusta County Regiments


From: S. COOPER, Adjutant and Inspector Gen.
December 2, 1861.

Summary:
Confederate Adjutant General Samuel Cooper writes Colonel Michael Harman, commanding at Staunton, with December, 1861, orders for troop movements.

Col. M. G. HARMAN, Staunton, Va.:

RICHMOND,

December 2, 1861.

Send to their destination without delay, by telegraph or express, the following to Gen. Donelson and Col. Starke, at Lewisburg:

Retire with your respective commands to the nearest point on the Virginia and Tennessee Railroad, and proceed thence to Bowling Green, Ky., and report to Gen. A. S. Johnston.

S. COOPER,
Adjutant and Inspector Gen.
