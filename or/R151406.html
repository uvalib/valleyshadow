Records Related to Augusta County Regiments


From: S. M. BARTON, Acting Assistant Adjutant-Gen.
December 5, 1861.

Summary:
Confederate Assistant Adjutant S. M. Barton forwards Colonel William B. Taliaferro orders from Staunton in December, 1861.

HDQRS. ARMY OF THE NORTHWEST,

Col. W. B. TALIAFERRO,
Cmdg. Brigade, Strasburg:

Staunton, Va.,

December 5, 1861.

COL.:

The general commanding directs me to inform you that he sent you to-day the following telegram, viz:
CONFIDENTIAL. HDQRS. NORTHWEST ARMY,
Staunton, December 5.

Col. W. B. TALIAFERRO, commanding brigade, will proceed from Strasburg to Winchester with his command.
W. W. LORING.

It is somewhat doubtful whether it reached you; at all events, carry out its instructions.

I am, sir, very respectfully,

S. M. BARTON,
Acting Assistant Adjutant-Gen.
