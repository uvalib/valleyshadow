Records Related to Franklin County Regiments


From: A. PLEASONTON.
December 8, 1863.

Summary:
Maj. Gen. Alfred Pleasonton commanded the Cavalry Corps of the Army of the Potomac which included the 17th Pa. Cavalry. Pleasonton provides a list of casualties.

Brig. Gen. S. WILLIAMS, Adjt. Gen., Army of the Potomac.

December 8, 1863.

GENERAL

I have the honor to forward the reports of division commanders, showing the duties performed by the Cavalry Corps during the recent operations of the army. These reports are sufficiently full without any additional report from these headquarters.

[1]See revised statement, p. 685.

I have the honor to be, your obedient servant,

A. PLEASONTON.
