Records Related to Franklin County Regiments


From: D. N. COUCH, Maj.-Gen.
October 19, 1863

Summary:
In this October, 1863, dispatch, Union General Darius Couch writes from Chambersburg to General in Chief Henry Halleck concerning sending troops toward Harper's Ferry.

Received 3.45 p. m.
Maj. Gen. H. W. HALLECK:

CHAMBERSBURG, PA.,

October 19, 1863

Cmdg. officer at Harper's Ferry has notified Gen. Ferry, in command of my forces in Maryland, that he only wanted cavalry. This latter has been sent to him. The infantry and artillery will return to Hagerstown for the present.

D. N. COUCH,
Maj.-Gen.

Bibliographic Information : Letter Reproduced from The War of The Rebellion: Official Records of the Union and Confederate Armies, Series 1, Volume 29, Serial No. 49, Pages 358, Broadfoot Publishing Company, Wilmington, NC, 1997.
