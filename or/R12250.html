Records Related to Augusta County Regiments


From: C. Q. TOMPKINS, Col., Virginia Volunteers.
May 29, 1861.

Summary:
Colonel C. Q. Tompkins reports from Staunton on the gathering of Union troops along the Ohio border in May, 1861.

Col. R. S. GARNETT.

STAUNTON, VA.,

May 29, 1861.

Troops are rapidly gathering along the Ohio border, several hundred at Gallipolis, and a large camp at Oak Hill. David Kirkpatrick, bearer of above to Staunton, will be in Richmond to-morrow, with letters from Col. Tompkins.

C. Q. TOMPKINS,
Col., Virginia Volunteers.
