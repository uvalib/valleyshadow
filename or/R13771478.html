Records Related to Franklin County Regiments


From: JOHN S. SCHULTZE, Assistant Adjutant-Gen.
July 27, 1864.

Summary:
Union Assistant Adjutant for the Department of the Susquehanna at Chambersburg writes General William Averell in July, 1864, to announce the sending of reinforcements.

Brig.-Gen. AVERELL:

CHAMBERSBURG,

July 27, 1864.

I have ordered Capt. George D. Stroud with his mounted company to report to you without delay, from Waynesborough, for duty.

By command of Maj.-Gen. Couch:

JOHN S. SCHULTZE,
Assistant Adjutant-Gen.

Bibliographic Information : Letter Reproduced from The War of The Rebellion: Official Records of the Union and Confederate Armies, Series 1, Volume 37, Serial No. 71, Pages 478, Broadfoot Publishing Company, Wilmington, NC, 1997.
