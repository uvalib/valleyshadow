Records Related to Franklin County Regiments


From: COUCH, Maj.-Gen.
January 16, 1864.

Summary:
In this January, 1864, dispatch, General Darius Couch, commanding the department of the Susquehanna at Chambersburg, writes Secretary of War Edwin Stanton regarding rumors that a mob planned to prevent the inauguration of Pennsylvania Governor Andrew Curtin.

Received 1.30 p. m.
Hon. EDWIN M. STANTON,
Secretary of War:

CHAMBERSBURG, PA.,

January 16, 1864.

It is reported that a mob will prevent Governor Curtin being inaugurated on the 19th instant. I think not. By invitation of the Legislature committee I shall be present.

COUCH,
Maj.-Gen.
