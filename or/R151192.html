Records Related to Augusta County Regiments


From: NAT. TYLER, Maj., Cmdg.
July 22, 1861.

Summary:
Confederate Major Nat Tyler writes from Staunton to Robert E. Lee in July, 1861, to discuss troops under his command at Staunton.

Gen. R. E. LEE:

STAUNTON,

July 22, 1861.

I am here by order of Gen. Jackson with the remnant of six companies of the Twentieth Regiment--A, B, C, D, E, and F--with orders to report at general headquarters. I have with me Companies G and H, which are on parole. Shall I bring all the companies to Richmond? Answer by telegraph.

NAT. TYLER,
Maj., Cmdg.
