Records Related to Augusta County Regiments


From: E. O. C. ORD.
March 5, 1865. (Received 1.20 p.m.)

Summary:
E. O. C. Ord forwards General Grant a March, 1865, telegram announcing Sheridan's capture of Staunton and other movements in the Shenandoah Valley.

Lieut.-Gen. GRANT:

March 5, 1865. (Received 1.20 p.m.)

The following telegram is just received:

HDQRS. TWENTY-FIFTH ARMY CORPS,
March 5, 1865--12.40 p.m.

Intelligent deserters report that the medical stores and machinery have been moved to Lynchburg; that Sheridan had captured Gen. Early and his entire command, numbering 1,800 men. Four brigades of Longstreet's corps have been sent to intercept Sheridan at Lynchburg, if possible. Sheridan captured Staunton on Thursday last. These deserters left Richmond at 7 p.m. yesterday.
G. WEITZEL,
Maj.-Gen.

E. O. C. ORD.
