Records Related to Augusta County Regiments


From: R. E. LEE, Gen., Cmdg.
August 27, 1861.

Summary:
Robert E. Lee writes General John Floyd in August, 1861, to discuss regiments expected to arrive in Staunton.

HDQRS.,

Gen. JOHN B. FLOYD,
Cmdg. Army of Kanawha, Camp Gauley, west of Lewisburg, Va.:

Valley Mountain,

August 27, 1861.

GEN.:

Since my letter of yesterday I have heard that the Thirtieth Georgia Regt. was expected at Staunton, and have directed that it be ordered to join your command. This, with the Fourteen Regt. North Carolina Volunteers, which you were advised was on its way to Lewisburg, will give you two well-appointed regiments.

I have the honor to be, your obedient servant,

R. E. LEE,
Gen., Cmdg.
