Valley Memory Articles


Augusta County: "[No title]," by Unknown, 1914

Summary: Short inquiry listing from Staunton woman, requesting news/knowledge of her uncles' Confederate service

Mrs. J. F. F. Cassell, of Staunton, Va., desires to hear from surviving comrades of any of her six uncles, Samuel, Adam, John, William, Levi, and David Plecker. She would like to know the companies in which they served during the war. They were reared near Centerville (now Milnesville), Augusta County, Va., and some of them later lived in Rockingham County. She inquires also for the company in which Isaac Smoot, M.D., of Madison County, Va., was enlisted.

Bibliographic Information: Source copy consulted: Confederate Veteran, Vol. 22, p. 191, 1914
