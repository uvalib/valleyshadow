Valley Southern Claims Commission Papers


Southern Claims Commission: Claim of Julia Hurley, 1873, Claim No. 16,876

Summary: Julia Hurley's claim, most likely for agricultural supplies and farm animals, was disallowed because she was never a citizen of the United States.

Items Claimed:

Item Claimed: Amount Claimed: Amount Allowed: Amount Disallowed:
The claim consists of 3 items amounting to $357.00 0 $357.00

Claims Summary:

The claimant is not a citizen of the United States. She was born in Ireland & was never naturalized.

The claim must be rejected.
