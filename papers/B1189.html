Freedmen's Bureau Records: Will A. Coulter to John W. Jordan, March 21, 1868

Summary:
Jordan is informed that his monthly report is overdue.

Bur. R. F. and A. L.
Hd Qrs Asst. Comr, Va Jordan, John W.
A.S.A.Comr

March 21, 1868

Richmond, Va.

Sir,

Your personal report for the month of February 1868, under Circular No 12, Series 1866, War Dept., Bur. &c, has not been received at these Head Quarters.

An explanation of the reason of this delay is required by the Bvt. Brig. Genl, Asst. Comr.

Respectfully yours

Will A. Coulter
Bvt. Capt. U.S. Army
A.A.A.Genl
