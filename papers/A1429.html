Augusta: Nelson H. Hotchkiss and W. B. Young to Polly W. Robberts, December 28, 1863

Summary:
Nelson Hotchkiss and W. B. Young promise to pay Mrs. Robberts in a year for the use of three servants.

$ 700. On the 25 day of December 1864 we or either of us Bind ourselves our heirs & co to pay to Mrs. [deleted: Betsy]Polly [added: W ] & Robberts Seven Hundred Dollars for the hire of two servant men & one woman names as follows. Woodson , George & Jenna - - - . This year and to furnish them with the usual amounts of clothing except Blankets

Witness our hands & seal this 28th Dday December 1863

N. H. Hotchkiss (Seal)
W B Young (Seal)

If the above Servants are rehired Mrs Robberts is to have the privalege of hiring them or finding them places. N. H. Hotchkiss
