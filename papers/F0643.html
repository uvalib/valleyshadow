Franklin County: Augustus Hover to His Mother, November 2, 1864

Summary:
Hover mentions his fear of the draft and asks about his family and friends.

Mother

November the 2nd A. D. 1864

HeadQuarters Near the Shenode valley

Dear Mother

I take the opportunity of writing a few lines to let you know that i am well. At present hoping this few lines will find you in the same state of good health Mother I may let you know of the late enlistment of my selfe to the U. S. service I enlisted on the 14 of september the reason I enlisted was I was afraid of the draft. I wish I know if any of our boys have been drafted i would like to know if Franklin Walker has been drafted or not. I would like to know how little Idea is getting along. No more at present but

remains your affectionate son

Augustus Hover

Please write soon
