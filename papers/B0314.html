Freedmen's Bureau Records: John A. McDonnell to Thomas P. Jackson, September 24, 1867

Summary:
McDonnell tells Jackson to send in his invoice for a lumber purchase immediately.

Bureau of Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands,
Head Quarters, 9th District Department of the Potomac, Mr. Thos. P. Jackson
Agent
Staunton, Va

Sept 24th 1867

Winchester, Va.,

Sir

Having received instructions to send in my Property Returns this month in time to reach Maj. White before the 25th your vouchers for purchase of Lumber arrived after my Returns were completed, and too late for me to receive Invoices for the articles from Richmond, and get in my Return in the specified time.

I therefore inclose a new set dated Oct. 1st which if returned immediately, properly signed will be sent in time for payment with the Sept. accts.

Very Respectfully
Your Obedt St

John A. McDonnell
Capt. & S.A.C.
