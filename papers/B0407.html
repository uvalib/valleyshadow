Freedmen's Bureau Records: John A. McDonnell to Roswell Waldo, September 21, 1868

Summary:
McDonnell send Waldo forms that he had requested.

Bureau R. F. & A. L.
Head Qrs. 9th Sub Dist. Va. Roswell Waldo
A.S.A.Comr
Staunton, Va.

Sept 21/68 Winchester, Va.

Sir

I have the honor to enclose one copy of Circular No. 6 1866 HdQrs Asst Comr. as requested.

I also send a supply of Teachers School Reports.

Very Respectfully
Your Obedt St

John A. McDonnell
Sub Asst Comr
