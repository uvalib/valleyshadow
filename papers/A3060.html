Augusta County: A. H. Pirkey to A. W. Kersh, November 4, 1868

Summary:
Pinkey writes back to Kersh asking him to hold on to his chairs until the entire set is done, as he has been sick.

Mr A. W. Kersh Port Republic

Nov 4th 1868

Dear Sir

yours of the 24th to hand I have not been able to get up after those chairs on account of sickness & I cant [unclear: say] when I will. It may be that I will wait untill they are all done, make [deleted: one] [unclear: 2] Rocking Chairs with the 5 Sets.

Hold on to them I will be certain to take them. if I dont get up untill you have them all don write to me make the Rocking Chairs high in the back.

Yours Resp

A. H. Pinkey

Address Port Republic
Rockingham Co
Va
