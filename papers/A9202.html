Augusta County: Tyler & Son to Enos Ott, May 19, 1864

Summary:
In this brief note, Tyler & Son asks Enos for provisions and informs him of their receipt of his check.

Messrs, A & E. Ott
Moffetts Creek
Augusta Co. Va.

May 19, 1864

Richmond

Dear Sirs:

Your Check to hand for $450.40 We have been very busy with the wounded the last few days. We want all kinds of provisions here very badly Send what you can of any kind

Most Resply

Tyler & Son
