Freedman's Bureau Records: R.S. Lacey to Andrew Mahoney, January 11, 1868

Summary:

Bureau of R. F. + A. L
Head Quarters of the Sub Dist of VA Andrew Mahoney

January 11, 1868

Lynchburg Va

Respectfully referred to Lieut Andrew Mahony 4th US.A. A.S. A.C. for report. The within named paper was refd A. Mahoney Jan 4 1868.

R.S. Lacey
Agent in Charge
