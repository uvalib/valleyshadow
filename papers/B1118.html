Freedmen's Bureau Records: W. Storer How to Orlando Brown, September 16, 1865

Summary:
How requests copies of several orders from the Richmond office.

Bureau Refugees, Freedmen & A. L.
Hd Quarters Sixth District, Va Col. O. Brown, Asst Comr
Richmond Va

Sept 16th 1865

Staunton Va

Colonel

I have the honor to apply for copies of Genl Order 6 and Spec Order 11.9.2 which have never been furnished me.

I am, Colonel
Very Respectfully
Your Obed Servt

W. Storer How
Capt & A.Q.M.
Supt Sixth Dist Va
