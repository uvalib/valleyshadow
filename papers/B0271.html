Freedmen's Bureau Records: William A. Burnett to John W. Jordan, April 27, 1868

Summary:
Burnett informs Jackson about the status of several court cases.

Clks Office County Court of Augusta Maj Jno. W. Jordan
Asst Comr.

April 27th 1868

Sir

Your communication to hand, Judgements have been rendered in favor of the Commonwealth vs. Henry Fisher and William Ross on this day, the remaining four 4 cases vs. John Craig, Robert, William Craig & Absalom Ross are undetermined and still pending without decision.

Yours &c hastily

William A. Burnett
