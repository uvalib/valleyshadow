Freedmen's Bureau Records: John A. McDonnell to John W. Jordan, May 5, 1868

Summary:
McDonnell forwards a Circular to Jordan from Headquarters.

Bureau R.F. & A.L.
Head Qrs. 9th Sub Dist. Va Col. John W. Jordan
A.S.A.C
Staunton, Va

May 5, 1868

Winchester, Va.

Colonel

Herewith enclosed I transmit one copy of Circular No. 8 C.S. from Head Qrs. Asst. Comr. Va. the receipt of which please acknowledge.

Very Respectfully
Your Obedt St

John A. McDonnell
Sub Asst Comr
