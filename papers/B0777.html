Freedmen's Bureau Records: Thomas P. Jackson to John A. McDonnell, May 11, 1867

Summary:
Jackson forwards a request for financial assistance for teachers and to build a school house.

Bureau R F & A. L.
Sub Dist Augusta & Highland Capt Jno A. McDonnell
Sub Asst Commr

May 11th 1867

Winchester

Captain

I have the honor to transmit herewith communication referring to appropriation asked in aid of School building at Staunton "and" Support of Teachers in the Valley during the coming Summer with request you will forward [unclear: same] to the proper quarter.

Your obt Servt

Thos P. Jackson
Agent
