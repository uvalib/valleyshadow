Freedmen's Bureau Records: Roswell Waldo to Mr. Piper, October 17, 1868

Summary:
Waldo requests payment on behalf of a young worker's mother.

Bureau RF & AL 4th Div 9 Sub Dist Mr. Piper, Esq.
Staunton, Va.

Oct 17 1868

Staunton, Va

Dear Sir:

I have the honor to enclose herewith account of Kitty Cox (colored) for labor of her son George in your Brick Yard at Staunton Va. - Time equivalent to two (2) months and three weeks - at $10 00 per month.

Please examine the account & if correct - remit said amt $27 50 to this office this Evening if possible.

Very respectfully
Your obt servant

Roswell Waldo
ASA Comr
