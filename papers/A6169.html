Augusta County: William [Sobenchaur?] to Edward Flood, February 2, 1865

Summary:
Captain William [Sobenchaur?] writes to Flood about working on a bridge nearby, and asks him to contact Colonel William Procter Smith when in need of supplies.

Staunton, Va.,

Feb. 2, 1865.

Lt. E. Flood,
Com F Pioneer Corps,
Lexington, Va.

Lt.--You will please write immediately on the receipt of this letter, to Col. W m Procter Smith, Chief Engi [unclear: V. D. ], at this place, & inform him what additional tools, or if any, you will need in working on the bridge at Buchanan.

Very resptly &c

W m sobenchaur
Capt Enjrs
