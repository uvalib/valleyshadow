Freedmen's Bureau Records: W. Storer How to Orlando Brown, September 22, 1865

Summary:
How lists which reports he has, and has not, filed with the Bureau office.

U. S. Military Telegraph To Col. O. Brown Asst Comr

Sept 22 1865

By Telegraph from Staunton

I have no report to make for the month of August under the head of officers & enlisted men, Lands, refugees & Freedmen in camps. Schools bureau property Bu. C.C. & [unclear: G.E.] Bu. funds or QM C.C. & G.E. My report of Civilians was sent
also report of rations issued Report of conditions of freedmen & copy of return of QM Stores & QM Cash account. Copies of the abstract & vouchers accompanying the latter will be forwarded immediately.

W. Storer How
Capt AQM Supt 6th Dist
