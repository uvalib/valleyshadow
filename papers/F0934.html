Franklin County: J. S. Myers to Simon Cameron, January 23, 1861

Summary:
J. S. Myers offers support for Cameron's appointment to the cabinet of the incoming administration.

Simon Cameron

January 23. 1861

Franklin

Hon. Simon Cameron
Washington City, D. C.
Dear Sir:--

I regret to know that some of our Republicans in Pennsylvania are opposing your appointment as a member of the Cabinet of the in-coming administration.

Can I do any thing to favor your appointment? If so, it would afford me pleasure to serve you, and if you will indicate the way, it shall be done.

Respectfully &c

J.S. Myers

I hope our friends will not conceive it to be necessary to abandon any of their principles in order to concileate Traitors, but hold a firm hand, and at the same time concileate as much as may be, without a sacrifice of principles or manhood.
