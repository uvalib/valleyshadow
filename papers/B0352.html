Freedmen's Bureau Records: J. J. DeLamater to Thomas P. Jackson, December 21, 1867

Summary:
Jackson receives a letter admitting Susan Berry to Howard's Grove Hospital in Richmond.

Bureau Refugees, Freedmen & A.L. Dist Va
Office Surgeon in Chief Dist A.A. Surg D. R. Brower U.S.A.
In charge of the Howard Grove Hospital, Richmond Va

Dec 12th 1867

Richmond, Va

Will receive in Hospital for treatment, when presented with this order
Susan Berry
an indigent freedwoman now of Staunton Va.

Transportation to Hospital directed by Brig Genl O. Brown Asst Comr Bureau & to be furnished by the civil authorities.

JJ DeLamater
Brvt Lt Col & Surg in Chief
Bureau R.F. & A.L. Dist Va

Thro' Thomas P. Jackson
Agent Bureau
Staunton Va
