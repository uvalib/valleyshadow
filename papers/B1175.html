Freedmen's Bureau Records: W. Storer How to Orlando Brown, April 10, 1866

Summary:
How announces his attention to make yet another tour through the upper portion of his District, asking that all communication be forwarded to him at Staunton.

Bureau of Refugees, Freedmen & A Lands
Sixth District Va Col. O. Brown, Assist Comr
Richmond, Va

April 10th 1866

Winchester Va

Colonel

I have respectfully to state that Bureau Affairs in the upper portion of this District need my personal attention, and that in consequence I start today on a tour through the several Sub Districts, desiring that any communications by mail or telegraph requiring my immediate attention may be addressed to me at Staunton, Va until my return to this pace which will probably be about the 25th day of April.

Hoping this action may meet your approval

I am, Colonel
Very Respectfully
Your Obedient Servant

W. Storer How
Bt Maj AQM & Supt
