Augusta County: William Proctor Smith to Edward Flood, January 28, 1865

Summary:
Smith informs Flood that their horses are nearly dead.

Lieut. E Flood
Comp. Pionier Party

Jany 28 1865

H a Luis Engis V.D.

Lieut.

Enclosed you will find orders for G. W. Myres to Report to Majer Rogers- His services are sadly needed as forage [unclear: Master]- our horses are nearly dead- You will have to detail some one else in his place-

Respectfully

Wm Procter Smith
Col. Engis
