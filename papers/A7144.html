Augusta County: John Scott to W. E. Whiting, February 3, 1869

Summary:
Scott issues a request for textbooks ("Guyot's" geography text, among others).

W E Whiting Staunton Va

Feb 3rd/69

Dear Sir

I sent a request for a few books this morning

The ladies think I have omitted to send for the following which I now ask for--hoping this little addition to the bill may reach you in time to put all in one box.

The books are much needed.

6 Copies Guyots No 2 Geog
6 Copies [unclear: Pinneo's] Primary Grammar

Respectfully

John Scott
