Franklin County: H.C. Fortescue, W.H. Etter, and J.A.S. Cramer to Simon Cameron, January 25, 1861

Summary:
H. C. Fortescue, W. H. Etter, and J. A. S. Cromer ask Cameron for a donation.

Simon Cameron

Jann 25th 1861

Chambersburg

To the Hon. Simon Cameron
Dear Sir--

We the undersigned Citizens of Chambersburg desirous of obtaining some usefull information relitive to our Country and knowing the [unclear: numerous] quantity of "Book", You have in your posision, would be exceeding thankfull to you for a small donation, shuch as you think best suited to our minds. And we will ever pray for your future success, in all your undertakings, and for the satisfactory adjustment of our Nation affairs. And,

We Remain Your
Respectfully

H. C. Fortescue
W. H. Etter
J. A. S. Cromer

Chambersburg Franklin Co Pa
