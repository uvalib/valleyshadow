Augusta: Patrick Brady to Alexander H. H. Stuart, June 24, 1867

Summary:
Patrick Brady writes to A. H. H. Stuart regarding a lawsuit settlement and a missing ledger.

Alex H H Stuart Esq

June 24/67

Philada

Dear Sir

I have Yours of the 21. to hand this morning. I wrote you the same day in reference to [added: the] abduction of my Ledger, to which I refer you.

It is difficult for me to say what Sum I would be willing to accept in Settlement of my claim against Brady, taking all the Circumstances in the Case into account I beg you will try and get the best offer possible when I will advise You at once whether I will accept it or not.



[page 2]
I have no desire to expose him in Case I can get a great settlement from him.

Respectfully Yours,

P Brady
