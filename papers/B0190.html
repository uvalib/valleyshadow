Freedmen's Bureau Records: W. A. Fried to [Frederick S. Tukey], March 16, 1867

Summary:
Fried's letter concerns a case of child abandonment.

Mar 16th 1867.

Waynesboro Augusta Co

This is to sirtify that Sallie Williams has left her child with [illeg.] [illeg.] [added: her [illeg.] mother] without her having agreed to take it and she is not about to take care of it having her own to take care of, the said Sallie Williams has gone to Alexandria to one Mrs [unclear: Hancham]

W. A. Fried
