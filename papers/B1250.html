Freedmen's Bureau Records: S. F. Chalfin to Walter S. Franklin, November 26, 1867

Summary:
Chalfin reports that Jackson's request for a messenger is denied.

Head Quarters First Military District
State of Virginia Bvt Lt Col W. S. Franklin U.S.A.
Commanding Sub District of Winchester
Winchester Va

November 26th 1867

Richmond Va

Sir

Referring to your endorsement of the 5th instant, forwarding letter of Mr. Thomas P. Jackson, Freedmen's Bureau agent, at Staunton Va., who requests the detail of an enlisted man as Messenger in his office. I am directed by the Commanding General to say that the request of Mr. Jackson is not granted. You will please so inform Mr. Jackson.

Very Respectfully
Your Obedient Servant

S. F. Chalfin
Assistant Adjutant General

Official copy furnished for the information of Mr. Jackson thro. Capt. J.A. McDonnell Mil Comr &c Winchester, Va.
[signed]Walter S. Franklin
Capt 21st Inf. Bvt. Lt. Col. USA
Comdg Sub Dist
