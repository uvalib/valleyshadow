Augusta County: Kenton Harper to William S. H. Baylor, May 24, 1861

Summary:
Colonel Kenton Harper writes to Major William S. H. Baylor about the proceedings of the Third Regiment that Col. Harper regards as mutinous.

May 24, 1861

Bolivar

Maj. Baylor
Sir

[unclear: For] I have just seen a copy of the proceedings of certain officers of the Third Regiment; intended to advance your interests which I regard as mutinous [deleted: and] derogatory, and [unclear: ]. I shall take at once such measures as my [unclear: ] and the interests of the [unclear: ] demand.

Yours

Kenton Harper
Col.
