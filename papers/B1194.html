Freedmen's Bureau Records: Will A. Coulter to Roswell Waldo, December 21, 1868

Summary:
Waldo is informed that his term of service will end when the Bureau is discontinued at the end of the month.

Bur. R.F. & A.L.
Hd.Qrs. A.C. Va. Waldo, Roswell
Clerk

Dec. 21, 1868

Richmond, Va.

Sir,

I am directed by the Asst. Comr. to inform you that by direction of the Secy of War, your appointment as an agent of this Bureau has be revoked, to date December 31st 1868 in consequence of the discontinuance of this Bureau at that time.

Yours respty

Will A. Coulter
Bvt. Capt. U.S. Army
A.A.A.Genl.
