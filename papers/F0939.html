Franklin County: W. Maxwell to Simon Cameron, December 16, 1862

Summary:
W. Maxwell offers Cameron his services in promoting his candidacy for senator.

Simon Cameron

Dec 16th 1862

Mercer Pa

Genl S. Cameron
Dear Sir

My son wrote me that he had seen you in Phila - He wished me to have our members vote for you for U. S. Senator.

If you wish for the vote of our Members, I have some influence with them and I think I can induce one at least of them to.

My work of life has been never to forget a friend and always to be at the service of my friends, if you wish my services - please say so & I will go to work -

Yours truly

W. Maxwell
