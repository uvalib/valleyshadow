Freedmen's Bureau Records: Daniel Carter to [Thomas P. Jackson], February 4, 1868

Summary:
Daniel Carter send Jackson a letter and a check for his wife, asking Jackson to pass it on to her.

Mr Thos P Jackson

Feb 4 th 68

Warm Springs

Enclosed you please find letter to my wife in which is $5.00 for her you will please send it to her as soon as you can.

and much obliged

Danl Carter

[Written in Jackson's hand] "Feb 14/68
Received above letter with $5 from Thomas P. Jackson
Witness Sally [added: her mark]X Carter"
