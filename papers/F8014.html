Franklin County: Andrew G. Curtin to Simon Cameron, [August ?] 11, 1860

Summary:
Curtin discusses possible patronage appointments.

Private

[illeg.] 11, [illeg.] 1860

Bellefonte

My der Sir

I write you in response to our friend Taggart and as you [unclear: just to assume from] you did not receive my letter.

I am of the opinion that we cannot get the [unclear: collectorship] for him, and [illeg.] the help to get him some honorable and lucrative place--

As several of your friends named [unclear: Mr Pluncual] for Atty Gen it will no doubt be gratifying to you to hear that I think of appointing him.

I will be pleased to hear from you.

Yrs truly

A. G. Curtin

Hon S. Cameron
