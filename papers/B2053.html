Freedmen's Bureau Records: R. M. Manly to John Scott, May 23, 1869

Summary:
Manly write Scott about rent vouchers, and about raising money to build a house for two teachers in Staunton.

Mr. John Scott,
Staunton, Va

May 23d '9

Sir,

Duplicate vouchers for rent of building at Staunton enclosed for signature.

Where shall the check be sent? It will probably not be received from Washington under two weeks.

If $500 in Cash [added: or its equivalent] is raised, enough money will be furnished by this Bureau to put up a house of two rooms, to accomodate two teachers.

Yours &c

R.M. Manly
Supt of Ed.
