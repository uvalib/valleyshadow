Augusta County: A. H. Byars to his Sister, December 7, 1862

Summary:
Byars writes about picket duty around Richmond and tells about his difficulties obtaining clothing.

Sister

Dec. 7th 1862

Camp near Mount Pleasant
Spotsylvania County, Va
