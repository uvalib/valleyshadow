Augusta County: William Proctor Smith to Edward Flood, February 10, 1865

Summary:
Smith asks Flood to send him cavalry boots.

Feby. 10 1865

(Unofficial) Staunton Va

Dear Lieut.

Many things May be considered more useful than a Shovel & pair of Tongs-but to me they are invaluable-Please accept my thanks for the Same.

I Send with this my measure for the boots-I want Cavalry boots-but I don't wish them to be heavy-have them made medium about half way between heavy & light- I don't wish the tops to come above the knee-Please have them made at once for I need them badly- don't want Scotch Soles- Wm Smith will explain everything to your workman-

Yours

Wm Proctor Smith

P.S. Please make me a foottub--
