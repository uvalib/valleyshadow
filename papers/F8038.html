Franklin County: W. Reilly to Simon Cameron, October 2, 1854

Summary:
Reilly discusses local politics in Franklin County.

Oct. 2d/54

Chambersburg

Hon S. Cameron
My dear Sir

You must excuse me for not answering your letter at an earlier day. You have no doubt seen our nominations. They are good men and if elected I think one of them at least would be your friend and vote for you. Col. Orr is a Mason. Could you do any thing for me by writing to some friend in [unclear: Quimata], as also one or two in Adams. I will be grateful for any assistance you can render me.

Yours &C

W. Reilly
