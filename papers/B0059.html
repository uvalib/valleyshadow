Freedmen's Bureau Records: Thomas P. Jackson to Samuel Kennerly, April 22, 1867

Summary:
Jackson refers a case to Dr. Kennerly for arbitration.

Bureau R.F. & A.L.
Sub Dist. Augusta & Highland Dr. Samuel P. Kennerly
Hermitage, Augusta Co

April 22, 1867

Staunton Va.

Sir

I enclose contract made between Mr. Samuel Parent and William Saulsbury witnessed by Sarah Fauber & Wm. G. Bailey.

A dispute has arisen as to settlement under said contract and both the parties have agreed to refer the matter to you for arbitration.

I enclose with this arbitration bond (hereby drawn) signed by Salsbury. Mr. Parent will sign it with you.

Hoping you will be able to bring about a speedy settlement.

I am
Yr Obt Servt

Thos. P. Jackson
Agent

2 Enclosures A & B
