Augusta County: Margaret Burke to W. E. Whiting, March 6, 1869

Summary:
AMA teacher Margaret Burke writes W. E. Whiting requesting funds.

W. E. Whiting Staunton Va.

Mar. 6th 1869

Dear Sir

Will you [illeg.] please send me a check for thirty dollars $30.00.

Respectfully

Margaret Burke

Staunton Va. box 115.
