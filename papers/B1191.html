Freedmen's Bureau Records: Will A. Coulter to Thomas P. Jackson, March 24, 1868

Summary:
Jackson receives notice of his appointment as Bureau Agent (presumably for his transfer out of Augusta County).

Bur. R.F. and A.L.
HdQrs Asst. Comr, Va Jackson, Thos. P.
Agent, Bur. R.F. and A.L. Va

March 24th 1868

Richmond, Va.

Sir,

I enclose herewith your appointment as Agent of this Bureau. The accompanying oath after being duly executed will be returned to these Head Quarters.

By order of Bvt. Brig. Genl. O. Brown, U.S.V. Asst. Comr.

Will A. Coulter
Bvt. Capt. U.S.A. A.A.A.Genl.
