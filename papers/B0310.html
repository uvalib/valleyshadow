Freedmen's Bureau Records: John A. McDonnell to John W. Jordan, May 4, 1868

Summary:
McDonnell discusses school report forms, and another, unspecified form.

Bureau R.F. & A.L.
Head Qrs. 9t Sub Dist. Va Col. John W. Jordan
A.S.A.Comr
Staunton, Va

May 4.68

Winchester,

Colonel

Herewith I send you Blank School Reports, as requested.

Forms No. 4 are required by Circular 5, Bureau Head Qrs. This Circular has not been furnished me. The forms were originally received from the Asst Comr with Circular Letter from Bu. Hd Qrs. dated Jany 14.68 which contained a memorandum at the foot stating that "Circular with blanks sent to each A.S.A.C." I presume it is in the file of Circulars from Hd. Qrs. Bureau in your office.

Very Respectfully
Your Obdt St

John A. McDonnell
Sub Asst Comr
