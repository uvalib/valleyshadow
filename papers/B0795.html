Freedmen's Bureau Records: Thomas P. Jackson to James Wilson, May 27, 1867

Summary:
Jackson refers a complaint by Alfred Stribling to Wilson for resolution.

Bureau R. F & A L
Off 4 Div. 9 Sub Dist James Wilson Esq J.P.

May 27/67

Churchville

Sir

Alfred Stribling (c) complains that Mr James Cook owes him for 15 days work at 50 c per day $7 50/100 .

Mr Cook refuses to pay [unclear: assyning] very insufficient reasons for his refusal and I respectfully refer the case to you for proper legal remedy before you.

Yrobtservt

Thos P Jackson
Agent
