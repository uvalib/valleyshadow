Augusta County: Frank W. [Lowes?] to Kenton Harper, June 24, 1861

Summary:
Colonel Kenton Harper receives orders from Colonel Jackson to detail a company with officers to transport some rations to headquarters.

24 June 1861,

Hd Qr 1st Brigade
Camp Stevens

Col.

You will detail our company with company officers to take our days rations and report to Lieut. Paxton at Hd Qr at 4 o'clock a.m. for oficial services

By order of Col. Jackson

Frank W. Lowes
aos

Col Harper
