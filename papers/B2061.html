Freedmen's Bureau Records: R. M. Manly to G. T. F. Boulding, January 19, 1870

Summary:
Manly writes that he cannot help Boulding.

Mr. G.T.F. Boulding
Mt. Solon, Augusta Co. Va

Jan. 19th 70

Sir

Your favor of the 15th inst. is received. I am sorry to inform you that it is not in my power to aid you.

Yours respty

R.M. Manly
Supt. of Ed.
