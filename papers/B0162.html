Freedmen's Bureau Records: Harry Keys to W. C. Echols, April 29, 1867

Summary:
Keys communicates how much money they need from the Bureau in order to build a school.

Rev. W. C. Echols
Staunton Va

April 29 1867

Waynesboro

We held a meeting yesterday and concluded that it would take about (300 00/100) three hundred dollars to complete the work.

Very Respectfully

Harry Keys
