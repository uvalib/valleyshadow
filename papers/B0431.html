Freedmen's Bureau Records: James Johnson to Thomas P. Jackson, December 26, 1867

Summary:
Johnson refers to a claim of Ben Martin against the McCutchen estate.

Bureau of Refugees, Freedmen and A. Lands,
Head Qrs Sub Ass't Commissioner, 6th Sub-District, Virginia

Dec 26 1867.

Fredericksburg, Va.

Sir

Ben Martin (col'd) the bearer of this paper is the party who in the behalf of his wife & others has this claim against the Estate of Mr McCutchen, referred to by you in your communication of Dec 17 inst.

Very Respectfully
Your obedient servant

James Johnson
Bvt. A.A.A.Genl. S.A.C.

To Thos P. Jackson
Agt B.R.F.&A.L.
Staunton Va
