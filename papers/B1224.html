Freedmen's Bureau Records: Frederick S. Tukey to [Captain] White, February 1, 1866

Summary:
Tukey acknowledges a transportation order.

Office Bureau Refugees, Freedmen &c Capt. White, Asst. A.A.G.
Bureau R. F. &c
Richmond

Feb 1st 1866

Staunton, Va

Capt.

I have the honor to acknowledge the receipt of order no. 238 for destitute freedpeople, from Staunton to Richmond. I should have done so ere this, but that the matter escaped my attention.

I am Captain,
Your obt. srvt.
for order

F.S. Tukey
Asst Supt. R.F. &c
