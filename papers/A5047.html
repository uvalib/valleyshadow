Augusta County: George W. Baylor to Mary C. Baylor, June 12, 1862

Summary:
George Baylor writes to his sister about clothes and food he wants her to send to him.

June the 12 1862

Camp near Wirs Cove

Dear Sister

It has been so long since I have written to you I dont know when was the last time. I wrote to [unclear: Est] yesterday eavining and I believe I gave her all the news I knowed only I want you to send me a shirt and a pair of cotton pants in the same size off my Sunday pants. I would like to have them as soon as you can send them. I [unclear: ort ] to have a Calico shirt at home I think. I would like to have something good to Eat

[page 2]
if you could send something down. I have goten tired of bread and meat. I want to get home about [illeg.] time if I can.

I have nothing more to write this time.

George W. Baylor

to Mary C. Baylor
