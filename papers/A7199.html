Augusta County: Mary D. Williams to W. E. Whiting, June 18, 1866

Summary:
In her communication forwarding a bill for $30, Williams mentions that the schools in Staunton are due to close on July 1.

Mr. Whiting Staunton, Va.

June 18th 1866

Sir

This bill, I believe, is correctly made out and will inform you that I want $30. The schools, I suppose, close the 1st of July--if not we shall probably be informed of it.

Address

Mary D. Williams
