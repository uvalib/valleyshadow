Freedmen's Bureau Records: John A. McDonnell to Roswell Waldo, December 22, 1868

Summary:
McDonnell instructs Waldo on whom to consult about his duties as Registering Officer, a position he will presumably assume once his commission with the Bureau has ended.

Bureau R.F. & A.L.
Head Qrs 9th Sub Dist. Va. R. Waldo
A.S.A.Comr
Staunton, Va.

Dec 22d/68

Winchester,

Sir

In reply to your letter 21st inst., I take the earliest moment to inform you I have no control whatever over the subject of your duties as Registering Officer.

It will be necessary for you to apply to Gen'l Stoneman or the Superintendent of Registration and Election, for the Divison in which your Counties are situated, for instructions.

If you desire to learn before such instructions arrive, it would be perfectly proper for you to transfer the articles to any of the County Regr officers, notifying the proper HdQrs of your action.

Very Respectfully,
Your Obedt St

John A. McDonnell
Sub Asst Comr
