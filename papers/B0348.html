Freedmen's Bureau Records: Walter S. Franklin to John A. McDonnell, June 17, 1867

Summary:
Franklin forwards a letter about the Methodist Episcopal Church in Staunton.

Head Quarters Sub-District of Winchester

June 17th 1867

Winchester

Sir

I have the honor to enclose herewith special orders in reference to the Methodist Episcopal Church at Staunton Va which you will please forward to the proper address.

I am Sir
Very Respectfully
Your obdt Servant

Walter S. Franklin
Captain 21st Infantry Bvt Lt Col USA
Commanding Sub-District

Captain John A. McDonnell
V.R.C. Mil Com
Winchester Va
