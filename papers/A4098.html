Augusta County: Jedediah Hotchkiss to Sara A. Hotchkiss, October 8, 1864

Summary:
Hotchkiss writes a brief note to his wife, telling her the location of the army.

Sara A. Hotchkiss

Oct 8th 1864

New Market Va --

My Dear Sara

We got here yesterday & are resting today -- the enemy has gone on down the Valley they did a great deal of burning -- I am very tirde -- it is really cold this morning -- Keep my coat until I send for it -- The news from Missouri & Georgia is very cheering -- I send you a piece of a Broome Co. paper Send it to Nelson -- the Courier is going & I can only say blessings on you all & love for you -- Write me often -- I suppose we shall go on --

Your Aff husband

Jed. Hotchkiss
