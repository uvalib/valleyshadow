Freedmen's Bureau Records: Frederick S. Tukey to John A. McDonnell, January 25, 1867

Summary:
Tukey reports no criminal trials for the month of January 1867.

Bureau R. F. & A Lands
Sub Dist. Augusta & Highland C. Capt. John A. McDonnell
Supt. 9th Dist. Dept Potomac
Winchester Va

Jany 25th. 1867

Staunton Va.

Capt

I have the honor to report in compliance with Circular No. 10 that no criminal trials have been brought to my notice in this Sub. Dist. for the month of Jany 1867.

I am Capt with much respect
Your Obt. Servt.

Frederick S. Tukey
Asst. Superintendent
B.R.F. & A. Lands
