Franklin County: J. R. Brenden to Simon Cameron, November 12, 1860

Summary:
Brenden writes in support of appointing Dr. Robert K. Sants to the office of "Marshall of this District."

Dr Sir,

Dr Robert K [unclear: Sanits] will apply for the appoint of Marshall of this District.

I know him to be one of the most efficient partymen in our County, he is a man of character and education has a great popularity with the Lincoln party which he fully deserves, and there is no [unclear: flinch] about him, he will stand by a friend in hell or heaven,

In haste
Yours

JR Brenden
12 Nov 60

Hon. Simon Cameron
