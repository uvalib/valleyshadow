Franklin County: Alexander K. McClure to Andrew G. Curtin, June 9, 186x

Summary:
McClure informs Governor Curtin that he has written an editorial about the success of the Governor's administration during the war.

June 9

Chambersburg

Dear Gov

You will find an Editorial article in this weeks Repository written by me, on Pennsylvania & the War, which should be copied into the Telegraph. You can have some one suggest it to [unclear: Bergner] or [unclear: Tomey]. It is a brief review of the success of your administration in organizing & caring for our soldiers.

I will go to Phila Wednesday. I hope [unclear: Magran] will be there.

Yours

AK McClure

Gov A G Curtin
