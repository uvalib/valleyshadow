Freedmen's Bureau Records: Frederick S. Tukey to Orlando Brown, March 11, 1867

Summary:
Tukey corrects a report he sent Brown earlier in the day, replacing the name of Edward M. Cushion with that of Harvey Risk, commenting on his political views.

Bureau R. F. & A. Lands
Sub. Dist. Augusta & Highland Bvt. Brig. Genl O. Brown
A.A.A.G. B.R.F. & A.L.
Richmond, Va.

March 11th 1867

Staunton, Va.

Genl

Please substitute the name of Harvey Risk in place of Edward M. Cushion in the Report which I sent you this morning. Mr. Risk is a Democrat but moderate and conservative and answers all the points.

Very Respectfully
Your Obt. Servt.

Fred'k S. Tukey
Asst. Supt.
