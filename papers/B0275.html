Freedmen's Bureau Records: Crawford & Bro. to Thomas P. Jackson, April 29, 1867

Summary:
The firm of Crawford and Brother agree to settle a claim filed through the Bureau.

Mr. Thos P. Jackson

Aprile 29th /67

Mount Alta Va

Sir

Yours of the 20th inst. was received today & we hast to reply - that we will be pleased to settle the claim of George & Abraham Simms by the 10th of May, or sooner if we can possibly do so. We assure you that the claim will be met promptly by the 10th of May.

Your Obt Servts

Crawford & Bro.

Address Mt. Alta P.O. Augusta Co Va
