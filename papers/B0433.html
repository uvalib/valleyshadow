Freedmen's Bureau Records: John A. McDonnell to Thomas P. Jackson, February 3, 1868

Summary:
McDonnell asks about the status of Jackson's request for transportation for Betsey Brown and her children from Staunton to Winchester.

Bureau R. F. & A. L.
HdQrs 9th Sub Dist. Va. Mr. Thos P. Jackson
A.S.A.C.
Staunton, Va.

Feby 3d/68

Winchester, Va.

Sir

You will please report without delay, the action taken by the Civil Authorities, on your application to them for transportation for Betsey Brown & children to this place.

If they refuse, you will also please state the lowest price for which they can be sent here.

Very Respectfully
Your Obedt St

John A. McDonnell
Sub Asst Comr
