Franklin County: Lizzie Taggart to Captain John Taggart, July 11, 186[3?]

Summary:
Lizzie provides her brother with a brief update of recent family news.

Captain John Taggart, 9th Penn. Reserve Infantry

July 11

Dear John

We received your note Willie is gone in Vincents Co. Sam is elected captain. McNary is Lieut. Colo. We will take it to McCallum. Moll has a very sore eye I think it is [unclear: erysipelus ] it is very much swollen below and around the eye. We had letters from Till & All & [illeg.]. Iris received the paper you sent, nothing new.

Give my love to all

Your sister

Lizzie
