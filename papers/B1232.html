Freedmen's Bureau Records: John W. Jordan to Orlando Brown, February 9, 1869

Summary:
Jordan asks for a copy of the order relieving him of duty as Bureau Agent in Staunton.

Bvt Brig Gen'l O. Brown
Richmond, Va

Feby 9th 1869

Lexington, Va.

General

I have the honor to request to be furnished with certified copies of the following named "orders"

Orders assigning me to duty in Va.
Orders relieving me at Farmville
Orders relieving me at Staunton.

Your early compliance with above request will greatly oblige

Your obt svt &c

Jno. W. Jordan
late Bureau Comr.
