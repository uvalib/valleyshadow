Freedmen's Bureau Records: Roswell Waldo to Mr. Bell, October 21, 1868

Summary:
Waldo passes along orders to Bell, a constable, informing him that the previous orders relating to a case have been revoked.

Bureau RF&AL 4th Div 9 Sub Dist Mr. Bell, Constable
Long Glade Augusta Co Va

Oct. 21, 1868

Staunton, Va

Sir:

By direction of Lieut F. E. [unclear: Tom] Mil Comr 17 Div. I have the honor to enclose herewith orders from that officer revoking previous orders relating to matters pending between Mrs. Elizabeth Rankin & RB Prager &c.

Very respectfully

Roswell Waldo
ASA Comr
