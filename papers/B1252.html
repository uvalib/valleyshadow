Freedmen's Bureau Records: Henry M. Whittelsey to Orlando Brown, May 8, 1867

Summary:
Whittelsey issues a transportation order of Nicholas Johnson, whose transport had been requested by Thomas P. Jackson, agent in Staunton.

[Form 6.]
War Department,
Bureau Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands,
Office Chief Quartermaster, Bvt. Brig. Gen'l O. Brown,
Assistant Commissioner, &c
Richmond, Va.

May 8th 1867

Washington,

General

I respectfully forward herewith, Transportation Order, No. 353, for transportation of Nicholas Johnson, Freedman, the same having been requested by letter of Thos. P. Jackson, forwarded to this office, approved by your endorsement of date May 6th 1867.

By order of Maj.-Gen. O. O. Howard, Commissioner.

Very respectfully, your obedient servant,

H. M. Whittelsey
Bvt. Brig.-Gen., Chief Q. M., Bureau R., F., & A.L.
