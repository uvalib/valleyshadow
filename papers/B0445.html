Freedmen's Bureau Records: John A. McDonnell to Roswell Waldo, September 14, 1868

Summary:
McDonnell forwards receipts for clothing issued.

Bureau R.F. & A.L.
Headqrs 9th Sub Dist. Va Roswell Waldo
A.S.A.Comr
Staunton, Va

Sept. 14 1868

Winchester

Sir

I have the honor to return herewith Receipt Rolls for clothing issued by you. You will please enter it upon Col. Jordan's Rolls enclosed to you with my Letter of 11th inst.

A supply of Clothing Rolls is this day sent you.

The headings and signature to certificate will be left blank.

The signatures of the recipients will be witnessed by you.

Very Respectfully
Your Obedt St

John A. McDonnell
Sub Asst Comr
