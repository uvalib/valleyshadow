Freedmen's Bureau Records: John A. McDonnell to Roswell Waldo, September 8, 1868

Summary:
McDonnell instructs Waldo to submit his Clothing Receipt Rolls by the 15th of the month.

Bureau R. F. & A. L.
Head Qrs. 9t Sub Dist. Va R. Waldo
A.S.A.C.
Staunton, Va

Sept. 8 /68

Winchester

Sir

Maj. White requested me to forward my Clothing & Property [unclear: Asss ] to him this month in time to reach Richmond by the 20t; you will therefore have sumit your Clothing Receipt Rolls by the 15th inst.

Very Respectfully
Your Obt Svt

John A McDonnell
Sub Asst Comr
