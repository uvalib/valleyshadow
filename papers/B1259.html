Freedmen's Bureau Records: J. J. DeLamater to Orlando Brown, June 20, 1868

Summary:
DeLamater forwards the transportation order for Edward Brooks, from Howard's Grove Hospital, in Richmond, to Staunton.

Bureau R. F. & A. L. Dist. Va.
Office Surg. in Chief [illeg.]

June 20th 1868

Richmond, Va.

Received from Bureau Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands, Richmond, Va., Transportation Orders No. 26 for Edward Brooks, Sr. requested by A. A. Surg. D. R. Brower, May 27th, 1868.

JJ DeLamater
Surg in Chief, Bureau R.F. & A.L. Dist. Va.

To Brevet Brigadier General O. Brown,
Assistant Commissioner
