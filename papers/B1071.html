Freedmen's Bureau Records: Thomas P. Jackson to James E. Carson, July 5, 1867

Summary:
Jackson informs Carson that a Joseph Harrison claims Carson owes him $41 plus interest.

Bureau R.F. & A.L.
Office 4th Div. 9 Sub DistVa Mr James E. Carson
Middlebrook

July 5/67

Staunton Va

Sir

A claim against you on behalf of Joseph Harrison (c) for $41 00/100 with Interest from June 1st 1866 is lodged in this office and I call your attention to the necessity of an early settlement. You will make some arrangement or explanation to me by the 13t Instant.

Yr. obt. servt

Thos P. Jackson
Agent
