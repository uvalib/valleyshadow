Freedmen's Bureau Records: J. T. H. Hall to Thomas P. Jackson, December 20, 1867

Summary:
Hall asks Jackson to transmit the order regarding the case between Crebs and Pitman to the Augusta County Clerk's Office.

Capt. Jackson

Dec 20th 1867

Woodstock, Va

Dear Sir

Will you please deliver the enclosed orders to Co. Clerk, Isaac Crebs v. Mrs. M. Pitman and you will oblige.

Yours &c

J.T.H. Hall
Bt Capt Vols &c &c
