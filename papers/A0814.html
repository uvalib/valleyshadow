Augusta County: M. G. Harman to Albert Garber, June 20, 1863

Summary:
Harman writes to his Uncle Albert to tell him about rumors of Thomas Garber's death.

June 20, 1863

Staunton

Dear Uncle,

I have just Heard Maj T.P. Eskridge told Mr. B. Evans that Tom was killed & his body left on the Field. I have been down to see Col Davidson & Bill but can hear nothing. I Hope it is untrue. Come in before you say anything to Aunt or the Family about it.

Truly your nephew

MG Harman
