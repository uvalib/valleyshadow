Franklin County: David Demus to Mary Jane Demus, January 24, 1865

Summary:
Demus writes that if it wasn't for the black troops fighting for the Union, the war might last another ten years. He also says he feels lucky to have remained safe in the war.

[Image of a United States flag in front of a rising sun.]

Mary Jane Demus

Jenawary the 24th/65

Morris iland
south C
