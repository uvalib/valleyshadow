Freedmen's Bureau Records: John A. McDonnell to John W. Jordan, April 21, 1868

Summary:
McDonnell acknowledges Jordan's arrival in Staunton, and informs him that there are no special instructions beyond what he may find in the office at Staunton.

Bureau R.F. & A.L.
Head Qrs. Sub Dist.Va. Col. John W. Jordan
A.S.A.Comr
Staunton, Va

April 21, 1868

Winchester,

Colonel

Your communication of 18th inst. reporting your arrival at Staunton and asking instructions has been received.

No instructions beyond those contained in the files of Circulars and orders in your office are deemed necessary.

Very Respectfully
Your Obedt Servant

John A. McDonnell
Sub Asst Comr

Any blanks, stationery &c required, can be obtained on application to this office
