Freedmen's Bureau Records: Samuel Hunt to Orlando Brown, November 8, 1865

Summary:
Hunt introduces two teachers who are en route to take up posts in Staunton.

American Missionary Association,
No. 61 John Street, Col. O. Brown
Asst. Comr. of Bureau Freedmen

Nov. 8 1865

New York,

Dear Sir

This will introduce you to Misses Williams and Dewey, whom we have appointed for Staunton. If they need any assistance will you have the kindness to extend to them the necessary courtesies they may require, and much oblige,

Very Respectfully yours

Sam'l Hunt
Supt of Ed
