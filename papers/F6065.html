Franklin County: Jacob Middows to George W. Brewer, August 18, 1864

Summary:
Jacob Middows sends his pay to his friend George Brewer to pass along to his mother through Joseph Rock. He also includes a sum from George Gates to his father.

August 18 1864

Camp Near Petersburg

Dear Friend George W. Brewer

ile state to you that I Exspressed $110.00 Dollars to you And you Pleas And Let Joseph Rock of Quincey Township Franklin Countey Pennsylvania Lift it And give it to My Mother E Middows And George Gates sent 50 00 Do to [unclear: hyou ] and his father of George Gates of Quincy Township Franklin Countey Pennsylvania is to lift his so if tha tha coms for it youl give it to them that is if I shold of gots shold happns to Lose our Resiets that tha

[page 2]
Can Draw it but if I dont lose the Resiet ile send it to then then tha can draw it

Yours
Truley

Jacob Middows
&
George Gates.

And by So doing youl Oblige your Friends G Gates and Jacob Middows to George W Brewer
Chambersburg
Franklin
County
Pensylvania
