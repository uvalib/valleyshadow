Freedmen's Bureau Records: John A. McDonnell to Thomas P. Jackson, August 17, 1867

Summary:
McDonnell's letter details the supplies he is sending to Jackson, asking him to use them with all possible economy.

Bureau of Refugees, Freedmen, and Abandoned Lands
Head Quarters 9th District Department of the Potomac Mr. Thos P. Jackson
Agent
Staunton, Va

Aug 17th, 1867

Winchester, Va.

Sir
