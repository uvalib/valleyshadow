Freedmen's Bureau Records: Frederick S. Tukey to Garrick Mallery, April 1, 1867

Summary:
Tukey reports no criminal trials involving freedmen during the month of March.

Bureau R. F. & A Lands
Sub Dist. Augusta & Highland Col. G. Malory
A.A.A.G. B.R.F. & A.L.
Richmond Va.
Through Dist. Hd. Qrs.

April 1st 1867

Staunton Va.

Col.

I have the honor to report in compliance with Circular No. 10 C.S. 1866 that no Criminal Trials were brought to my knowledge in this Sub-Dist during the month of March, 1867.

I am Col. with much Respect
Your Obt Servt.

Frederick S. Tukey
Asst. Supt.
