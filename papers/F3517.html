Franklin County: Frank Grube to General Purviance, February 14, 1863

Summary:
Frank Grube, an assistant surgeon, requests leave prior to a temporary transfer from the 126th Pennsylvania to the 134th Pennsylvania.

Med. Dept. 134th Regt. Pa. Gen. Purviance
Adj. 134th Pa.

February 14, 1863

Sir

Having just received the enclosed order from the Surgeon Genls. office--which which with the accompanying endorsements fix the date of my examination there on the twenty third (23rd instant. I respectfully request leave of absence for fifteen days (15) beginning February twenty first (21)--to comply with its terms and make some private arrangements preparatory to my transfer.

Respectfully Your Obt. Servt.

frank grube, asst. Surg., 126 Reg. Pa.
temporarily detailed to 134th Pa.
and Citizen of the State of Kansas
