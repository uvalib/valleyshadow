Franklin County: David Demus to Mary Jane Demus, November 28, 1864

Summary:
Demus writes that he hopes to be home soon and will bring money with him.

Mary Jane Demus

november the 28th

morris Island Soth Car
54th Mastues redgn

I Pleasent time to set dow to inform you that i am Well at Present and hope Whean the fu lynes reach you tha ma finde you in the same state of healt i Wod be hapey to hear frum you and you and you nead not be [unclear: saveing] of yor money for i Will be hom in a sorte time and i donte Wonte you to get a [unclear: sole] if you Can dow withe oute for i Will fetch you [unclear: Wasn] home Whean i [unclear: bum] and if you [unclear: bont] [unclear: dow] With [unclear: oru] Wiy you ma get Wan bute but dont pay too mach for it fer i [unclear: ges] [unclear: thar] ar hi [unclear: thar] and if you Cant get Wone Wiy let me now nothing mor at present but still reman

yor Dear husben

David Demes to Marrey Jane Demus
