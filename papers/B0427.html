Freedmen's Bureau Records: Thomas P. Jackson to Nicholas K. Trout, February 3, 1868

Summary:
Jackson asks Trout to provide transportation for Betsey Brown and her children to Winchester.

Bureau of Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands
Office 4th Division, 9th Sub-District, Virginia. N K Trout Esq
Mayor &c
Staunton

Feb 3d 1868

Staunton, Va.

Sir

I respectfully refer enclosed papers to you inviting attention to endorsement of Brig Genl O. Brown Asst Comr. of Jan 29/68 and requesting that if possible early provision be made to forward Betsey Brown and her three (3) children to Winchester. These papers to be returned.

Yr obt servt

Thos P. Jackson
Asst Sub Asst Comr
