Freedmen's Bureau Records: W. Storer How to Orlando Brown, May 21, 1866

Summary:
This letter refers to the proper forwarding of reports.

Bu. R.F. & A.L.
HdQrs Sixth Dist. Va General O. Brown
Asst. Comr.
Richmond, Va

May 21, 1866

Winchester

General

Your telegram of this date has been received. To ensure compliance, Lieut Cook, Asst Supt Sub. Dist. B at Staunton, Bt Maj Carse Asst. Supt. at Lexington and Capt Wolff, Asst. Supt. of Sub Dist "A" at Covington, have been directed to forward their reports under the Circular direct.

Very Respectfully
Your Obedt Srvt

W. Storer How
Bt. Maj. & A.Q.M.
Superintendent
