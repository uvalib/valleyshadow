Freedmen's Bureau Records: W. Storer How to Frederick S. Tukey, October 4, 1865

Summary:
How authorizes Tukey to use a room in the Court house in Staunton as a school.

Bu. R.F. & A.L.
Sixth Dist. Va. Mr. F. S. Tukey
Asst. Supt.
Staunton, Va

Oct 4t 1865

Staunton, Va

Sir

By permission from Lt. Col. C. Clay 58th Pa Vols comd'g sub. dist. of Staunton you are at liberty to use the upper story of the Court House recently occupied by me as an office, for a school room, for the children of freedmen in your sub-District 'B'.

Proper care will be taken of the Rooms in order that they may be given up when legitimately claimed, in as good condition as they now are.

I am Sir
Very Respy

W. Storer How
Capt &c
