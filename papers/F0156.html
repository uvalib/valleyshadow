Franklin County: Sam Pile to George Miller, January 10, 1863

Summary:
Sam Pile writes to George Miller about his life in the military and how he would rather be at home. He feels that the North is fighting for "the wrong cause."

George M. Miller

Jan'y 10th 1863

Winchester, Va

George M. Miller

Friend George I suppose you will be surprised when you find this letter is from your old friend Sam Pile, well its a fact. I am still alive and well, I suppose you wonder how in the devil I come to be all the way here in Virginia. I can tell you that best another time.

I hear that you are still at your old business, making legars, setting rabbit looops and fishing, take my advice and stick too it, for it is a darned sight better than soldering, that is in the wrong cause.

Well George I've been knocking about this Southern Confederacy considerably since I wrote to you last.

[page 2]
and a devil of a time I've had I dont know where I will strike to next, the next time you hear from me I will tell you something it would not pay to tell it at the present time. George I hear that Al Weiser is dead, poor Natty we have had many a gay time together The 87 Rgt P.V. is now at this place. I was somewhat surprised when I found it out, I was mighty glad to see many of my old friends, and to hear from old York for it has been a long while since I heard from there last

I want you to write me a long, long, letter, as soon as you get this and tell me what you have been doing for the last two years. direct your letter to Miss Maggie Burnett Winchester Va. now I know

[page 3]
you will wonder what that means, it means I will be more likely to get it if addressed to a lady, dont fail to write. Whisky is a scarce article in this part of the world, only eight dollars a quart for stuff that would burn the hair off a dog, when you write pour some on the paper then mabe I will get a smell. If you see any of my folks tell them you heard from me. If you can send me some York papers, to the same address.

Give my respects to all enquiring friends, be a good democrat and hate abolitionists and you will stand a good chance in the next world.

Yours truly

Sam Pile
