Freedmen's Bureau Records: Thomas P. Jackson to Garrick Mallery, May 1, 1867

Summary:
Jackson reports no criminal trials involving freedmen for the month of April.

Bureau R. F. & A. L.
Sub. Dist. Augusta & Highland Capt. Garrick Mallery 43rd U.S.I.
A. Asst. Adj.Genl
Richmond

May 1st 1867

Staunton Va.

Captain

In compliance with requirements of Cir. 10 S. 1866 B. R. F. & A. L. I have the honor to report that no Criminal Trials involving the rights of Refugees or Freedmen have occurred since my assignment to this District April 3d /67

I learn that in Highland county, since Refugees have been hardly dealt with at courts held previously, but I have not been able to procure full details on which to [unclear: provide] a report.

Yr. obt. servt

Thos P. Jackson
Agent

Through Capt. J. A. McDonnell
Sub. Asst. Comr 9th District
