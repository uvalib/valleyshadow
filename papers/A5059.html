Augusta County: W. F. Thomas to Charles W. McGuffin, April 10, 1865

Summary:
The day after Lee's surrender at Appomatox, Charles McGuffin receives an order from a captain to bring his wagon to Whitesville.

April 10th 1865

Mr C W McGuffin

You will proceed at once to Whitesville, and bring up my wagon if there if not hunt it up and bring to Danville.

W. F. Thomas
Capt & A. Q. M.
