Augusta: John N. Hite to J. O. Miller, September 8, 1864

Summary:
Hite acknowledges his receipt of the letter and draft from Miller and says that he wishes to get home soon.

M r J. O. Miller

Sept 8th 1864

Elmira New York

Dear sir

I will inform you that I am well and getting along very well now I am glad to acknoledge [added: the] reception of your letter with the draft it has releived my very much I am very thankfull to you for it,

I have nothing of intrest to write to you at this time & hove ritten a letter home but hove not received an answer yet I hope we will all get home this winter I will show my letter to a close I hope to hear from you often write soon and direct as before

Yours truly

J. N. Hite
