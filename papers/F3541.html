Franklin County: Thom Vincent to Captain Henry Clay Wood, August 27, 1862

Summary:
Assistant Adjutant General Thom Vincent directs Captain Henry Clay Wood concerning the promotion of James C. Austin from captain to major.

Captain Henry Clay Wood

August 27, 1862

Washington

Captain

I am instructed to direct you to muster out of service James C. Austin as captain Co. "B," 126th regt. Penna Vol. to date from August 12th 1862 and muster him into service as Major from Same Regiment. Enrollment and Muster to date from Aug. 13th, 1862 at Washington City D.C.

I am Captain
Very Respectfully
Your Obt. Servant

Thom Vincent
Asst. Adjt. General
