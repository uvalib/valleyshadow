Freedmen's Bureau Records: Annie E. Branham to Thomas P. Jackson, July 16, 1867

Summary:
Branham writes that she will agree to pay Joshua Harris if Jackson drops the suit against her.

Mr. Jackson

July 16th 1867

Staunton

Dear Sir,

If you will withdraw the suit of Joshua Harris against me, I will settle upon the basis of twenty four dollars & ninety five cents.

Annie E. Branham
