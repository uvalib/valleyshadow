Freedmen's Bureau Records: W. Storer How to J. J. DeLamater, August 31, 1865

Summary:
How reports that he is unable to provide DeLamater with medical information about his district because he does not have enough personnel to gather it.

Bureau Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands
HdQrs Sixth Dist. Va Lieut. J. J. DeLamater, U.S.A.
Chief Med. Officer
Richmond, Va.

Aug. 31st 1865

Staunton

Doctor

I reply to your interrogatories of Augt 22d 1865, I have no report to make under the headings indicated and can make no estimate of future requirements for the lack of Asst. Superintendents to supply the necessary data. At the earliest possible period I will obtain the information desired, and make a statement of probable and actual wants.

I am, Doctor
Very Respectfully &c

W. Storer How
Capt & AQM
Supt. 6th Dist Va
