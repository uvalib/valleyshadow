Augusta County: John Quincy Adams Nadenbousch to Hester J. Nadenbousch, June 29, 1864

Summary:
Nadenbousch writes a quick note to his wife announcing his plans to come home in a few days. He instructs her to show hospitality to his business partner who will deliver the note.

June 29/64

Staunton Va

Dr Hester

This will be handed you by Dr McChesney my partner in the Hotel here He is a very nice gentleman You extend to him the hospitalities of Your house if he gets there before me

I am trying hard to make some arrangement to get off from the business here, but I will be down in a few days. I will start so soon as I Can the way is clear

Yours ever

J Q A Nadenbousch
