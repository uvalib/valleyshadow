Freedmen's Bureau Records: Thomas P. Jackson to John A. McDonnell, April 24, 1867

Summary:
Jackson forwards a complaint regarding one party's failure to pay money owed. As the case crossed division lines, the complaint was forwarded to R.S. Lacey, the Sub Assistant Commissioner of the seventh Sub-District, and it was subsequently passed on to J.W. Sharp, the Agent in charge of Rockbridge County.

Bureau R. F. & A. L.
Sub Dist. Augusta & Highland Capt. John A. McDonnell
Sub Asst Comr
Winchester

Ap 24/1867

Staunton Va

Captain

I respectfully forward claim of Reily McDonald, Staunton against C.W. Oltmann, late of Staunton but now said to be at Lexington (at Washington College in employ of one Col. Allen. Oltmann gave McDonald an order on Mr. Jed. Hotchkiss of Staunton but he refuses to pay for the reason he has no funds of Oltmann in hand and so states in writing.

Yr obt servt

Thos P. Jackson
Agent
