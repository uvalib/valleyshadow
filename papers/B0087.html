Freedman's Bureau Records: Jacob F. Chur to Bvt. Brig. Genl. O. Brown, July 27, 1867

Summary:

Bureau Refugees F + A. L.
Hd Qs. Asst. Com H.Q. Bvt. Brig. Genl. O. Brown

July 27, 1867

Raleigh

Respectfully returned to Bvt. Brig. Genl O. Brown Asst commissioner, Virginia (thru Hd Qs. Commissioner.) Attention invited to endoresment of Bvt. Maj. A. Coats of 23rd inst.

Jacob F. Chur, Bvt. Lieut. Col. A.A.A.G.

(On the absence of the Asst Coms)
