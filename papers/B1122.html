Freedmen's Bureau Records: W. Storer How to Orlando Brown, September 29, 1865

Summary:
How's letter to Colonel Brown asks a procedural question.

Bureau Refugees, Freedmen & A. Lands
Hd Qrs Sixth District Va Col. O. Brown, Assist Commissioner
Richmond Va

Sept 29th 1865

Staunton Va

Colonel

I have the honor to request information as to whether fines imposed by a Provost Marshall in cases relating to Freedmen should be turned over to an officer of this Bureau.

I am Colonel
Very Respectfully
Your Obedient Servant

W. Storer How, Capt
Supt Sixth Dist., Va
