Freedmen's Bureau Records: W. Storer How to Orlando Brown, November 21, 1865

Summary:
How informs Brown that he will be leaving for Staunton to conduct a tour of part of the District.

Bureau of Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands,
Head Quarters, Sixth District, Virginia, Col. O. Brown, Assist Commr
Richmond Va

Nov 21st 1865

Winchester, Va.,

Colonel

I have respectfully to inform you that I find it necessary to [unclear: proceed] on a tour of duty through the upper portion of this District, and shall leave here tomorrow for Staunton, at which place I expect to arrive on the evening of Thursday, 23d, and it is my present intention to return from thence on the following Wednesday 29th.

Should anything require my immediate attention in the interim, be pleased to notify me by letter or telegraph at Staunton, sending nothing by mail to Staunton after the 27th of this month.

I am Colonel
Very Respectfully
Your Obedient Servant

W. Storer How, Capt AQM
Supt Sixth Dist. Va
