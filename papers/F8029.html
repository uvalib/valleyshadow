Franklin County: D. O. Gehr to Simon Cameron, November 10, 1860

Summary:
Gehr asks Cameron to help secure a clerkship for Gehr's son.

D. O. Gehr's
Son
Promised to
help him to
a Clerkship
Nov. 1860

Nov 10/60

Chambersburg

Hon Simon Cameron.

I introduce to your acquaintance My Son Hastings Gehr, he is desirous of getting a Clerkship this winter at Harrisburg during the Session of the Legislature as transcribing clerk or some post under Forney at Washington City during the Session of Congress, I put him in a law office a few years since and he was admitted to the bar last April, but is not inclined to commence the profession for a few years, would prefer some other calling for the present, any appointance you may render him will be gratefully remembered

Your Friend

D. O. Gehr
