Freedmen's Bureau Records: John Crawford and Archy Brock to Thomas P. Jackson, March 2, 1868

Summary:
Crawford and Brock announce that their committee will be ready to begin building a church as soon as they have lumber, but that they need Jackson to submit the order.

Mr Jackson

March 2d 68

Waynesboro

We the committe for building the Church in this place are ready to commence work.

Mr. Watts says he is "ready to deliver the lumber and wants an order from you." Will you please give us one for the bill of lumber.

We don't know any thing about what Harry Keys has done or what arrangements he has made with you. Please let us know how it stands now, so we will know what to do. All we lack is the lumber. Will be much obliged to you for an order for it.

Very respectfully

John Crawford
Archy Brock
Commt
