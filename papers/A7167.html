Augusta County: John Scott to Samuel Hunt, November 11, 1865

Summary:
Scott announces his arrival in Virginia, stating that he will keep Hunt "informed in regard to our movements."

Rev. S. Hunt Staunton Va Staunton

Nov 11 1865

We Arrived safely, at about dark. Stayed at Richmond from 10 1/2 on Friday until this morning.

Proceeded first after landing there to the Depot and found that we could not proceed until this morning

Called on Col Brown who after some delay refered me to Chapl'n Manley and after further delay he was found, but as his house was already full of Teachers, and as the A. M. A. had no teachers staying in Richmond he could only refer us to the Monumental Hotel as a good place to stay.

And we are now in Staunton

I will keep you informed in regard to our movements.

Respectfully

John Scott
