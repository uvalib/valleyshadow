Franklin County: Sanford Schroeder to William W. Walker and George Walker, [December 1862]

Summary:
Sanford Schroeder sends notice to William W. Walker and George Walker of their brother's illness and his wish to see them.

Received Dec. 19 1862
From Waynesboro Va. Capt. Wm. W. Walker
or Lt. Geo Walker
Co. E. 12th Regt. Pa.
Humphreys Div.

Your brother is very ill. Would like to see you.

Sanford Schroeder
