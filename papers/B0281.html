Freedmen's Bureau Records: H. A. Goodloe to [Thomas P. Jackson], November 2, 1867

Summary:
Goodloe forwards information about a Lewis Lurner on to Jackson, as a claim was filed against Turner at the Bureau.

Adams Express Company,
Great Eastern, Western & Southern
Express Forwarders.

Nov. 2d 1867

Goshen [Bridge]

Dear Sir

I recd a letter from you a few days ago wishing to know if I knew Lewis Lurner and if he had any Property. I know him he is living with me and Draws his pay as fast as he earns it to pay up last years recpts he has nothing that I know of. I told him you had a Claim in your hand against him he said there was some owing of him and he would have to push them to pay you.

Yours Respectfully

H. A. Goodloe
