Augusta County: John Scott to E. P. Smith, April 27, 1869

Summary:
Letter confirming receipt of vouchers

W. E. Whiting Esq Staunton Va

Apr 27th 1869

Dear Sir

I have just read from the disbursing officer--(Maj Geo Q White B R F H L) Richmond Va.--vouchers to be signed preparatory to the pay't of the $17.00 rent for month of Apr

If the money is rec'd it will appear on the credit side of my monthly acct with you, at the close of the present month,--for which acct please send blanks by return mail.

John Scott
