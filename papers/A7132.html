Augusta County: Letter from John Scott, December 25, 1868

Summary:
Scott writes to confirm receipt of school materials (charts and maps).

Staunton Va

Dec 25th/1868

The Boxes numbered [deleted: 28 came] 2804 + 2805 came and were found to contain all represented in the accompanying Invoice.

But the Eureka Chart--are marked $15.00 which must be a mistake. The charts are not mounted or varnished nor on pasteboard but only on thin sheets of paper, and there are but two numbers 1 and 2.--and perhaps--a doz. of each no. (which was more than was needed all of the same kind)--Now the maps, an excellent set are only marked $16.00 all mounted and with cloth back etc.

These charts should not cost over $4. by any means and probably not that. You may wish to correct that.

Please inform Mr Smith that as the month is nearly up, I shall need Blanks for Monthly Reports very soon.

Respectfully

John Scott
