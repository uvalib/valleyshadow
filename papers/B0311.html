Freedmen's Bureau Records: John A. McDonnell to Thomas P. Jackson, March 3, 1868

Summary:
McDonnell asks Jackson to report when he will be in Monterey, the seat of Highland County, and if its County Court meets at the same time as Augusta's.

Bureau R.F. & A.L.
Head Qrs. 9th Sub Dist. Va. Mr. Thos. P. Jackson
A.S.A.C.
Staunton, Va.

Mch 3d 1868

Winchester,

Sir

In compliance with the requirements of Cir. No. 1, C.S. HdQrs. Asst. Comr. Va, one copy of which is herewith transmitted, and the receipt of which you will please acknowledge, you will at once designate the days at which you can be found at Monterey. It is desirable that, if Highland County Court sits at a different time from the Augusta Co. Court, you should make your monthly visit at the time the Court is in session.

Very Respectfully
Your Obdt Servant

John A. McDonnell
Sub Asst Comr
