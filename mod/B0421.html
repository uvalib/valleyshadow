Freedmen's Bureau Records: Statement of John McChesney, June 20, 1867

Summary:
James Hite records the statement of John McChesney regarding the care he gave to an injured soldier, A. B. Sherer.

Newport, Augusta County Virginia
P.O. Moffat's Creek

A.B. Sherer [illeg.] to John McChesney

June the 11 Two visits & meds introducing [unclear: 'batheter twist] and dressing wound $8.00

12 & 13 Two visits and dressing wound and medicines 6.00

14 16 & 19 Three visits and dressing wound and medicines 9.00

21 26 & 28 Three visits and medicines dressing wound 9.00

29 Visit meds extracting the bullet and dressing wound 5.00

$37.00

Augusta County Viz: This day doth John McChesney personally appeared before me a magistrate for said county and made oath to the above amount given under my honor this 20th day of June 1867.

James F. Hite, Justice of the Peace
