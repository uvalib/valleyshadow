Freedmen's Bureau Records: W. Storer How to Frederick S. Tukey, March 26, 1866

Summary:
How's letter appears to relate to Oscar Morris' employment as Bureau messenger, and his accusations against F. Tukey.

Bureau Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands
Head Quarters, Sixth District Virginia Mr F. S. Tukey Assistant Superintendent
Sub-District B Staunton Virginia

March 26th 1866

Winchester, Virginia

Sir

The following extract for instruction [unclear: made] from the [unclear: branch] [unclear: O---] in Richmond [illeg.] a freedman for your information

"You will also reduce the pay of Oscar Morris [illeg.] at Staunton." "We find no difficulty in having men at ten dollars per month [illeg.] for the same service"

Please report upon the matter.

I am Sir
Very Respectfully
Your Obedient Servant

W. Stover How
Brevet Major [deleted: ]
