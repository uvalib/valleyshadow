Freedmen's Bureau Records: W. Storer How to Orlando Brown, August 16, 1865

Summary:
How submits a request for supplies.

Bureau Refugees, Freedmen &c
Headquarters 6th District Virginia Colonel O. Brown
Assistant Commissioner Bureau of Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands
Richmond Virginia

August 16th 1865

Staunton

Colonel

I have the honor to transmit herewith estimate for supplies of Stationery required for the use of the Bureau in this District in the present Quarter.

I am Colonel Very Respectfully
Your Obedient Servant

W. Storer How
Captain & Assistant Quartermaster
Superintendent Bureau of Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands 6th District Virginia
