Freedmen's Bureau Records: John W. Jordan to [William Cithrow], May 13, 1868

Summary:
Jordan asks the local teacher in Waynesboro to complete a report about schools for blacks in the area.

Bureau Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands,
Office 4th Division, 9th Sub-District, Virginia.

May 13th 1868.

Staunton, Virginia,

To The Teacher of Colored School, Waynesboro, Virginia,

You will please fill out the enclosed Blank and return same to this office by the 28th inst. (including Sabbath School Report).

Respectfully,
Yours etc

John W. Jordan
Assistant Sub Assistant Commissioner
Bureau of Refugees, Freedmen &c
