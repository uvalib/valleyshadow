Augusta County: William F. Dold to John H. McCue, July 4, 1860

Summary:
Dold writes to McCue to inquire about a possible teaching position available at Lovingston. This is a letter fragment.

July 4th 1860

Mr. J. H. McCue:
Dear Sir,

Having just recd a letter from Mr. J. Hotchkiss stating that you want a teacher at Lovingston, I therefore drop you a few lines to ascertain what the prospects may be of obtaining the situation.

I have recently graduated at Emory & Henry College, and can furnish ample references of good moral character &C, not only from the faculty of E. & H. Col. but also from my former teacher. [?] Rev. W.
