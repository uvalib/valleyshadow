Augusta County: James J. A. Trotter to John McCue, October 30, 1860

Summary:
Trotter asks McCue for help in raising money to alleviate Trotter's debt.

James J. A. Trotter

October 30th 1860

Staunton

John H. McCue Esqr
Dear Sir

I Suppose you will think I. am troublesome but it is necessity that makes me write to you again I. am hard up and compelled to raise Some money or have my property Sold by the Sheriff is there any way by which you can help me Some you wrote to me that you expected to make Some arrangement to get Some money it would be a very great accomodation to me at this time if you could do Something for me

Very Respectfully

James J. A. Trotter
