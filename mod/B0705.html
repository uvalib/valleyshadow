Freedmen's Bureau Records: Roswell Waldo to John A. McDonnell, November 23, 1868

Summary:
Waldo wishes to know a few details about an upcoming school report.

Bureau Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands 4th Division 9th Sub-District Captain John A McDonnell
Sub Assistant Commissioner
Winchester Virginia

November 23/68

Staunton

Captain: -

I have the honor to request to be informed if the school report to be submitted by Circular 26. from Richmond October 21/68 - is to include those schools taught under the auspices of local church societies as well as [deleted: by] American Missionary Association. Also if the form on back of said Circular is to be considered merely as a guide by which a form of proper dimensions is to be made up - or is said report to be submitted on back of the Circular?

Very respectfully

Roswell Waldo
Assistant Sub Assistant Commissioner
