Franklin County: Andrew G. Curtin to Simon Cameron, March 19, 1860

Summary:
Curtin discusses possible patronage appointments.

March 19--1860

Bellefonte

Dear Sir

Our mutual friend Daniel Taggart is [illeg.] [illeg.] I have said to him as I now do to you that I will unite with you in asking for him the [illeg.] [illeg.] Lincoln's administration you may fix on him the line.

On this subject I will be pleased to hear from you

Yours Truly

Andrew G. Curtin
