Freedmen's Bureau Records: W. Storer How to Orlando Brown, October 2, 1865

Summary:
How forwards two monthly reports - of rations issued and of men working in the Bureau.

Bureau Refugees, Freedmen & Abandoned Lands
Head Quarters Sixth District Virginia Colonel O. Brown
Assistant Commissioner
Richmond Virginia

October 2d 1865

Staunton

Colonel

I have the honor to transmit herewith my Report of Officers & Enlisted men doing duty in this Bureau, for the month of September 1865, and Report of Rations issued during the month of August 1865.

I am Colonel
Very Respectfully
Your Obedient Servant

W. Storer How, Captain, Assistant Quartermaster
& Superintendent
