Augusta County: John Scott to E. P. Smith, December 26, 1868

Summary:
Scott informs Smith about the teachers' dire need for proper "beding."

Mr Smith Staunton Va

Dec 26th/68

I am much troubled about beding for the lady who came down without it.

If you send another teacher instead of, or in adition to this one (one of which I hope you will do for I am almost discouraged) please have her bring some beding.

I dont want to buy anything here or any more than necessary

1 Blanket in office
2 Blanket
2 Quilts
1 [unclear: Bedside]
1 [illeg.]
1 Pillow

Very Respectfully

John Scott
