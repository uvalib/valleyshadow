Augusta County: Jedediah Hotchkiss to Sara A. Hotchkiss, October 14, 1862

Summary:
Hotchkiss briefly describes a successful raid by Stuart into Pennsylvania, which passed through Chambersburg.

Sara A. Hotchkiss

Tuesday P.M. Oct -- 14th 1862

My Dear Wife --

I only now have a chance to mail my letter at New Town -- and will add that Stuart went to Pennsylvania -- took a round through Chambersburg &c -- captured some 2000 horses, a large number of negroes -- stores &c and destroyed immense stores -- He went the whole way around the camp of McClellan & came away back by Leesburg -- he crossed the Potomac 1st at Hancock -- I am now at New Town -- My bundle went up to Maj. Bell -- so it may get to you - - I am very well --

J. H --
