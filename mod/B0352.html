Freedmen's Bureau Records: J. J. DeLamater to Thomas P. Jackson, December 21, 1867

Summary:
Jackson receives a letter admitting Susan Berry to Howard's Grove Hospital in Richmond.

Bureau Refugees, Freedmen & Abandoned Lands District of Virginia
Office Surgeon in Chief District Assistant Adjutant Surgeon D. R. Brower United States Army
In charge of the Howard Grove Hospital, Richmond Virginia

December 12th 1867

Richmond, Virginia

Will receive in Hospital for treatment, when presented with this order
Susan Berry
an indigent freedwoman now of Staunton Virginia.

Transportation to Hospital directed by Brigadier General Orlando Brown Assistant Commissioner Bureau & to be furnished by the civil authorities.

JJ DeLamater
Brevet Lieutenant Colonel
Bureau Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands District of Virginia

Through Thomas P. Jackson
Agent Bureau
Staunton Virginia
