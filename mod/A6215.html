Augusta County: Byrd and Sibert to David H. Evans, May 5, 1868

Summary:
Byrd and Sibert direct David Evans to collect money due them from a third party.

Mr D. Evans

May 5th 1868

Bridgewater Va

Dear Sir:

According to promise we enclose you note on Parkins, Nelson [illeg.] for 70$ due 1st April--also an order on Same for 25$ due March 20th.

Please present again for collection if it is not convenient for them to pay the money, take a negotiable for the amount of the note and order, payable in 20 or 30 Days. Notify us as to the result, and we shall be under many obligations to you for your attention to it.

Yours Truly

Byrd & Sibert
Dr Sibert
