Freedmen's Bureau Records: John A. McDonnell to Thomas P. Jackson, July 22, 1867

Summary:
McDonnell informs Jackson that his monthly estimate of rations required has not been received in McDonnell's office.

Bureau Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands,
Head Quarters 9th District Department of the Potomac, Mr. Thomas P. Jackson
Agent
Staunton Virginia

July 22d 1867

Winchester, Virginia,

Sir

Your attention is invited to my letter of May 22d last inclosing copy of Circular 32, requiring an Estimate of Rations to be forwarded to this office for consideration, to reach here before the 20th of each month, for the month following.

No such Estimate was received in June for this month, and the one for August has now been due several days.

Your immediate attention to this is desired.

Very Respectfully
Your Obedient Servant

John A. McDonnell
Captain & Sub Assistant Commissioner
