Augusta County: Samuel Moses to Charles Moses, April 21, 1862

Summary:
Samuel Moses writes his father that he may be able to return home soon.

Charles Moses Back Creak Shenando Augusta County Va, Samuel M Moses
a prisoner of war in Camp Chase
April 21, 1862

April 21, 1862

Dear father

I seat my self to inform you all that I am well and hope those few lines and find you all well. I received your letter dated march the 26. I was glad to hear from you all gave my love to mother + Brothers + sisters you wanted to know when I could come home I do not know when but I dont think it will be very long I must bring my letter to a close by saying write soon as you get my letter

and remember your son until death

Samuel M Moses

To Charles Moses
