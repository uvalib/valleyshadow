Freedmen's Bureau Records: James Henry Alexander to [George T. Cook], June 7, 1866

Summary:
Alexander states that McCutchen will not pay him a sum a money unless forced to.

June 7 1866

Mr McCutchen has refuse to pay that money and says he will not pay it unless you send him a note to do it [unclear: will] you please send him one the first opportunity

James Henry Alexander
