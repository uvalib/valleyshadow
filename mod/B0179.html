Freedmen's Bureau Records: Thomas P. Jackson to A. M. Garber, July 19, 1867

Summary:
Jackson asks Garber if he has any information about a man who was taken to Alabama by Garber.

Bureau of Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands,
Office 4th Division, 9th Sub-District, Virginia.
Dr. A M Garber
Livingston
Sumter Co
Alabama A. M. Garber, Esquire

July 19, 1867

Staunton, Virginia,

Sir

Referring to my note of 10th Inst. can you furnish any information which may aid me in discovering Isaac Galway or Galloway (colored) who ws taken to Alabama by Dr. Alex Garber.

Your Obedient Servant

Thomas P Jackson
Agent
