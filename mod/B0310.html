Freedmen's Bureau Records: John A. McDonnell to John W. Jordan, May 4, 1868

Summary:
McDonnell discusses school report forms, and another, unspecified form.

Bureau Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands
Head Quarters 9t Sub District Virginia Colonel John W. Jordan
Assistant Sub Assistant Commissioner
Staunton, Virginia

May 4.68

Winchester,

Colonel

Herewith I send you Blank School Reports, as requested.

Forms No. 4 are required by Circular 5, Bureau Head Quarters. This Circular has not been furnished me. The forms were originally received from the Assistant Commissioner with Circular Letter from Bureau Head Quarters dated January 14.68 which contained a memorandum at the foot stating that "Circular with blanks sent to each Assistant Sub Assistant Commissioner." I presume it is in the file of Circulars from Head Quarters Bureau in your office.

Very Respectfully
Your Obedient Servant

John A. McDonnell
Sub Assistant Commissioner
