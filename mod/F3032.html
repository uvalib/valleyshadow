Franklin County: Jacob Christy to Mary Jane Demus, [no date]

Summary:
Christy writes to his sister about sending him a picture of herself.

Mary Jane Demus

please to send me your likeness and Elizabeth send hers to please to write soon and direct your letter to

Company J 54 Mass volunteers
Morris Island by the way of Port Royal S.C.

Jacob E. Christy

Mary Jane Demus
