Freedmen's Bureau Records: John W. Jordan to Roswell Waldo, November 6, 1868

Summary:
This communication from Jordan to Waldo concerns two claims. Jordan writes that one is in order, but he needs clarification on the second one. The substance of the matter is unclear from the letter.

Bureau of Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands,
Office Fifth Division, Seventh Sub-District of Virginia,
Counties of Bath, Alleghany and Rockbridge.

November 6 1868

Lexington, Virginia

Mr Waldo

Your line received relating to the Railroad claim & Transportation matter. The first is collected and all right the other I cannot understand and can get no information concerning it but will send the money back and take the [unclear: risk].

As soon as I get my [unclear: assets] in which I am expecting daily will send you amount covering back that is $27.14/00 dollars.

Yours &c

John W Jordan
