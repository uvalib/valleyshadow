Augusta County: Frank W. [Lowes?] to Kenton Harper, June 24, 1861

Summary:
Colonel Kenton Harper receives orders from Colonel Jackson to detail a company with officers to transport some rations to headquarters.

24 June 1861,

Head Quarters 1st Brigade
Camp Stevens

Colonel

You will detail our company with company officers to take our days rations and report to Lieutenant Paxton at Head Quarters at 4 o'clock a.m. for official services

By order of Colonel Jackson

Frank W. Lowes
aos

Colonel Harper
