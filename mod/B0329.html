Freedmen's Bureau Records: Frank A. White to Thomas P. Jackson, July 23, 1867

Summary:
This letter appears to relate to legal services Jackson rendered before his tenure as agent in Staunton.

Bureau of Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands,
Office Chief Quartermaster and Disbursing Officer, State of Virginia. Thos. P. Jackson Esq.
Agent Bureau Refugees Freedmen &c
Staunton, Virginia

July 23d 1867

Richmond, Virginia,

Sir,

I have the honor to enclose herewith for your signature to the receipts vouchers in your favor for services rendered as arbitrator of the Taylor Farm in the month of November 1866. Please return them at your earliest convenience.

Very Respectfully
Your Obedient Servant

Frank A. White
Chief Clerk

in the absence of Brevet Major Geo. D. White
Chief Quarter Master and Disbursing Officer
