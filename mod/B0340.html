Freedmen's Bureau Records: J. T. H. Hall to Thomas P. Jackson, August 24, 1867

Summary:
Hall asks Jackson to have Isaac Crebs, a resident of Augusta County, provide a statement regarding his claim against the Pitman estate.

August 24th 1867

Woodstock, Virginia

Friend Jackson

May I again trouble you? Please send the enclosed letter to County Clerk requesting an immediate reply. Also request Mr. Isaac Crebs of Augusta County to furnish such statement of his claim against the Pitman estate, and such facts in the case as he may be disposed.

Respectfully,
Your Friend

J.T.H. Hall
Lieutenant Veterans Reserve Corps & Military Commissioner

Please answer as soon as convenient JH
