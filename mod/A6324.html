Augusta County: David H. Evans to Mary Anna Sibert, December 17, 1868

Summary:
Evans writes briefly to Sibert to explain why he could not send a letter earlier and announces he will be visiting Sunday.

Dec 17 1868

Staunton

My Dear Mollie

My love I have just time to write you a line. I will be up Sunday if I live I would have written to you on last Tuesday but Johnny Noon was out of town & I Could not have Sent it he just got back a few moments ago & told me he would send this up. I love you with all my heart & Soul all are well send love to you all my love to all Good bye

Yours Loving

D H Evans
