Freedmen's Bureau Records: W. Storer How to Orlando Brown, February 12, 1866

Summary:
How submits his reports concerning officers and civilians employed by the Bureau.

Colonel O Brown
Assistant Commissioner &c

February 12th 1866

Colonel

I have the honor to transmit herewith the following report, required by instructions this day received for the month of January 1866, viz:

Report of "Officers on duty in the District" Report of "Civilians employed as Assistant Superintendents" Report of "Civilians employed in Freedmen's Courts"

I have no other reports to make for that month.

I am, Colonel, &c

W.S.H.
Captain and Assistant Quartermaster
Superintendent &c
