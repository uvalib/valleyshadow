Augusta County: Travis E. Luckett to John H. McCue, November 30, 1853

Summary:
Travis E. Luckett inquires into the cost of having the slaves he recently purchased insured by the Lynchburg Hose & Fire Insurance Company.

Mr J. H. McCue

November 30th 1853

Warm Springs

Dear Sir

I perceive in the "[unclear: Virginia] Messenger" that you are the Agent at Staunton for the "Lynchburg Hose & Fire Insurance Company, Having recently purchaced a few valuable negroes whose lives I wished to have insured. You will please inform me at your earliest convenience, as to the Reputations, Rates &c of the Company & much oblige

Yours etcetera

Travis E. Luckett
