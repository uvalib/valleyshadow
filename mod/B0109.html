Freedman's Bureau Records: E. Whittlesey to Asst. Commr. For Virginia, March 6th, 1868

Summary:

WAR DEPARTMENT
Bureau Refugees, Freedmen, and Abandoned Lands, Asst. Commr. For Virginia

March 6th, 1868

Washington

Respectfully returned to the Asst. Commr. For Virginia, Whose attention is invited to the enclosed copy of letter from Rev. S. E. Colburn.

By order of Major Genl O. O. Howard

E. Whittlesey
A.A.A. Genl
