Freedmen's Bureau Records: S. R. Bond to John W. Jordan, June 11, 1868

Summary:
Bond informs Jordan that a claim has been rejected. Although the case was resolved while Jordan was the Agent in Staunton, it appears to be related to his previous post.

Law and Claim Office
S. R. BOND,
Will practice in the Courts of the District, the Court of Claims, and before all the Departments.
488 1/2 Seventh Street, Colonel John W. Jordan
Farmville, Virginia

June 11, 1868.

Washington D.C.,

Dear Sir

Enclosed please find discharge of Randall Brown whose claim has been rejected on the ground that he has received $100, the 1st installment - all he is entitled to.

Very Respectfully Yours

S.R. Bond
