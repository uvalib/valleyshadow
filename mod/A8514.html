Augusta County: W. W. Gibbs to John H. McCue, January 1, 1858

Summary:
W. W. Gibbs, in response to John H. McCue's request, informs McCue that Thomas Bowan has a cook to hire.

John H McCue Esquire

January 1/58

Your letter received and I. went to every place I. thought I could procure you a cook or nurse but could find none all having been disposed of. I hear Thomas Bowan near Greenwood Tunnel has a good cook for hire if you are not supplied you had best write to him or come over and see him such as your want is hard to find our respects to you and lady

Your friend Very Truly

W. W. Gibbs
