Freedmen's Bureau Records: Isaac Galloway to Thomas P. Jackson, October 25, 1867

Summary:
Galloway writes that the information Jackson gave him about how to find his son was not accurate, and he asks for more information if possible.

Thomas P Jackson Esquire
Staunton Virginia

October 25th 1867

Wilmington Delaware

Respected Sir

I have written to my son at Livingston Alabama but the letter has been returned to me unclaimed On August 3d you kindly gave me the directions where to write but it seems strange that no such direction found him. I enclose the letter to you and beg you would try and find him for me if not too much trouble.

Yours truly
and respectfully

Isaac Galloway
