Freedmen's Bureau Records: J. J. DeLamater to Orlando Brown, June 20, 1868

Summary:
DeLamater forwards the transportation order for Edward Brooks, from Howard's Grove Hospital, in Richmond, to Staunton.

Bureau of Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands, District of Virginia
Office Surgeon in Chief [illeg.]

June 20th 1868

Richmond, Virginia

Received from Bureau Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands, Richmond, Virginia, Transportation Orders No. 26 for Edward Brooks, Sr. requested by Acting Assistant Surgeon D. R. Brower, May 27th, 1868.

JJ DeLamater
Surgeon in Chief, Bureau of Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands, District of Virginia

To Brevet Brigadier General O. Brown,
Assistant Commissioner
