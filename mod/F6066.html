Franklin County: D. M. Dull to George W. Brewer, January 7, 1867

Summary:
D. M. Dull makes final arrangements for a business meeting in Harrisburg with George W. Brewer.

January 7th 1867

hoveytown

Mr Brewer

Dear Sir I received your letter this morning I will meet you in Harrisburg tomorrow Wednesday- I will go to Harrisburg tonight and will remain there to you come down the reason I do not write you in time is I did not receive your letter in time to this morning but I think you will get this in time come down as soon as you get this letter & if I am not in Harrisburg I will leave the papers with my Brother James I Dull there for you with what instruction I may have but come down if you can

I fear trouble but Mr MClellance told me that MClure had put collateral in his hands to pay this money and that he had a judgement to make him secure

Your Truly

D. M Dull
