Franklin County: A. H. Senseny, Medical Certification for William W. Walker, January 14, 1863

Summary:
A. H. Senseny certifies William W. Walker's sickness in support of an application for a leave of absence.

Jany. 14th 1863

Chambersburg

Captain W. W. Walker of Company E" 126th Regt. PA. Having applied for a certificate on which to ground an application for leave of absence, I do hereby certify that I have examined this officer and find that he is still suffering from the effects of a low form of fever. And that in consequence thereof, he is, in my opinion unfit, for duty. I further declare my belief that he will not be able to be able to resume his duties in a less period than six weeks,

A. H. Senseny M.D.

I concur in above
James Hamilton M.D.

Approved
Wm. R. Brown
Capt. 15th U.S. Infy.
Commanding [deleted: ]
Chambersburg Pa.
January 14th, 1863
