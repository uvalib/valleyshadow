Augusta County: F. S. Tukey to Unknown, November 4, 1865

Summary:
Tukey requests a series of items needed for AMA schools in Staunton.

Nov 4th/65

Wanted Immediately
for the colored schools in Staunton Va
(3) Three teachers (see letter this date (W.L.C.)
300 Copies ("GreenBacks") Saunders Primer
100"" 1st Readers
50 "" 2d
25 "" New Speller and Definer
25 Monteiths Geography #2 or something equivalent
24 dozen (2 [illeg.]) Rd Oral [unclear: Corner] Slates medium size
500 Slate Pencils (soap stone-short)
1 Box school Crayons (for black board)
Copy Books no. 3/1 2/2 1/3 doz
1 [illeg.] Pen Holders
6 " Pens. (be sure they will fit the Holders)
1 doz Papers Ink Powder
1 Sett Saunders Charts sheets only
1 " Outline Maps with key ([unclear: Cornells] or Mitchells)
6 Copies Golden [illeg.] + Golden Chain
6 "" Censor
6 " Harp of Freedom

Direct to

F. S. Tukey Supt Freedmen

Staunton Va
Care Col O. Brown Com.
Richmond
Clothing will required here in considerable quantities. It is now-cold weather
