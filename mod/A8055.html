Augusta County: Piper Frankhouser to John McCue, June 20, 1860

Summary:
Frankhouser tries to collect debt from McCue.

Piper & Frankhouser
1860

June '20th 60

Staunton

Mr. J H McCue
Dear Sir

We wrote to you a few days at Nelson C House, but suppose you did not receive it.

Our object now is to say to you that your first note for 86 50/100 $ was due on the 15th inst. what can we depend upon.

We would be glad if you could make arrangements to pay it now, as we really need help now.

Respectfully your
friend

Piper Frankhouser
