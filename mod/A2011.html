Augusta County: James Dinwiddie, Capt Ch Conts, W. Macdormish to William B. Gallaher, Jan 20 1865

Summary:
William B. Gallaher receives a letter from his business associates about their contract for leather.

William B. Gallaher

Jan 20 1865

Richmond Arsenal

Sir,

Your letter of the 19th was delivered to me this morning by your brother.

I enclose a new contract for leather/4 copies/which please sign in the presence of a witness and return to me.

I forwarded to your address, several days ago the contract for the additional number of Hides, with a proviso with regard to loss by the public enemy. I am at a loss to account for it not having reached you.

Very respectful

Jas. Dinwiddie
Jas. Dinwiddie
Capt Ch Conts
W. Macdormish
