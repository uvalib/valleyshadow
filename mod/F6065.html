Franklin County: Jacob Middows to George W. Brewer, August 18, 1864

Summary:
Jacob Middows sends his pay to his friend George Brewer to pass along to his mother through Joseph Rock. He also includes a sum from George Gates to his father.

August 18 1864

Camp Near Petersburg

Dear Friend George W. Brewer

I'll state to you that I expressed $110.00 Dollars to you And you please And Let Joseph Rock of Quincy Township Franklin County Pennsylvania Lift it And give it to My Mother E Middows And George Gates sent 50 00 dollars to [unclear: you ] and his father of George Gates of Quincy Township Franklin County Pennsylvania is to lift his so if they they comes for it you'll give it to them that is if I should of gots should happens to Lose our receipts that they

[page 2]
Can Draw it but if I don't lose the receipt I'll send it to them then they can draw it

Yours
Truly

Jacob Middows
&
George Gates.

And by So doing you'll Oblige your Friends G Gates and Jacob Middows to George W Brewer
Chambersburg
Franklin
County
Pennsylvania
