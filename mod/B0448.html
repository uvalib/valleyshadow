Freedmen's Bureau Records: Receipt of Charles Grandison, June 17, 1868

Summary:
Grandison receives $12.50 in a settlement against Harvey Risk.

Bureau of Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands
Office 4th Division, 9th Sub-District, Virginia

June 17t 1868

Staunton, Virginia

I received this day from Colonel John W. Jordan Assistant Sub Assistant Commissioner &c Twelve and 50/100 Dollars, being the amount in full of judgment awarded by him, in my favor against Harvey Risk.

Witness A.L. [unclear: Manf----]

Chas [added: his X mark] Grandison
