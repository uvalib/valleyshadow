Freedmen's Bureau Records: A. M . Garber to Thomas P. Jackson, July 27, 1867

Summary:
Garber reports on the condition of one of his former slaves (presumably in response to an inquiry from the Staunton Bureau office).

Agent of the Bureau of Freedmen Refugees &c Staunton Virginia Livingston Alabama

July 27 1867

I have received your letter or the 19 inst. Isaac Galloway, my former slave, lives in this neighborhood, he has a wife & two children, and is doing about as well as the freedmen generally.

Your Obedient Servant

A. M. Garber
