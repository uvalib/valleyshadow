Augusta County: Powell & Co. to John McCue, February 21, 1860

Summary:
Powell & Co. inquire about money McCue owes them.

Powell & Co
Staunton
1860

Feby 21. 1860

Staunton

Dear Sir

When you left here you was owing us about $37- dollars and promised us about one week before you left that you would certainly pay us before you did leave but we haven't seen it yet Mr McCue you know the bill we have against you was for cash [unclear: articles] such as [unclear: flour] & [unclear: groceries] & more than that you know we have accomodated you in the loan of money several times & after doing such favours then have to lose $37--it is rather hard. So we are in hopes you will send us the amount soon by attending to the [unclear: aban] you will oblige your friend let us hear from you by return mail

Cis Powell & Co
