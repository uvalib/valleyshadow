Augusta County: Hannah M. Berry to Thomas M. Smiley, [1862]

Summary:
Enclosed secession badge made by author for recipient to wear.

Thomas M. Smiley

[1862]

Cousin Tom

I send you this Secession badge as a token or the regard I have for you. It is small but I wish you to wear it for my sake. I would be glad to receive a letter from you & would most assuredly take pleasure in answering it.

Your friend & Cousin

Hannah M Berry
