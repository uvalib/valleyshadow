Freedmen's Bureau Records: W. Storer How to D. T. Wells, January 29, 1866

Summary:
How forwards a request that two men be arrested and sent to Richmond.

Lieutenatn D.T. Wells, Acting Assistant Adjutant General
Military District Shenandoah
Winchester, Virginia

January 29 1866

Sir

I have the honor to submit the enclosed communication of Colonel J.W. [unclear: McEnt--] Provost Judge Richmond, Virginia as forwarded by Colonel Orlando Brown, Assistant Commissioner Bureau of Refugees, Freedmen & Abandoned Lands, at Richmond, Virginia with the request that the freedmen George Whit & John Whit may be arrested and sent to Colonel O. Brown Assistant Commissioner at Richmond, Virginia.

I am, Sir &c

W. Storer How
Captain &c
