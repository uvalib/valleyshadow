Augusta County: Mary D. Williams to W. E. Whiting, March 5, 1866

Summary:
Williams asks W. E. Whiting to send funds for four months salary and travel expenses.

Mr. Whiting Staunton, Va.

March 5th 1866

Sir

Please send me the four months salary due starting from Nov. 7th to March 7th

Traveling expenses from Greenfield Mass to New Haven $3,35
Carriage in New Haven 1,00
From New Haven to New York 2,00
Carriage in Richmond 50
Board in Richmond 3,00
Express in Richmond ,69
From Richmond to Staunton 6,00
____
16,54

Very respectfully,

Mary D. Williams
