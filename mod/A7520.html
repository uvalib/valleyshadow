Augusta County: David H. Evans to Mary Anna Sibert, September 10, 1867

Summary:
Evans reaffirms his love for Sibert and announces his plans to visit her soon.

Miss Mollie Sibert
Mount Solon
Augusta Co
Va

Sept 10th 1867

Staunton

Miss Mollie

Your welcome letter reached me by last Friday's mail I was truly glad to hear from you no I have not forgotten you but think of you Every hour through the day do you think I could forget one that I love more than all this world. I have only ten minutes to write this before the mail starts I started to write you a long letter this morning just as I commenced Brother came in & told me I had to go way & I just got back. so you must Excuse this & not think Ill of me for not writing you a longer

[page 2]
letter Ella Magnus & my self are coming down to Solon next Sunday if nothing prevents & I want you to come up with us your two weeks are out I think your Ma. said you could come in that time so we are coming prepared to bring you back with us My love to your Ma.

Remember me to my friends all send love to you I send love & Kiss write soon

Yours

David H Evans
