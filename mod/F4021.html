Franklin County: Alexander K. McClure to Eli Slifer, July 20, 1859

Summary:
McClure writes that the Valley Spirit has libeled him in an effort to sabotage his nomination in the elections for the State Senate.

Private

July 20

Chambersburg

Dear Col

The Spirit - [unclear: Locofoco] - in a desperate effort to defeat my nomination for Senator, has this morning published a [unclear: tissue] of the most infamous libels - of which the [unclear: alure] is one. Please send me some such an answer as the enclosed.

So far from harming one it has on had the opposite effect.

Truly thine

M C
