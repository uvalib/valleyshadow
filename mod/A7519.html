Augusta County: David H. Evans to Mary Anna Sibert, September 3, 1867

Summary:
Evans reports on a recent trip.

Miss Mollie Sibert
Mount Solon
Va

Sept 3 1867

Staunton

Miss Mollie

We [unclear: rived] home safely and had a very pleasant drive through the [unclear: vall] which I enjoyed very much we [unclear: rived] in Staunton at half past ten. Ella had a good warm Supper prepared for us which Magnus and my self did ample justice to you may look for Magnus down after you in two weeks if not sooner Ella says she don't think she will let you stay that long so you will have to hurry up & get through with your spinning this week so you can come up with him next week I think I will

[page 2]
go over to night and tell Ella to send for you this week for we can't do with out you I have thought of you all day and wondered if Miss Mollie thought of DH Evans while she was spinning. I would like to see you at the Spinning Wheel I know you look interesting. you must Excuse this short note I have been keep so busy since I came home that I have not had time to write I am interrupted ever few minutes while I am writing this Excuse all mistakes and write to me soon My love to your Ma, & all of my friends I send a kiss to Miss Mollie all are well

Good Buy

David H. Evans
