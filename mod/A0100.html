Augusta County: Charles B. Carter to Eliza Carter, April 26, 1862

Summary:
Carter writes to his wife about life in the Camp Chase Prison. He spends his time making crafts to sell, and is trying to send the money home.

Mrs Eliza Carter, Waynesboro Augusta County Virginia (via norfolk va under flag of truce)
Charles B Carter Prisoner of War at Camp Chase prison Ohio

AP 26 1862

Camp Chase Columbus Ohio

Dear Wife et
