Augusta: William H. Parker to Robert Hanson Fleming, May 2, 1865

Summary:
This is Fleming's furlough order, which grants him leave and rations for his trip home.

May 2nd, 1865

Sir:

You are hereby detached from the Naval-School, and leave is granted you to visit your home.

You will report by letter to the Honorable Secretary of the Navy as soon as practicable.

Paymaster Wheless will issue you ten days rations, and all quartermasters are requested to furnish you with transportation.

Respectfully,
Your obedient servant,

William H. Parker
Commandant
By order of the
Secretary of the Navy

Midshipman Robert Hanson Fleming
Confederate States Navy
