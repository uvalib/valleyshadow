Freedmen's Bureau Records: Garrick Mallery to N. C. Brackett, June 26, 1867

Summary:
Mallery forwards transportation orders for Brackett and three teachers through Jackson in Staunton.

Bureau of Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands,
Head Quarters Assistant Commissioner, State of Virginia Mr. N. C. Brackett,
(thru Mr. Thomas P. Jackson, Agent)

June 26th 1867

Richmond, Virginia,

Sir,

I enclose herewith transportation orders Number 950, 952, 953, & 958 for Miss Annie M. Shaw, Miss Gilpha R. Harper, Miss Ann A. Wright, & Mr. N. C. Brackett. Please sign & return enclosed receipt.

Yours respectfully

Garrick Mallery
Assistant: 43d Infantry
A.A.A.General
