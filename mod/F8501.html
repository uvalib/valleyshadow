Franklin County: William [McLellan] to Edward McPherson, February 11, 1860

Summary:
William McLellan provides Edward McPherson with a list of people to whom German documents can be sent.

February 11/60

Chambersburg

Dear Sir

I herewith forward you a list of persons in this vicinity to whom you can forward German Documents through the Chambersburg Post Office.

Yours Respectfully

William McLell

John Goetman Jacob Shaffer
