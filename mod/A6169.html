Augusta County: William [Sobenchaur?] to Edward Flood, February 2, 1865

Summary:
Captain William [Sobenchaur?] writes to Flood about working on a bridge nearby, and asks him to contact Colonel William Procter Smith when in need of supplies.

Staunton, Virginia,

February 2, 1865.

Lieutenant Edward Flood,
Company F Pioneer Corps,
Lexington, Virginia

Lieutenant--You will please write immediately on the receipt of this letter, to Colonel William Procter Smith, Chief Engineer [unclear: Valley Division ], at this place, & inform him what additional tools, or if any, you will need in working on the bridge at Buchanan.

Very respectfully etc.

William sobenchaur
Captain Engineers
