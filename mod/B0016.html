Freedmen's Bureau Records: S. P. Lee to Thomas P. Jackson, January 25, 1868

Summary:
Lee, the Sub Assistant Commissioner for the 10th Virginia Sub-District, sends Jackson a supply of women's clothing.

Bureau of Refugees, Freedmen, and Abandoned Lands,
Headquarters 10th Sub-District of Virginia. Thomas P. Jackson
Assistant Sub Assistant Commissioner
Staunton, Virginia

January 25 th 1868

Alexandria, Virginia

Sir:

Enclosed please find Duplicate and Triplicate Boxes of clothing. Viz.

40 Womens Dresses
40 Womens Petticoats
25 Womens Chemises

shipped to your address this day, upon the arrival of the same please receipt the Duplicate and forward it to Captain Geo. Q. White, Chief Quartermaster of the Bureau at Richmond to whom the clothing will be invoiced.

I also enclose Memorandum Receipts in duplicate, which please sign and return to me.

Very Respectfully
Your Obedient Servant

S.P. Lee
Captain & Brevet Lieutenant Colonel US Army
Sub Assistant Commissioner 10th Sub District Virginia
