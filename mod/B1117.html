Freedmen's Bureau Records: W. Storer How to Orlando Brown, September 15, 1865

Summary:
How indicates that he is forwarding a supply request and a report of civilians employed in his District.

Bureau Refugees, Freedmen & Abandoned Lands
Head Quarters Sixth District Virginia Colonel O. Brown
Assistant Commissioner
Richmond, Virginia

September 15th 1865

Staunton

Colonel

I have the honor to transmit herewith an Estimate for Stationery for 4th Quarter 1865, and Report of Civilians employed by me in the month ending August 31st 1865.

I am, Colonel
Very Respectfully
Your Obedient Servant

W. Storer How
Captain & Assistant Quartermaster
Superintendent Sixth District, Virginia
