Augusta County: Frank W. Larry to Kenton Harper, June 21, 1861

Summary:
A soldier writes to Colonel Kenton Harper about the status of some needed supplies being sent in wagons to Col Harper.

June 21, 1861

Head Quarters1stBrigade
Near tisburg

Colonel

A small wagon is going of today & may be sufficient to bring what you want, if not another wagon will go tomorrow and bring what you may still need, if you prefer it a detail of new men from up Regiment can guard the wagon, Colonel writes to see you as soon as practicable.

Very respectfully

Frank W Larry

Colonel
