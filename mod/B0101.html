Freedman's Bureau Records: Thomas P. Jackson to Captain John A. McDonnell, January 24, 1868

Summary:

B.R.F + A L. Office 4" Dn 9 S Dist Va Captain John A. McDonnell

January 24, 1868

Staunton

Respectfully returned to Capt Johhn A McDonnell Sub Asst Com Betsey Brown chrildren one Sarah F. 10 years, Henry 3, Margaret 2. Delay on returning this paper is caused by difficulty in finding Betsy Brown who was compelled to leave her first lodging place

Tho P Jackson, Asst Sub Asst Com
