Freedmen's Bureau Records: John W. Jordan to J. Marshall McCue, April 27, 1868

Summary:
Jordan inquires about the status of a case against six men.

Bureau of Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands,
Office 4th Division, 9th Sub-District, Virginia. J. Marshall McCue
Presiding Justice
Augusta County, Virginia

April 27, 1868.

Staunton, Virginia

My dear Sir,

I have the honor to request to be informed if the following named case has been set for trial today and if not what condition the case is in at this date or please furnish me with any information, touching the matter involved.

Commonwealth of Virginia vs. Henry Fisher, William Ross, John Craig, Robt Craig, Wm Craig ([unclear: Rvs] in [unclear: Summers]) and Abraham Ross.

Respectfully,
Your obedient servant

Jno W. Jordan
Assistant Sub Assistant Commissioner &c
