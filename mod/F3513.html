Franklin County: John H. Reed to General L. Thomas, January 10, 1863

Summary:
John H. Reed encloses his physician's certificate designating his unfitness for travel.

L. Thomas Adj., Genl. U.S.

January 10, 1863

Johnstown

Sir

Enclosed please find a Physician's certificate of my unfitness to travel.

My furlough on account of wounds commenced Dec. 23d for twenty days signed by L. Thomas Adjt. Genl.

There being no Army Physician in this place I send the certificate of my attending physician as directed by Regulations.

My address is Johnstown Cambria Co. Penna.

Very Respectfully your obedient servant

J.H. Reed Capt. Co. D
126 Regt Penna Vol
