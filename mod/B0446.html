Freedmen's Bureau Records: John A. McDonnell to Roswell Waldo, September 11, 1868

Summary:
McDonnell instructs Waldo to send horse supplies to the Agent in Woodstock.

Bureau Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands
Head Quarters 9th Sub-District Virginia Roswell Waldo
Assistant Sub Assistant Commissioner
Staunton, Virginia

September 11, 1868

Winchester

Sir

Please send to Mr. Watkins James, A.S.A.C. Woodstock, Va.
one (1) Riding Saddle
one (1) Riding Bridle
one (1) Saddle Blanket

Very Respectfully
Your Obedient Servant

John A. McDonnell
Sub Assistant Commissioner
