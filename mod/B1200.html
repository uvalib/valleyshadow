Freedmen's Bureau Records: W. Storer How to Orlando Brown, September 22, 1865

Summary:
How lists which reports he has, and has not, filed with the Bureau office.

U. S. Military Telegraph To Colonel O. Brown Assistant Commissioner

September 22 1865

By Telegraph from Staunton

I have no report to make for the month of August under the head of officers & enlisted men, Lands, refugees & Freedmen in camps. Schools bureau property Bureau C.C. & [unclear: G.E.] Bureau funds or Quartermaster C.C. & G.E. My report of Civilians was sent
also report of rations issued Report of conditions of freedmen & copy of return of Quartermaster Stores & Quartermaster Cash account. Copies of the abstract & vouchers accompanying the latter will be forwarded immediately.

W. Storer How
Captain Assistant Quartermaster Superintendent 6th District
