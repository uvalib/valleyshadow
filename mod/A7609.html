Augusta County: John Quincy Adams Nadenbousch to Hester J. Nadenbousch, September 24, 1863

Summary:
Nadenbousch sends a quick note to his wife asking her to write him by "flag of truce" if necessary.

September 24/63

Staunton Virginia

Dear Wife

I am quite well have not heard one word from you since I left home. The people here from our section of country are well

Write me by flag truce if can't otherwise My love to all friends

Yours ever

John Quincy Adams Nadenbousch

Carried by the kindness of a friend who visits an ill relation
John Quincy Adams Nadenbousch
