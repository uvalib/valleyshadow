Augusta County: William Proctor Smith to Edward Flood, January 28, 1865

Summary:
Smith informs Flood that their horses are nearly dead.

Lieutenant Edward Flood
Company Pioneer Party

January 28 1865

H a Louisiana Engineers Valley Division

Lieutenant

Enclosed you will find orders for G. W. Myres to Report to Major Rogers- His services are sadly needed as forage [unclear: Master]- our horses are nearly dead- You will have to detail someone else in his place-

Respectfully

William Procter Smith
Colonel Engineers
