Freedmen's Bureau Records: Thomas P. Jackson to Henry Brown, December 31, 1867

Summary:
Jackson writes that Brown is "harboring" a girl who is indentured to a J. Mitchell, and must return her. Jackson's subsequent note indicates that she was returned.

Bureau of Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands
Office 4th Division, 9th Sub-District, Virginia. Henry Brown (colored)
Mr. Lee Waddell

December 31st 1867

Staunton, Virginia

Information is lodged that Rebecca Eley Jameson or Brown an indentured apprentice to Mr. J. [unclear: K.] Mitchell is harbored in your house.

This is to notify you to return said girl, to Mr Mitchell's house and for the future refuse to [unclear: receive] her without permission from Mr Mitchell

Thos P. Jackson
Agent
