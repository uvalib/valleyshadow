Freedmen's Bureau Records: R. M. Manly to Garrick Mallery, April 23, 1869

Summary:
Manly authorizes a rent payment for a school building in Staunton.

Bureau Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands,
Head-Quarters Assistant Commissioner, District of Virginia. Brevet Colonel Garrick Mallery
Acting Assistant Adjutant General

April 23, 1869

Richmond, Virginia

Colonel

I have the honor to request that rent be paid, at the rate of $17.00 per month from the 1st of April inst. to John Scott of Staunton, Va for the school house used by the American Missionary Association, in that town.

Very Respectfully
Your Obedient Servant

R. M. Manly
Assistant Superintendent Schools
