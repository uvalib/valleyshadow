Freedmen's Bureau Records: Albert Ordway to Orlando Brown, February 1, 1866

Summary:
Ordway writes about the letter of Priscilla Marshall, from Franklin County, Pennsylvania, concerning her missing children.

Headquarters, Department of Virginia
Office of the Provost Marshal General,
[deleted: Armies operating against Richmond, Va.] Brevet Brigadier General O. Brown
Assistant Commissioner etc. etc.

February 1 1866

Richmond, Virginia

General

From my letter book it appears that the letter concerning the two colored children for whom I advertised was written by Mrs. P. Marshall, Antrim Township, Franklin County Pennsylvania -- their Mother who stated that the children were carried off by some rebel soldiers. I have not the original letter on file as it was returned through General Smith's office, July 5, '65

Very respectfully
Your obedient servant

Albert Ordway
Colonel & Provost Major General
