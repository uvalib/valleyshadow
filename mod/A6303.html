Augusta County: David H. Evans to Mary Anna Sibert, May 26, 1868

Summary:
Sibert reports on family news and his inability to visit this weekend.

May 26 1868

Staunton

My Dear Mollie

Your dear & welcome letter of the 19th has been received & read with much pleasure, you Must blame me this for writing you a long letter. I have been so busy all the Morning that I have not had time to write to you my love. I am so glad that Ada has come so you can spend the Summer with Ella she will move next week in her new house

I am sorry I can't pay you my usual visit this Saturday. Brother is

[page 2]
moving & it will be impossible for me to get off I will be down Saturday a week for certain

I still love you the Same with all my heart & Soul. I must Close Else I won't get this note in the office in time for the Mail

My love to your Ma & Pa Excuse this writing it was done in haste Ella & family are all well I send love & a Kiss to you my love. I do wish I could come down this Saturday to See you but I can't God Bless you good bye

Yours

D H Evans
