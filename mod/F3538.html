Franklin County: A. A. Humphreys to Lt. Col. F. T. Locke, April 11, 1863

Summary:
A. A. Humphreys recommended John W. Stewart as the Assistant Commissary of Muster to replace E. W. Alcott.

Head Qrs. 3rd Division
5th Corps Lt. Colonel F.T. Locke
A.A. Genl. 5th Corps

April 11th, 1863

Colonel

In compliance with endorsement from Corps Head Quarters made in application of Lt. E. W. Alcott to be removed from duty as Asst. Commissary of Muster I have the honor to designate 1st Lt. John W. Stewart Adjt. 126th Regiment Penna Vols as a suitable officer to relieve Lieut. Alcott.

Very Respectfully
Your Obedient Servant

A. A. Humphreys
Brig. Genl. Cmdg. Div.
