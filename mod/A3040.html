Augusta County: Tony Pastor to Annie Harris, March 8, 1864

Summary:
Pastor writes to Annie from prison, keeping an upbeat attitude.

March 8 th 1864

Old CapitolWashington D.C.

Dear Annie

Not having an opportunity of answering your kind and very welcome letter after its reception, I now take the opportunity of replying in a short epistle, although I fear it will not prove very interesting, owing to circumstances over which I have no control .

I have been quite well since my arrival at this place, with the exception of a slight cold. The rest of the boys are generally well. Stuart Brown has received a letter from his brother John, they are all well.

My kindest regards to all inquiring friends and believe me as ever yours.

Tony Pastor

Direct to "Old Capitol Prison"
Washington D.C.
