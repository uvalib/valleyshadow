Freedman's Bureau Records: O. Brown to John A. McDonnell, January 15th, 1868

Summary:

H'd Q'rs Asst Comm Dist of Va
Bureau of R. F. & A. L. John A. McDonnell

January 15th, 1868

Richmond, Va.

Respectfully re- turned to John A. McDonnell, Officer in charge 9th Sub. Dist., Va,., inviting attention to endorsement of Lt. A. Mahony, A.S. A. C.

O Brown
BA Brig. Genl. Vols.
Asst.Comm
