Freedmen's Bureau Records: John A. McDonnell to Roswell Waldo, September 14, 1868

Summary:
McDonnell forwards receipts for clothing issued.

Bureau Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands
HeadQuarters 9th Sub-District Virginia Roswell Waldo
Assistant Sub Assistant Commissioner
Staunton, Virginia

September 14 1868

Winchester

Sir

I have the honor to return herewith Receipt Rolls for clothing issued by you. You will please enter it upon Colonel Jordan's Rolls enclosed to you with my Letter of 11th inst.

A supply of Clothing Rolls is this day sent you.

The headings and signature to certificate will be left blank.

The signatures of the recipients will be witnessed by you.

Very Respectfully
Your Obedient Servant

John A. McDonnell
Sub Assistant Commissioner
