Freedmen's Bureau Records: Thomas P. Jackson to John A. McDonnell, July 2, 1867

Summary:
Jackson inquires if McDonnell has information about a complaint regarding money owed.

Bureau of Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands,
Office 4th Division, 9th Sub-District, Virginia, Captain John A. McDonnell
Sub Assistant Commissioner
Winchester

July 2d 1867.

Staunton, Virginia,

Captain

Reily McDonald (colored) calls frequently to know if the Bill for balance $8 50/100 due to him by Mr. C.W. Oltmann, sent from this office April 24th (Letter Number 60 [unclear: v.1]/67) has been paid, as he needs the money. McDonald is sure that Oltmann is at Lexington.

Your obedient servant

Thomas P. Jackson
Agent
