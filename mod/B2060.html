Freedmen's Bureau Records: R. M. Manly to G. T. F. Boulding, January 15, 1870

Summary:
Manly reports that due to a shortage of funds, he will be able to provide limited to support to Boulding's school in Mt. Solon.

G.T.F. Boulding

January 15 1870

Sir:

Funds are getting low and I shall not be able to pay more than $5 per month toward your school. If you wish to go on, depending upon the people for the [unclear: rest], you must write and let me know, and send your report on the last day of each month.

Yours &c

R.M. Manly
Superintendent of Education
