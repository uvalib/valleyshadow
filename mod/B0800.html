Freedmen's Bureau Records: Thomas P. Jackson to A R McCray, May 30, 1867

Summary:
Jackson asks the recipient to appear in his office in order to settle a complaint against him.

Bureau Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands
Office of 4th Division, 9th Sub-District, Virginia Mr. A R McCray

May 30/67

Fishersville

Sir

William Salsbury (colored) complains that you are indebted to him under contract entered into by you August 11/66 which contract is lodged at this office. Please call on me Saturday June 1st when I hope arrangements can be made for settlement without further trouble.

Your obedient servant

Thos P Jackson
Agent
