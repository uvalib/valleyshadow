Franklin County: David Demus to Mary Jane Demus, December 7, 1864

Summary:
Demus talks about his desire to return home again and mentions seeing one of Mary Jane Demus's relatives.

Mary Jane Demus

December the 7th 1864

Morris island S C

My Dear

i take this present time to inform you that i am Well at present and hope When these few lines reach your Dear hand they may find you in the same state of health i am sorry that i Did not get a letter from you this mail but i Can't say W hether you Wrote or not for the mail Went to the regiment so that We Did not get any letters yet but a how happy i Would be to get a letter from you for i think it so long since i heard from you i Can tell you that going home just spoiled me for i Can't get it out of my head all that i Can do i Can't get it out of my head a My Dear i think sometimes i Can't [unclear: sty] out till the two years is up but i think if you write Why it Will [unclear: Ware] away but When I look at your Dear face Why it looks so Well Why i say to myself how Can i stay away from you but i hope that the time Will soon slip around but i Can tell you that if i ever get back i shall never leave you again but i hope and trust that god may spare me to get home again i think that We Can live together all the Days of my life al l the men that is in [unclear: tome] Can't get me again it Would take a Wagon and six horses to pool me out and i Can't tell you anything about the Boys in this letter for i have not heard from them and i did not get to s ee them but i saw Jacob Cristy on the [unclear: Dock] he is [unclear: ordly] [unclear: fros] the [unclear: Cirlen] of the 55th Mass. volunteers but i heard t hat the [unclear: Cirl] Was kill and i Can't tell Where he is but i think that they Will all back again soon i have not got time to write much but i hope When these few lines reach you Why you Will write soon nothing more at present but still remain

your Dear husband

David A Demus

to his Dear beloved Wife yur till Death
Mary Jane Demus
here is one Dollar for you to get your postage stamp so that you need not pay for them and if it get home safe i Will send you some more
