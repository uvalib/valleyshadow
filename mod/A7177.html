Augusta County: Annie A. Dunn to Samuel Hunt, March 23, 1866

Summary:
Dunn states that she began her work for the AMA in Staunton on October 15, 1865.

Rev. S. Hunt Staunton Va.

March 23rd 1866

Dear Sir

Dear Sir Yours of the 22nd is just received in which you ask the date as to when I commenced my labors here in Staunton. I commenced them on the fifteenth day of Oct. 1865.

Very Respectfully

Mrs Annie A. Dunn

Box 115 Staunton Va.
