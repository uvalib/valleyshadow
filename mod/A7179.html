Augusta County: Annie A. Dunn to E. P. Smith, February 20, 1867

Summary:
Dunn's communication pertains to transportation arrangements, presumably for her return to the northeast.

Edw. P. Smith--Esq. Staunton Va. Box 115

Feb 20 1867

Dear Sir

Yours of the 23rd is received. Will you please defer sending the transportation until you hear from me again. I have some good reasons for making this request which I will make known to you in a few days.

Very Respectfully

Mrs Annie A. Dunn
