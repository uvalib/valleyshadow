Franklin County: Medical Report for John H. Peters, [n. d.]

Summary:
This document is an unsigned medical report for John H. Peters, detailing his vital statistics and his death.

occupation printer[illeg.] taken sick about a month ago with Fever from which he is now recovering: Tongue abnormally clean: pain over region of umbilicous in pressure; --eyes congested; heart sounds natural rhythm regular 96, - pulse intermittent, small 96;- nervous condition good. The pulse is 102 full, and today regular. Eyes less congested. Pulse 78, regular, full, strong. General appearance improving.

Died at 3 a.m. of the 30th inst.
