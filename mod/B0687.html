Freedmen's Bureau Records: Roswell Waldo to Mr. Piper, October 17, 1868

Summary:
Waldo requests payment on behalf of a young worker's mother.

Bureau Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands 4th Division 9 Sub-District Mr. Piper, Esquire
Staunton, Virginia

October 17 1868

Staunton, Virginia

Dear Sir:

I have the honor to enclose herewith account of Kitty Cox (colored) for labor of her son George in your Brick Yard at Staunton Virginia - Time equivalent to two (2) months and three weeks - at $10 00 per month.

Please examine the account & if correct - remit said amount $27 50 to this office this Evening if possible.

Very respectfully
Your obedient servant

Roswell Waldo
Assistant Sub Assistant Commissioner
