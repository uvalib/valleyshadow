Freedmen's Bureau Records: Robert R. Nelson to Thomas P. Jackson, December 23, 1867

Summary:
Nelson states that he will call on Jackson in a few days so that Jackson may review his accounts as related to a dispute between Nelson and a Harner Green.

Captain Tho. P. Jackson

December 23rd 1867

Jennings Gap

Sir

I have received yours of the 21st by Harner [unclear: Green] (colored), & reply by note this morning as it is absolutely impossible for me to call in person just now. I will however say that I have [unclear: offsets] against the amount he claims, I will call ro see you in a few days & let you examine my accounts.

Yours obediently

Robt. R. Nelson
