Augusta County: Annie A. Dunn to W. E. Whiting, December 8, 1865

Summary:
Letter from Annie A. Dunn to AMA secretary W. E. Whiting forwarding a bill for travel expenses.

Mr. W. E. Whiting N. Y.

Dec. 8th 1865

Please find a Bill of my expenses from Calais Me. to Staunton Va. My whole bill amounts to thirty two (32) dollars, but as I came through Washington instead of Via Richmond (as you are now sending all of your Teachers), I presume it has cost me a larger sum, so whatever you choose to deduct from my Bill I shall be perfectly satisfied, as I certainly do not want to be any extra expense to the Association.

Respectfully

Mrs Annie A. Dunn

Staunton Va. Box 115
