Freedmen's Bureau Records: W. Storer How to Orlando Brown, April 28, 1866

Summary:
How's letter refers to the provision of transport for a Staunton woman.

Bureau of Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands
Sixth District Virginia Brevet Brigadier General O. Brown
Assistant Commissioner Richmond Virginia

April 28th 1866

Winchester Virginia

General

I have the honor to acknowledge the receipt of transportation for Louisa Frintz freedwoman from Staunton to Greenwood Depot Virginia Central Railroad

I am General
Very Respectfully
Your Obedient Servant

W. Storer How
Brevet Major Assistant Quartermaster & Superintendent
