Augusta County: C. T. Wills to John H. McCue, March 31, 1854

Summary:
C. T. Wills provides John H. McCue with a number of "Blanks" to alleviate his workload.

Mr. John H McCue

March 31st 1854

Office Lynchburg Hose & Fire Insurance Company
Staunton Va

Dear Sir

Herewith please find a number of Blanks, which I send with the hope that they may be the means of relieving you of some of the troubles attendant on the agency which you hold for us, and also with the Hope that I shall have the pleasure of hearing from you more frequently hereafter.

Yours truly

C. T. Wills, Secretary
