Freedmen's Bureau Records: Cecil Clay to Lieutenant [Huster], January 8, 1866

Summary:
Clay forwards a deposition regarding Thomas Wholey's claim to the War Department.

January 8, 1866

Staunton Virginia

Lieutenant

I enclose you a deposition taken by a witness to a claim of Mr. Thomas Wholey who owns the ground on which the barracks of this post are built. He claims 2200 feet of lumber & wants to know [unclear: when] [unclear: disposition] is to be made of their barracks. The Quartermaster Department has I imagine the control of these quarters.

Very respectfully
Yours &c

Cecil Clay
Lt. Col. &c

Lieutenant Huster
Assistant Adjutant General
