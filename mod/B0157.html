Freedman's Bureau Records: Benj. C. Cook to Lt. Paul R. Hambrick S. A. C. , December 17, 1867

Summary:

Bureau R. F. and A. L.
Office A. S. A. C. 1st Div 3rd Sub. Dist. Lt. Paul R. Hambrick S. A. C.

December 17, 1867

Richmond, Va

Respectfully returned to Lt. Paul R. Hambrick S. A. C. with the information that the within named person John M. Coyle (not Quarles) enquired for resides in Charlestown Jefferson Co., W. Va.

Benj. C. Cook
Bvt. Capt. and A. S. A. C.
