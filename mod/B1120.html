Freedmen's Bureau Records: W. Storer How to Orlando Brown, September 19, 1865

Summary:
How requests permission to move his District headquarters from Staunton to Winchester, as he feels the more important part of the district are more accessible.

Bureau Refugees, Freedmen & Abandoned Lands
Sixth District Virginia Colonel O. Brown Assistant Commissioner
Richmond Virginia

September 19th 1865

Winchester Virginia

Colonel

I respectfully request permission to remove my Head Quarters from Staunton to Winchester for the reason that the most important portions of my District are more accessible from here, than from there, and, in my opinion, my duties may be better discharged at this, than at that place.

Please address your reply to Winchester Va. so that it may be seen whether the mail communications are frequent and regular, and send via Washington.

I am Colonel
Very Respectfully &c

W. Storer How Captain & Assistant Quartermaster
Superintendent Sixth District Virginia
