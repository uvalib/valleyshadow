Augusta County: John Scott to E. P. Smith, March 1, 1869

Summary:
Letter requesting confirmation of shippment for books ordered for Staunton classrooms.[It is unclear whether the date as written is correct]

Mr Smith Mission Home, Staunton Va

[deleted: Feb 30] Mar 1 1869

Sir

The box of Books shipped on the 13th per Steamer E C Knight and marked U S. John Scott Staunton Va Care Geo [unclear: Q] White Richmond Va and numbered 3180 had not arrived.

Please ascertain if the E C Knight actually goes to Richmond

John Scott
