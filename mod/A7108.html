Augusta County: John Scott to W. E. Whiting, January 12, 1866

Summary:
Standard request for schoolbooks (largely arithmetic textbooks).

Mr Whiting Staunton Va

Jan 12/66

In case you have not already sent 50 copies of Greenleafs Arith, or Thompsons Arith (Com. School) or Practical) you will please send 50 copies of (Greenleafs Com. Schl Arith or the same no of Thompsons Practical also 40 [deleted: 35] " Colton &Fitches Introductory Geography

John Scott

P. S. If you find that (from the 1st to the 8th) you sent the [deleted: book] 50 copies of Arith. Then forward me immediately by Express 40 copies of Colton &Fitches Geog. (Introductory) and I will pay the Expressage
