Franklin County: William E. Tolbert to Emma Tolbert, February 26, 1865

Summary:
William Tolbert asks his sister Emma to write soon and to send photographs with her letter.

Feb 25th/65

Dear Sister Emma

I wish you would write and tell me what disposition you made of the Photographs I had taken when at home and if you have any at home please send me one when you wrote and also [added: one] of you own and tell me what the news about the town. Give my complements to Kate, Annie, & Emma. Write soon.

From

Will
