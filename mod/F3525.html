Franklin County: Ministers of the German Reformed Church, Recommendation for John Ault, [n. d.]

Summary:
Several ministers of the German Reformed Church recommend John Ault as an Army chaplain.

The undersigned ministers belonging to the German Reformed Church, resident in Franklin County Penna, and personally acquainted with Rev. John Ault, of Loudon in the same county, do certify that he is a regularly ordained minister of the German Reformed Church, and do testify to his good and regular standing as such minister, and cheerfully and earnestly recommend him for appointment as an Army Chaplain.

Bernard C. Wolf pr[illeg.] of [illeg.]
Minister of Ger. Ref. Church

Isaac G. Brown
Mercersburg Pa.

Saul R. Fisher, State Clerk
of S[illeg.]d of Ger. Ref. Church.

B. Bausman
Chambersburg, Pa.

W. R. H. Deatrick
Pastor Grindstone Hill

Samuel Philips
Past. of Carlisle Charge
