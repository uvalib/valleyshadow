Freedmen's Bureau Records: W. Storer How to Orlando Brown, February 14, 1866

Summary:
How alerts Brown to his upcoming tour to Staunton and the upper portion of his district.

Colonel Orlando Brown
Assistant Commissioner

February 14th 1866

Richmond, Virginia

Colonel

I have the honor to inform you that I shall start today on a tour through the upper portion of my District reaching Staunton, probably, on the 17th of the month, and respectfully request that any business requiring immediate attention may be directed to me at that place until about the 24th of this month.

Captain George W. Wells, Assistant Quartermaster & Assistant Superintendent will transact the [unclear: ordinary] office business during my absence.

I am &c

W. Storer How
Captain &c
