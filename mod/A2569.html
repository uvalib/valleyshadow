Augusta County: Jedediah Hotchkiss to Sara A. Hotchkiss, December 17, 1862

Summary:
Hotchkiss gives his wife instructions for sending him a box.

Sara A. Hotchkiss

My Dear --

Let Nelson take the hinges and lock off of my map box, up stairs, to put on my box, and you can send me the key in a letter. Nelson will find some way to get it to me -- Paste on it the following direction -- Jed. Hotchkiss Topographical Engineer Lt. Gen. Jackson's Staff 2nd Corps, Army of Northern VirginiaCare of Maj. W. J. Hawks Guinea Station Richmond, Fredericksburg, & Petersburg Railroad
