Freedmen's Bureau Records: John A. McDonnell to Roswell Waldo, November 26, 1868

Summary:
McDonnell's letter to Waldo attempts to clarify an aspect of the reporting procedure for schools in Waldo's division.

Roswell Waldo
Assistant Sub Assistant Commissioner
Staunton, Virginia

November 26t 1868

Winchester

Sir

In reply to your communication of 23d inst. I have to state that schools sustained entirely by the people of the locality, receiving neither teachers or assistance from Northen Educational Societies nor benevolent associations nor from the Bureau need not be reported on Form "A" - The form in the back of Circular 26 is intended merely for the guidance of officers making the report.

Very Respectfully
Your obedient servant

John A McDonnell
Sub Assistant Commissioner
