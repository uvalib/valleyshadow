Franklin County: Alexander K. McClure to Eli Slifer, October 13, 185x

Summary:
McClure promises his support in Slifer's reelection bid, as well as the support of other politicians loyal to McClure.

October 13th

Chambersburg

Dear Colonel,

I now care for but one thing - that is your re-election. In that command me in any way & to any extent.

I have written both out members, and asked it of them to go with me for you. They both owe their election to me, and know it. I fear nothing in that quarter. I will write [unclear: Dularos] also. He ought to pay some respect to my work in the matter. It was my $300 judiciously expended in that county [deleted: ] him.

Yours truly

AK McClure

I will try & spend a night with you in a few days
