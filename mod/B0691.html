Freedmen's Bureau Records: Roswell Waldo to Mana Bumgarner, October 21, 1868

Summary:
Waldo requests that a teacher answer questions pertaining to a school for freedmen.

Bureau Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands 4th Division 9 Sub District Miss Mana Bumgarner
Teacher colored school
Arbor Hill Augusta County Virginia

October 21. 1868

Staunton, Virginia

I enclose herewith Blank School report which please fill out & return to this office by the 28th instant

It is absolutely necessary that you answer Every question to the best of your ability If any question is asked which does not apply to your duties answer accordingly

Very respectfully

Roswell Waldo
Assistant Sub Assistant Commissioner

Miss Emily Rodney (colored) Greenville
Mr. W. C. Echols (colored) West View
