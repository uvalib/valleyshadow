Freedmen's Bureau Records: Receipt of Thomas Jones, December 1, 1868

Summary:
Jones receives $4.15 in wages owed to him from Henry Doom.

Received of Roswell Waldo Assistant Sub Assistant Commissioner Bureau Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands Staunton, Virginia December 1, 1868 -- the sum of four dollars & fifeen cents in full for wages due me from Henry Doom for shucking corn &c

Thomas [added: his X mark] Jones

Witness [illeg.]
