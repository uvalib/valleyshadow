Freedmen's Bureau Records: Receipt of Marriage Register, December 23, 1868

Summary:
Burnett acknowledges the receipt of the Register of Marriages from Roswell Waldo.

Received December 23d 1868 of R. Waldo Assistant Sub Assistant Commissioner Bureau Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands the record pertaining to Marriages of colored persons within the County of Augusta who cohabited as such prior to the late war, which record is filed in the clerks office of Augusta County Court for future reference.

William A. Burnett, Clerk
