Freedmen's Bureau Records: Thomas P. Jackson to William Woods, January 6, 1868

Summary:
Jackson informs William Woods that he owes Joseph Lewis $7.00 in rent.

Bureau of Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands,
Office 4th Division, 9th Sub-District, Virginia. William Woods colored
Staunton

January 6 1868

Staunton, Va.,

Joseph Lewis colored states he cannot collect from you rent of stable you occupy under agreement made October 14/67 which you propose to vacate January 8/68.

Amount due from October 14/67 to January 8/68 2 months & 24 days @ $2.50 per mo. is $7.00

Please call at this office and pay above amount in full or in part on or before Saturday January 11/68.

Thos. P. Jackson
Agent

January 8/68 Paid on account of above $5.00 TPJ
