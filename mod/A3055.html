Augusta County: J.Y. McCutchan to Virginia Miller, July 14, 1864

Summary:
McCutchan thanks Mrs. Miller for sending a package of items and asks for shoes, money, and news.

Mrs. V.L. Miller
York
Pennsylvania Mrs. V.L. Miller First Division Fort Delaware

July the 14th 1864

Mrs Miller

It is with pleasure that I acknowledge the receipt of a small package containing a Testament ham soap comb thread buttons pins all of which I am very thankful to you for being just what I wanted I have been compelled to do without shoes since I have been here and would like to have a pair if you can send them here or I can buy them here if you can send me a little money as I will refund it to you or your Father if Providence permits I inferred from the verses you marked that you would do me a favor if required pardon me if I misunderstood you write to me at any rate and tell who Mary B. Miller is & give me all the news you can I will preserve your letter hope this will find you and all yours well

I am respectfully your friend

J.Y. McCutchan

[note at top of letter]Company D are all well
