Augusta: Patrick Brady to Alexander H. H. Stuart, April 8, 1868

Summary:
Patrick Brady writes to Alexander H. H. Stuart on the pending litigation Brady vs. Brady.

Hon. Alex H. H. Stuart

April 8/68

Philada

Dear Sir

I wrote You on the 31 ult with an offer of [unclear: Com nomide] in Brady vs. Brady, did you receive my letter & if so what is his answer, in case it comes up before the Court at the approaching term will it be necessary for me to be present.

Very truly yours

P Brady
