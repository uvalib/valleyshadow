Freedmen's Bureau Records: John A. McDonnell to Thomas P. Jackson, July 27, 1867

Summary:
McDonnell tells Jackson that he cannot authorize the issue of clothing in his district.

Bureau of Refugees, Freedmen, and Abandoned Lands,
Head Quarters 9th District Department of the Potomac, Mr. Thomas P. Jackson
Agent
Staunton, Virginia

July 27, 1867

Winchester, Virginia,

Sir,

In reply to your communication, I have the honor to state that I cannot authorize the gratuitous issue of the Bureau's clothing in your charge.

Very Respectfully,
Your Obedient Servant

John A. McDonnell
Captain & Sub Assistant Commissioner
