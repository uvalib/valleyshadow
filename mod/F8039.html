Franklin County: William Seibert to Simon Cameron, November 12, 1860

Summary:
Seibert asks Cameron for a patronage appointment to the Post Office.

Seibert
[illeg.] Chamberburg
November 1860.

November 12/1860

Chambersburg

Honorable Simon Cameron
Dear Sir

I have helped many of my fellow men. Now I need help myself. Can I have yours. as an applicant for the Post Office of this place-If so please cheer me up by the welcome yes.

Yours, you well
know,

William Seibert
