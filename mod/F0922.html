Franklin County: William McLellan to Thaddeus Stevens, April 23, 1862

Summary:
Reports the progress in a court case against Thaddeus Stevens.

Thaddeus Stevens

Apl 23/62

Chambrg

Dear Sir

Nill has decided the [unclear: ejectment] in your favor. [unclear: Hugh] intends taking the case to the Supreme Court. Finally I send their argument to you when made or shall the answer in [unclear: here]

Yours Resp

Wm McLellan
