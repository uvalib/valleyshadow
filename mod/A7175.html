Augusta County: Annie A. Dunn to Samuel Hunt, February 2, 1866

Summary:
Dunn writes Hunt to apologize for not sending a monthly report. She adds that she has been sick and missed some time at school during the month of January.

Rev. Samuel Hunt Staunton Va.

2nd 1866

Dear Sir

Please pardon me if I do not send a written Report for the month of January. I have been out of health for several days past but am better now and hope to resume my duties on Monday next.

Things are moving along finely, and our Schools are flourishing, we have every thing to encourage us.

Very Respectfully

Mrs Annie A. Dunn
