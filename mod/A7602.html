Augusta County: P. McThurchwell to Lorenzo Sibert, August 5, 1862

Summary:
This pass from the Confederate Provost Marshal of East Tennessee gives Lorenzo Sibert and three others permission to travel in the State.

No. 7164
Head-Quarters, Department East Tenn.,
Office Provost Marshal,

Aug 5, 1862.

Knoxville, Tenn.,

Permission is Granted L Sibert & 3 Men to visit [unclear: Sweetvale] Tenn, upon honor, not to communicate in writing, or verbally, for publication, any fact ascertained, which, if known to the enemy, might be injurious to the Confederate States of America.

P McThurchwell
Colonel and Provost Marshal.

(Haws & Dunkerley, Printers, Knoxville.)
