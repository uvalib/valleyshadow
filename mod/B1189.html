Freedmen's Bureau Records: Will A. Coulter to John W. Jordan, March 21, 1868

Summary:
Jordan is informed that his monthly report is overdue.

Bureau of Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands
Headquarters Assistant Commissioner, Virginia Jordan, John W.
Assistant Sub Assistant Commissioner

March 21, 1868

Richmond, Virginia

Sir,

Your personal report for the month of February 1868, under Circular No 12, Series 1866, War Department, Bureau &c, has not been received at these Head Quarters.

An explanation of the reason of this delay is required by the Brevet Brigadier General, Assistant Commissioner.

Respectfully yours

Will A. Coulter
Brevet Captain U.S. Army
Acting Assistant Adjutant General
