Freedmen's Bureau Records: Memo by H. H. Peck, Overseer of the Poor, September 12, 1866

Summary:
Peck acknowledges receiving a circular from the Bureau.

Staunton, Augusta County Virginia

September 12 1866

Received this day of 1st Lieutenant George T. Cook Assistant Superintendent. Bureau Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands Copy of Circular No. 10 from Headquarters of the Bureau, C.S.

H.H. Peck, President of the Board of Overseers of the Poor of Augusta County Virginia
