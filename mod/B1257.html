Freedmen's Bureau Records: Henry M. Whittelsey to Orlando Brown, September 4, 1867

Summary:
Whittelsey forwards transportation order for Margaret Frazier and other children, requested by Thomas P. Jackson.

[Form 6.]
War Department,
Bureau Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands,
Office Chief Quartermaster, Brevet Brigadier General O. Brown,
Assistant Commissioner, &
Richmond, Virginia

September 4 1867.

Washington,

General:

I respectfully forward herewith Transportation Orders, No. 2221, for transportation of Margaret Frazier & others, Freedchildren the same having been requested by letter of Thomas P. Jackson, Agt., forwarded to this office, approved by your endorsement of date August 30th 1867.

By order of Major General O. O. Howard, Commissioner.

Very respectfully, your obedient servant,

H. M. Whittelsey
[deleted: Brevet Brigadier General,] Chief Quarter Master, Bureau Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands
