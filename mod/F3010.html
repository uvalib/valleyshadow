Franklin County: David Demus to Mary Jane Demus, March 5, 1864

Summary:
Demus reports that his head wound has not yet healed.

Mary Jane Demus

March the 5th 1864

Jacksonville
Florida Headquarters

My Dear

My Dear i receive your kind and affectionate Wife i receive it this night With the greatest of pleasure [unclear: kines] my Dear i am happy to hear that you have such good health i am happy to say thank god that i have had good health so far my head is not healed up yet but i Don't think that I Will get any Cold in it for the Weather is pretty hot here and has been hot ever since i have been in the south there is please to excuse this bad writing and the mistake i Was writing i that that i only had one sheet of paper and When I found out Why i Was writing two sheet of paper and i know that you Would have to laugh When got it and look at it i Will give you the right Direct how to write your letters you must Direct your letter to me

i am going to rit it on hear

David Demus

Co. K 54 Mass.
volintears
