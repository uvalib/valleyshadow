Franklin County: Alexander K. McClure to Eli Slifer, October 28, 1863

Summary:
McClure asks Slifer what he know about Senator Montgomery and requests information on the Pennsylvania quota.

McClure and Stoner
Editors and Proprietors
Franklin Repository Office,
Chambersburg, Pennsylvania

Oct 28, 1863

Chambersburg, Pennsylvania

Dear Col

Where does Senator Mont-
gomery of the [unclear: Northernlul] and [unclear: ] lie - what does he follow & has he ever been in [unclear: fuller] life. He is the only Senator I know nothing of.

As soon as the quota of Pennsylvania as ascertained please advise me of it. The statement in the Bulletin that it is 38,000 cant be correct, is it?

Yours

AKMcClure

Honorable Eli Slifer
