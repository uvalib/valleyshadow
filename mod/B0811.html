Freedmen's Bureau Records: Thomas P. Jackson to Mr. Crawford and Brother, June 19, 1867

Summary:
Jackson orders the Crawford brothers to settle their debt with the Simm brothers immediately.

Bureau Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands
Office of 4th Division, 9th Sub-District, Virginia Misters Mess. Crawford & Brother

June 19/67

Mt. Alto Augusta County

I received your letter of June 6/67 and expected you would call according to promise. George and Abraham Simms represent that their property is levied on for debt and you must forthwith pay to them or me the $25 with Interest which you acknowledge you owe to them.

Your obedient servant

Thos P Jackson
Agent
