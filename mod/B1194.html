Freedmen's Bureau Records: Will A. Coulter to Roswell Waldo, December 21, 1868

Summary:
Waldo is informed that his term of service will end when the Bureau is discontinued at the end of the month.

Bureau of Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands
Headquarters Assistant Commissioner Virginia Waldo, Roswell
Clerk

December 21, 1868

Richmond, Virginia

Sir,

I am directed by the Assistant Commissioner to inform you that by direction of the Secretary of War, your appointment as an agent of this Bureau has be revoked, to date December 31st 1868 in consequence of the discontinuance of this Bureau at that time.

Yours respectfully

Will A. Coulter
Brevet Captain U.S. Army
Acting Assistant Adjutant General
