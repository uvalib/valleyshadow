Franklin County: Thomas [S?], Medical Certification for John H. Reed, January 9, 1863

Summary:
John H. Reed's physician finds him unable to travel. John Lomans concurs in the phsician's diagnosis.

January 9th, 1863

Johnstown

I hereby certify that I did this ninth day of January carefully examine Captain J. H. Reed of Company D, 126th Regt. Pennsylvania Vol. and find him still unable to travel in consequence of wounds received in the service of the United States.

Cambria County
Personally came before one of the justices of the peace for said county of Cambria John Lomans, a practical physician who upon his [deleted: ] say that the above is just and correct. Sworn and secured before me this 9th day of January, 1863.
J[illeg.] Ru[illeg.]

Tho. S[illeg.]
