Augusta County: David Speck to John McCue, November 3, 1859

Summary:
Speck seeks to collect debt from McCue.

David Speck
1860

Nov. 3rd 1859

Arbor Hill

To: John H. McCue
Dear Sir

I wish you would send me ten Dollars. It being the remainder of what you owe me. i am very much in need of money now. I am informed you are doing very well in Nelson County which i am glad to hear. write me on receipt of this. let me know if you are flush. Send the ten along

yours. Respectfully

David Speck

I will Recpt. David Speck
