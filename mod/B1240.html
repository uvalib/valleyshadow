Freedmen's Bureau Records: W. Storer How to Orlando Brown, April 23, 1866

Summary:
How requests transportation for Eveline Stuart to join her husband, James, in Ohio.

Bureau of Refugees, Freedmen and Abandoned Lands
Sixth District Virginia Brevet Brigadier General O. Brown
Assistant Commissioner, Richmond, Virginia.

April 23/1866

Staunton, Virginia.

General,

I have the honor to request that transportation may be furnished to Mrs. Eveline Stuart, freedwoman from Staunton, Virginia to Ironton, Lawrence County Ohio, as she wishes to go there and the facts concerning her husband are as stated in the accompanying letter from Mrs. Augusta Jordan, dated Washington, D.C. April 14, 1866.

I am, General
Your Obedient Servant

W. Storer How
Brevet Major, Assistant Quartermaster & Superintendent

Official copy
James A. Bates
Captain & Acting Assistant Adjutant General
