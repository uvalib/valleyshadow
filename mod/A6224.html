Augusta County: William L. Evans to David H. Evans, August 19, 1868

Summary:
William L. Evans writes his brother David H. Evans giving him permission to make a business decision.

H. Evans & Bro.
Wholesale Dealers in
Tobacco, Cigars, Snuffs, Papers, &c.

Aug 19 1868

Bridgewater Va

Dr Bro,

Do as you think Best. the quicker we can get into it the better for us delay costs a great deal some times--I Would rather go into it in the Spring I believe--Connay will sell that House as soon as he can get his Price for it. I Know he needs Money

Truly

Wm L Evans
