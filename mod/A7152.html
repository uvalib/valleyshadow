Augusta County: John Scott to E. P. Smith, March 12, 1869

Summary:
A request for books ("Colburns First Lessons in Intellecual Arith," "Greenleafs primary," etc.).

Rev E P Smith Staunton Va

Mar 12/69

Dear Sir

The Colburns First Lessons in Intellecual Arith 23 copies at 30 ea I return to you by express this morning--as they are rather difficult for the pupils for whom they were intended and Miss Burke does not like them on that account

I want some mental arith that contains the Multiplication Table as one of the principle things and also the ordinary Tables of weights and measures

Greenleafs primary I think is such I know Greenleaf has one but it may not be called Primary Please send by express 4 [illeg.] of uniform kind with

[page 2]
multiplication table in it or a few little cards with the table but, the Books are better

I am sorry to trouble you but the books are important to the school

Very Respectfully

John Scott
