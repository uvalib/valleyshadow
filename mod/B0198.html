Freedmen's Bureau Records: Peter Hanger Jr. to Thomas P. Jackson, January 2, 1868

Summary:
Peter Hanger, Jr. writes Jackson that the Bureau may use a five dollar donation how they see fit.

Thomas P. Jackson, Esq.

January 2d 1868

Waynesboro Va

Dear Sir

Yours of yesterday, in regard to the use of the $5. 00 for what purpose to make of it you can use it any way best for charitable purposes whatever you think best do with it and I will be satisfied.

Yours truly

Peter Hanger, Jr
