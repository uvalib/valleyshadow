Summary: Thayer's Circus will perform in Staunton including Johny Booker the boy gymnast, Dick Sands the clog dancer, and Billy Pastor, "the celebrated New York comique."
[No Title]
(Column 01)
Summary: The paper reminds readers that Thayer's circus will be in town on September 8th. It will include "better performers, finer horses and wagons, more varied and novel entertainments" than any other circus.
The Circus! The Circus!
(Column 01)
Summary: The paper prints a long description of Thayer's Circus. It will be in Staunton on the 8th.
Married
(Column 02)
Summary: Henry H. Varner and Miss Agnes A. Showalter, both of Augusta, were married on August 25th by the Rev. Martin Garber.
(Names in announcement: Henry H. Varner, Agnes A. Showalter, Rev. Martin Garber)
Died
(Column 02)
Summary: James Byers died at his residence near Parnassus on August 25th. He was 77 years old.
(Names in announcement: James Byers)
Died
(Column 02)
Summary: Dorcas Luella Whitsel, infant child of J. W. and S. J. Whitsel, died near Arbor Hill on August 22nd of cholera morbus.
(Names in announcement: Dorcas Luella Whitsel, J. W. Whitsel, S. J. Whitsel)
Died
(Column 02)
Summary: Daniel Falls died suddenly near Churchville on August 29th. He was 50 years old.
(Names in announcement: Daniel Falls)
