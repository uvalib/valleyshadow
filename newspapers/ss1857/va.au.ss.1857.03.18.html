Go To Page : 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 |

-Page 01-

To the Editors of the Staunton Spectator
(Column 5)
Summary: Letter about the travelers stuck on the train during the 1/18/57 snowstorm. The travelers stayed at his hotel or tavern, and he takes issue with their descriptions of it, and of him. He answers the first writer's complaints about food, service and accomodations one by one.
Trailer: Very Respectfully, THE PROPRIETOR
Weather Report for February, 1857
(Column 7)
Summary: Weather report for Feb., including temperature, wind, precipitation.
Trailer: Yours &c. J. B.; Mossy Creek Academy. Feb. 1857.
For the Spectator; Hole in the Ground
(Column 6)
Summary: First hand report on a volcano supposedly erupting in Pendleton Co. Writer describes it as a "hole in the ground."
(Names in announcement: D. A. Plecker)
Trailer: D. A. Plecker

-Page 02-

Slavery in Virginia
(Column 2)
Summary: Discusses reports from the Petersburg Express and the Charleston Mercury about the number of slave in VA, and the continiuing viability of the institution there. Mercury believes that the Deep South can't "rely" on the border states to resist abolition. Also notes a report in the New York Herald that Northerners are about to make a xconcerted effort to abolitionize VA. SS wants to disbelieve it, but just isn't sure. Ultimately, isn't worried baout abolition (see p)
Fire Company
(Column 1)
Summary: Another effort being made to start a fire company in town; SS hopes it meets with success. This reports the officers: James H. Waters, President; B. Bucher, Vice President; R. W. Hardy, 1st Director; R. J. Wise, 2nd Dir.; John A. Kice, 3d Dir.; T. E. Colemar, 4th Dir.; John G. A. Finn, 1st Engineer; Owen Keefe, 2nd Dir.; S. Steinbuck, 3rd Dir; John S. Dull, 4th Dir.; H. K. Harvey, 1st Hose Dir.; John W. Hardy, 2nd Dir.; J. Pollitz, Secretary; G. F. Elick, Treasurer.
(Names in announcement: James H. Waters, B. Bucher, R. W. Hardy, R. J. Wise, John A. Kice, T. E. Colemar, John G. A. Finn, Owen Keefe, S. Steinbuck, John S. Dull, H. K. Harvey, John W. Hardy, J. Pollitz, G. F. Elick)
Editorial Convention
(Column 1)
Summary: South Side Democrat and Rockingham Register have endorsed SS's call for an editorial convention (see ------). Convention will be held 5/11 in Staunton.
