John M. Stanley Acquitted
(Column 01)
Summary: John M. Stanley was acquitted in his trial for the killing of Jacob Scherer. Judge Fultz presided over the case. Capt. James Bumgardner and Judge Sheffey argued for the prosecution; George M. Cochran, Jr., and Col. Baldwin for the defense.
(Names in announcement: John M. Stanley, Jacob Scherer, Judge Fultz, Capt. James Bumgardner, Judge Sheffey, George M. CochranJr., Col. Baldwin)
