[No Title]
(Column 1)
Summary: List of Douglas electors from each Virginia electoral district.
Notice
(Column 1)
Summary: Notice for two meetings, one for the National Democracy in support of Douglas and Johnson and another one for Breckinridge and Lane supporters at the Court House.
Trailer: Many Democrats
The Staunton Convention--The People versus the Politicians--Roll on the Ball!
(Column 2)
Summary: A summary of the Staunton Convention. Calls the Convention a good representation of the people and not the politicians, and says that the spirit of compromise was strong.
Origin of Article: Richmond Index
Editorial Comment: The editor of the Vindicator will "cheerfully yield our editorial space" for this summary.
To the Democracy of Virginia
(Column 3)
Summary: A summary of the interactions between the Charlottesville and Staunton Conventions.
(Names in announcement: M.G. Harman)
Judge Hopkins' Dispatch
(Column 4)
Summary: A letter from G. W. Hopkins to the Staunton Convention supporting Douglas.
(Names in announcement: G.W. Hopkins)
Trailer: G. W. Hopkins
State Democratic Convention. Second Day.
(Column 5)
Summary: A description of the events of the second day of the Staunton Convention. M. G. Harman and Col. Baylor took part in the proceedings.
(Names in announcement: M.G. Harman, Col. Baylor)
