To the Members of the Staunton Bar
(Column 02)
Summary: The Mayor of Churchville writes the members of the Staunton bar to warn them that sentiment is growing in the county to pass legislation making it more difficult for lawyers to manage estates.

-Page 03-

[No Title]
(Column 01)
Summary: Mr. Richard Hurly and others are taking the census in Staunton. It is thought that the population will be found to be around 6000 persons.
(Names in announcement: Richard Hurly)
[No Title]
(Column 01)
Summary: The paper reports that African Americans have been leaving Staunton for states further south.
