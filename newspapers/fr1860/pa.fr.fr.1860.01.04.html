Burning a Religious Paper
(Column 04)
Summary: Editorial condemns the Democratic Party and President Buchanan's political appointees, especially Post Master General Holt. Editorial refers to letter from parishoner of United Bretheren of Christ in Hardy County, Virginia, whose religious newspaper was confiscated and burned by the local postmaster because it called for abolition.

An Irishman's Sufferings in South Carolina
(Column 1)
Summary: This article relates the whipping and tarring and feathering of an Irish worker in Charlteston, South Carolina, for his expressed anti-slavery opinions.
Origin of Article: New York Tribune
