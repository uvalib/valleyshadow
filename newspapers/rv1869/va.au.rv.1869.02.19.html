Full Text of Article:

Gen. Stoneman has made the following appointments of Magistrates in this county:

Samuel Landes vice Wm. Crawford, Zach Calbreath vice John S. Ellis, Robt. Terrill vice Geo. A. Bruce, Frank N. Myers vice J. W. Bell, Jno. Wissler vice Wm. Chapman, David Myers vice Saml. B. Finley, Elijah Curry vice Dr N. Van Lear, Chas. Bates vice Jas. D. Patterson, John A. Blume vice Wm. G. Sterrett, Henry K Eakle vice Jas. D. Craig, Alexander Anderson vice E. M. Cushing, Jno. Smith vice Jno. M Huff, Saml. Cline vice Theophilus Gamble, Jacob Bear vice Jas. W. Calhoun, Wm. Showalter vice J. Marshall McCue, Geo. A. Shuey vice James Wilson, Junius F. Maupin vice Chas. C. Francisco, Jacob Stover vice Absalom Koiner, David Firth vice Henry Eldson, Henry Rippetoe vice Wm. W. Montgomery, Daniel Fishburn vice Wm. D. Anderson, Abner Shumake vice J. Burke, David L. Snyder vice Chesley Kinney, Miletus G. Jones vice John C. Rivercomb.

Since the above was in type the following appointments in this county have been announced:

Geo. W. McCutcheon Sheriff, vic Samuel Paul, term expired; John H. Johns, Overseer of the Poor, vice Jas. A. Wilson.


Married
(Column 02)
Summary: Merritt W. Magann of Nelson and Miss Elizabeth Stover of Augusta were married near Stuart's draft on January 4th by the Rev. C. S. M. See.
(Names in announcement: Merritt W. Magann, Elizabeth Stover, Rev. C. S. M. See)
Married
(Column 02)
Summary: Alpheus G. Henkel of New Market and Miss F. M. Killian, daughter of Phillip Killian, were married on February 10th at the residence of the bride's father near Sherando, Augusta County, by the Rev. Jacob Killian.
(Names in announcement: Alpheus G. Henkel, F. M. Killian, Phillip Killian, Rev. Jacob Killian)
Died
(Column 02)
Summary: Judge N. Cleary died suddenly at the American Hotel of congestion of the lungs. He was 53 years old.
(Names in announcement: Judge N. Cleary)
Died
(Column 02)
Summary: Miss Nannie Catlett died at the residence of her uncle, N. P. Catlett. She was 21 years old.
(Names in announcement: Nannie Catlett, N. P. Catlett)
