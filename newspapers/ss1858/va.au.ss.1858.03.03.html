The Kansas Constitution
(Column 1)
Summary: Lecompton Constitution will gain eventual passage through Congress with the administration's strong support. In order to gain northern support, however, administration may seek an amendment allowing the people of Kansas to alter the constitution in the future, specifically in order to eliminate slavery.
Political Movements
(Column 2)
Summary: Buchanan administration's policy in Kansas likely to cause permanent division among northwestern Democrats.
