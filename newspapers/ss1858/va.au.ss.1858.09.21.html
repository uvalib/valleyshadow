Church Dedication
(Column 1)
Summary: New Methodist Episcopal Church at Mt. Meridian to be dedicated on 10/10/18. Rev. B.N. Brown of Alexandria and other ministers to attend.
Married
(Column 1)
Summary: Jas. S.H. Brady married to Mary Francis Grove on 9/16/1858 at Methodist Parsonage by Rev. G.G. Brooke. All of Staunton.
(Names in announcement: James S. H. Brady, Mary Francis Grove, Rev. G. G. Brooke)
Married
(Column 1)
Summary: Wm. F. McGuffin, of Augusta County, married to Hannah E. Carson, of Rockbridge County, on 9/9/1858 by Rev. Wm. M. McElwee at Mr. Mann's Hotel, in Lexington. Bride daughter of John Carson, dec'd.
(Names in announcement: William F. McGuffin, Hannah E. Carson, Rev. William M. McElwee)
Died
(Column 1)
Summary: Albert Obaugh, 20, died at Mt. Solon on 8/25/1858. Death caused by dropsy. Son of Adam Obaugh.
(Names in announcement: Albert Obaugh, Adam Obaugh)
