Summary: Rev. Benjamin Arbogast, A.M. married to Fannie C. Gibbons at Locust Dale by Rev. J.W. Wolf on 2/2/1858. Arbogast Principal of Wesleyan Female Institute. Bride daughter of John Gibbons, Esq., of Rockingham County.
