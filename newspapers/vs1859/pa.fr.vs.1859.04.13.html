Black Republicanism in Pennsylvania
(Column 4)
Summary: Pennsylvania Republicans in the Legislature voted to table a resolution prohibiting racial intermarriage. According to the Spirit, this shows that Republicans, despite protests, do believe in Negro equality. Gets blurry at the bottom.

Accident
(Column 3)
Summary: Noel was injured when the fire engine ran over his lower limbs.
(Names in announcement: Isaac Noel)
Full Text of Article:

--Mr. Isaac Noel met with rather a severe injury, one evening of last week, by the Hope Fire engine passing over his lower limbs, while running to a fire.
