Valley Virginian: December 24, 1868

Go To Page : 1 | 2 |

-Page 01-

Learn a Trade
(Column 03)
Summary: Article urging young men to learn a good trade.

-Page 02-

An Abominable Nuisance
(Column 01)
Summary: The paper calls on the "town authorities" to take action against what they call the "gangs of worthless lounging, vulgar niggers that congregate on the corners of the streets Sabbath after Sabbath, completely blockading the sidewalks, rendering it almost impossible for gentlemen and ladies to pass on their way to and from church."
Christmas
(Column 01)
Summary: The paper describes the anticipation children are feeling for Christmas day.
