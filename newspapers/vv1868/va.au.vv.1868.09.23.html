A Trip
(Column 05)
Summary: Account of a trip to the region's "watering places," including descriptions of each.

-Page 02-

[No Title]
(Column 03)
Summary: Judge Meredith granted a charter of incorporation to the "Sibert Patent Iron and Steel Company." The company will hold a patent on Sibert's process for manufacturing iron and steel, and will work with other companies wishing to benefit from it. The capital stock will be 5,000 shares at $100 each with the privilege of increasing to 10,000 shares. The main office will be in Richmond. The article includes a list of the officers.
(Names in announcement: Judge Meredith, Lorenzo Sibert, Daniel C. Brady, John D. Imboden, S. M. Barton, H. Woods, J. L. Weeks, H. J. Rogers, George W. Norris, J. S. Carswell, John A. Hambleton, L. R. Smoot)

-Page 03-

A Good Idea
(Column 01)
Summary: Mr. Harvey Risk proposes to conduct a census of Staunton, provided the town furnishes the books.
